# graft — Round 12: Community, Leaderboards, Shared Challenges

## Goal
Add social layers to Graft — enabling shared challenges, leaderboards, and community features that create accountability and motivation through friendly competition.

---

## Scope

### Social Features
- **Graft friends**: Add friends by username or contact — see their practice activity in a social feed
- **Activity feed**: See what your friends practiced today, this week — cheer them on with reactions
- **Shout-outs**: Send a "well done" notification to a friend after their session
- **Accountability partner**: Link with one friend — both get weekly check-in notifications about each other

### Challenges & Competitions
- **Time-based challenges**: "Practice 5 hours this week" — challenge any friend
- **Streak challenges**: "Maintain a 30-day streak" — race against friends
- **Skill-specific duels**: "Who can reach level 5 in guitar first?" — timed competition
- **Challenge history**: Past challenges with outcomes — win/loss record per friend

### Global Leaderboards
- **Weekly leaderboard**: Global rankings for weekly practice time per skill category
- **Monthly leaderboards**: More substantial competitions
- **Skill-specific boards**: Separate boards for Music, Sports, Languages, Coding, etc.
- **Anonymous mode**: Opt out of leaderboards — practice for yourself, not competition

### Shared Goals
- **Team goals**: Create a team (up to 10 people) with a collective practice goal
- **Team streak**: Team-wide streak — everyone must practice to keep the streak alive
- **Team stats**: Combined practice time for the team, per member breakdown
