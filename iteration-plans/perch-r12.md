# perch — Round 12: Social Features, Travel Community, Shared Adventures

## Goal
Add social layers to Perch — enabling shared travel tracking with friends and family, a community of conscious travelers, and collaborative trip planning.

---

## Scope

### Shared Trip Tracking
- **Travel buddy sync**: When traveling with someone, sync trip data — both see the same trip
- **Group travel**: For group trips, one person creates the trip, others join — shared itinerary and carbon tracking
- **Family travel dashboard**: Parents can see kids' travel activity (with consent)
- **"Where are you?"**: Lightweight location sharing during active travel — "I'm in Tokyo!"

### Collaborative Trip Planning
- **Shared trip planner**: Co-create a trip with friends — vote on destinations, transportation, accommodation
- **Carbon budget for groups**: Set a group carbon budget — track collective footprint
- **Shared carbon offset**: Pool money to offset the group's trip together
- **Trip photo album**: Shared photo album for the trip — auto-populated with photos from all participants

### Travel Community
- **Conscious traveler network**: Anonymous social feed — share your low-carbon travel wins
- **Destination sustainability scores**: Community-rated sustainability for destinations
- **Travel tips exchange**: "How do I travel carbon-neutral to Portugal?" — community Q&A
- **Low-carbon destinations**: Curated list of the most accessible, lowest-carbon destinations

### Competitive Challenges
- **Monthly carbon challenge**: "Travel 500km this month — under your carbon budget"
- **Friend challenges**: Challenge friends to travel lighter — who can have the lowest carbon footprint?
- **Leaderboard**: Monthly rankings — who traveled the greenest?
