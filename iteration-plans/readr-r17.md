# readr — Round 17: Awards, Brand, Community, Press

## Goal
Build Readr's brand, earn industry recognition, and cultivate a passionate community of readers and thinkers who use quotes to think and write.

---

## Scope

### Awards & Press
- **Apple Design Awards submission**: Showcase Readr's beautiful quote cards and AI intelligence
- **Product Hunt**: Launch Readr 2.0 on Product Hunt
- **Literary press**: Literary Hub, The Paris Review, Electric Literature — Readr as a reader's tool
- **Design press**: CSS Design Awards, Awwwards — Readr as a beautifully designed app
- **"Best of" nominations**: App Store Best of — work with PR team

### Brand Development
- Refresh brand assets: new app icon, clean typography, paper white + ink palette
- Design system: full component library
- Readr's visual language: "Every great thought, captured"
- Promotional video: 30-second cinematic — quotes as seeds of thought

### Community Building
- **Readr community**: Simple forum — share favorite quotes, discuss collections
- **"Quote of the Year"**: Annual competition — community votes on best quote of the year
- **Creator spotlight**: Highlight power users — how they use Readr for writing, research, reflection
- **Reddit**: Active presence in r/quotes, r/books, r/philosophy, r/writing
- **Twitter/X**: Famous quotes shared daily — build following around the Readr account

### Publisher & Author Partnerships
- **Publisher outreach**: Partner with publishers to feature quotes from upcoming releases
- **Author collections**: Authors create official quote collections from their books
- **Early release quotes**: Authors share exclusive quotes before book release
