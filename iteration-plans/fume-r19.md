# fume — Round 19: Team Building, Company Structure, Investment Readiness

## Goal
Build the internal team and company infrastructure to support Fume as a standalone, growing product company — preparing for potential investment and sustainable long-term operation.

---

## Scope

### Team Building
- Hire: 1 senior iOS engineer, 1 Android engineer, 1 ML engineer (on-device models)
- Part-time or contractor: designer for ongoing UI/UX iteration
- Consider: Head of Growth (part-time initially)
- Contractor relationships for localization (10 languages)
- Build a hiring plan for the next 12 months

### Engineering Infrastructure
- CI/CD pipeline: GitHub Actions for automated builds, TestFlight / Google Play internal testing
- Crash reporting: Crashlytics / Sentry
- Analytics: Mixpanel or PostHog (privacy-compliant)
- Error monitoring: track which features cause most issues
- Accessibility linter in CI: catch accessibility regressions

### Company Structure
- Legal: establish LLC or corporation for Fume
- Banking: separate business account for Fume revenue/expenses
- Accounting: set up bookkeeping (QuickBooks or equivalent)
- IP: trademark Fume brand, logo

### Investment Readiness
- **Metrics deck**: MAU, DAU, retention curves, conversion funnel, LTV, churn
- **Product vision document**: Where is Fume going in the next 3-5 years?
- **Competitive landscape**: Fume vs. Notion, Obsidian, Roam, Readwise — positioning
- **Growth plan**: How do we get to $1M ARR? What's the path?
- **Team**: Who's on the founding team? What roles need to be filled?

---

## Out of Scope
- Fume 3.0 major release (R20 — vision document only)
- Company formation and investment (already covered)
