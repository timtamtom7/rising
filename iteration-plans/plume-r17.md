# plume — Round 17: Awards, Brand, Community, Press

## Goal
Build Plume's brand, earn industry recognition, and cultivate a passionate reading community — positioning Plume as the definitive reading companion for serious readers.

---

## Scope

### Awards & Press
- **Apple Design Awards submission**: Showcase Plume's beautiful editorial design and reading intelligence
- **Product Hunt**: Launch Plume 2.0 on Product Hunt
- **Literary press**: Literary Hub (lithub), The Millions, Electric Literature — Plume as the reader's companion
- **Consumer tech press**: TechCrunch, The Verge — Plume as a beautiful app
- **"Best of" nominations**: App Store Best of — work with PR team

### Brand Development
- Refresh brand assets: new app icon, elegant serif typography, warm cream + ink palette
- Design system: full component library
- Plume's visual language: "A home for every book you've ever loved"
- Promotional video: 60-second cinematic — reading as a way of life

### Community Building
- **Plume community forum**: Discourse-based — reading recommendations, book discussions, writing tips
- **"Reader of the month"**: Highlight top community reviewers and list creators
- **Local reading groups**: Meetup integration — find local book clubs using Plume
- **Reddit**: Active presence in r/books, r/bookclub, r/reading
- **Newsletter**: Weekly "Plume Reading Preview" — curated picks, community highlights

### Author Partnerships
- **Author Q&As**: Partner with authors for in-app Q&A sessions
- **Early release tracking**: Track upcoming book releases — pre-order and get notified on release day
- **Author recommendations**: Authors share their reading lists in Plume
