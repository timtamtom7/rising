# Cast — R16: Subscription Tiers & Monetization

## Goal
Introduce Cast Pro and Cast Venue subscription tiers for power users and commercial venues.

---

## Scope

## Free Tier (Core)
- Cast to 1 device at a time
- Basic stream discovery
- Simple queue
- 5 watch party attendees
- Local device discovery

## Cast Pro ($4.99/month or $39.99/year)
- Cast to multiple devices simultaneously
- ML-powered recommendations
- Unlimited queue
- Unlimited watch party attendees
- Priority device discovery
- Quality optimization
- iCloud sync
- Shortcuts integration
- Priority email support

## Cast Venue ($14.99/month or $99.99/year)
- Everything in Pro
- Multi-zone casting (up to 10 zones)
- Digital signage mode
- Content management system
- Venue dashboard
- Commercial licensing support
- Zone scheduling
- Real-time zone monitoring

## Cast Enterprise (custom pricing)
- Everything in Venue
- Unlimited zones
- SSO / SAML
- Audit logs
- Custom branding
- SLA guarantees
- Dedicated account manager

## Subscription Infrastructure
- StoreKit 2 for in-app purchases
- RevenueCat for cross-platform subscription management
- 14-day free trial for Pro and Venue
- Same iCloud account → unlock on all devices
- Family Sharing: Pro unlocked for all family members

---

## Out of Scope
- Lifetime purchase option
- Ad-supported free tier
- Content subscription aggregation
