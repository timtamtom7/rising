# haul — Round 15: Haul 2.0, International Expansion, Localization

## Goal
Ship Haul 2.0 — a comprehensive redesign — combined with international expansion to take Haul global with region-specific packing knowledge.

---

## Scope

### Haul 2.0 — Major Redesign
- **New UI**: Warm, travel-inspired design — passport stamps, map motifs, leather texture accents
- **Packing flow redesign**: New 3-step packing flow: Where → What → Pack
- **Visual packing view**: See your suitcase as you're packing — tap items to confirm
- **SwiftUI 5 + iOS 18+**: Full redesign with latest SwiftUI capabilities, interactive widgets, App Intents for Siri

### Apple Intelligence Integration
- **Siri + Haul**: "Hey Siri, add a toothbrush to my packing list" — one voice command
- **Travel prediction**: Apple Intelligence predicts your next trip from calendar — "You have a trip to NYC next week — want to start packing?"
- **Live Activities**: Active trip as Live Activity on Lock Screen — packing checklist + countdown to departure

### International Expansion
- Full i18n: English (base), German, French, Spanish, Italian, Portuguese, Japanese, Korean, Simplified Chinese, Arabic (RTL)
- Localized screenshots for App Store in top 5 languages
- **Regional packing guides**: "Packing for Japan" — culture-specific advice (remove shoes indoors, don't overpack, etc.)
- **Regional airline info**: baggage allowances for regional airlines (Ryanair vs. Delta vs. ANA)
- **Regional weather data**: Local weather services (Japan's JMA, Europe's ECMWF)
- PPP-adjusted pricing for developing markets
- Local payment methods: Alipay, UPI, Boleto, Paytm
