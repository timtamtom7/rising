# Muse — R16: Subscription Tiers & Monetization

## Goal
Introduce Muse Pro and Muse Studio subscription tiers for musicians and professional studios.

---

## Scope

## Free Tier (Core)
- 3 projects
- Basic MIDI editing
- 10 tracks per project
- Built-in instruments (basic)
- Basic loops
- Local storage

## Muse Pro ($9.99/month or $79.99/year)
- Unlimited projects
- Unlimited tracks
- All built-in instruments (full)
- ML composition assistance
- Style transfer
- Advanced MIDI editing
- Unlimited loops
- iCloud sync
- Shortcuts integration
- Priority email support

## Muse Studio ($19.99/month or $149.99/year)
- Everything in Pro
- Unlimited collaborators
- Client portal
- Sample sharing community
- Studio management dashboard
- Project archival
- Industry format export (stems, OMF, AAF)
- High-quality audio streaming
- Team admin controls

## Muse Enterprise (custom pricing)
- Everything in Studio
- Unlimited team members
- SSO / SAML
- Audit logs
- Custom branding
- SLA guarantees
- Dedicated account manager

## Subscription Infrastructure
- StoreKit 2 for in-app purchases
- RevenueCat for cross-platform subscription management
- 14-day free trial for Pro and Studio
- Same iCloud account → unlock on all devices
- Family Sharing: Pro unlocked for all family members

---

## Out of Scope
- Lifetime purchase option
- Ad-supported tier
- Per-track/per-sample sales marketplace
