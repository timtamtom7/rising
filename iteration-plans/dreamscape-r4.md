# Dreamscape — Round 4: Sleep Lab, Dream Journal Export, Social

## Features

### Sleep Lab
- Log sleep conditions: mattress type, room temperature, sound, light, food before bed, screen time
- AI correlation: "You remember your dreams 3x more when you sleep at 68°F"
- Sleep setup optimization — tips based on your data

### Dream Journal Export
- Export dream journal as beautiful PDF book
- Annual dream yearbook — highlights, top symbols, dream count
- Timeline export — share your dream journey as a visual timeline
- Print-ready format

### Social / Community
- Share dream symbol anonymously — "I've been dreaming about water for months"
- See aggregate patterns: "70% of people who dream about water are processing anxiety"
- Dream interpretation help — tap to see what this symbol commonly means
- No accounts — anonymous only

### Widgets
- Dream recall widget — "What did you dream about last night?" with quick log
- Symbol of the day — learn what your most common symbols mean
