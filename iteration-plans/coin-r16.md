# Coin — R16: Subscription Tiers & Monetization

## Goal
Introduce Coin Pro and Coin Household subscription tiers with meaningful feature differentiation.

---

## Scope

## Free Tier (Core)
- Up to 3 accounts
- 50 transactions/month
- Basic budgets (3 budgets)
- 30-day history
- Manual transaction entry
- Basic charts

## Coin Pro ($4.99/month or $39.99/year)
- Unlimited accounts and transactions
- Unlimited budgets
- ML spending predictions
- Financial health score
- Receipt scan (camera)
- Tax category export
- Full history
- Cash flow predictions
- Shortcuts integration
- Priority email support

## Coin Household ($7.99/month or $59.99/year)
- Everything in Pro
- Up to 5 household members
- Shared budgets and goals
- Joint expense tracking
- Family financial dashboard
- Allowance tracking
- Accountant access

## Coin Business ($14.99/month or $99.99/year)
- Everything in Household
- Business expense categories
- Tax preparation export
- Multi-currency
- Invoice tracking
- 1099 tracking
- Accountant portal

## Coin Enterprise (custom pricing)
- Everything in Business
- SSO / SAML
- Audit logs
- API access
- Dedicated account manager
- Volume pricing

## Subscription Infrastructure
- StoreKit 2 for in-app purchases
- RevenueCat for cross-platform subscription management
- 14-day free trial for Pro and Household
- Same iCloud account → unlock on all devices
- Family Sharing: Pro unlocked for all family members

---

## Out of Scope
- Lifetime purchase option
- Ad-supported free tier
- Bank account connections (manual entry only — privacy)
