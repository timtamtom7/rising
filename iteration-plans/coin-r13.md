# Coin — R13: Enterprise & Business Features

## Goal
Expand Coin for small business and freelancer use with invoicing, tax categories, and business expense management.

---

## Scope

### Business Expense Categories
- Tax-deductible vs. personal classification
- Business expense tagging (office, travel, software, meals)
- "Reimbursable" flag: track money owed back to business
- Export category report for accountant

### Tax Preparation Export
- Annual expense summary by category
- CSV/PDF export for TurboTax, QuickBooks, H&R Block
- Date range: calendar year or fiscal year
- Tax-deductible total for the year

### Freelancer / Contractor Tools
- Invoice tracking (link invoice to expense)
- Client-based expense grouping
- 1099 tracking for contractors
- Mileage log (manual entry)

### Multi-Currency
- Configure base currency for display
- Bills in foreign currencies show converted amount
- Daily exchange rate updates
- Historical rate at time of transaction

### Business Dashboard
- Monthly P&L summary (revenue minus expenses)
- Top expense categories
- Tax liability estimate
- Cash flow forecast

### Accountant Access
- Read-only access for accountant (guest role)
- Accountant can export all data
- Client can lock certain accounts from accountant view

---

## Out of Scope
- Full accounting ledger (use QuickBooks)
- Payroll tracking
- Multi-company support
