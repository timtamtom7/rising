# Wade — Rounds 3, 4, 5

## Round 3: Budgets, Recurring, Polish

### Budget Goals
- Set a monthly spending limit
- Alert when approaching limit ("You've spent £1,400 of £2,000 this month")
- Daily spending cap
- Category-specific budgets ("£300/month for food")

### Recurring Expenses
- Mark expenses as "recurring"
- Track monthly bills separately
- Upcoming bills list ("Rent due in 3 days")
- Auto-detect recurring: "You spend ~£50 on groceries every week"

### Multi-Currency
- Track in multiple currencies
- Set primary currency for display
- Show spending in original currency + converted

---

## Round 4: Reports, Export, Platform

### Reports & Trends
- Monthly spending breakdown (pie chart by category)
- Compare this month vs last month
- "Biggest expense category this month: Transport (£340)"
- Yearly spending summary

### Data Export
- Export to CSV
- Export to PDF (formatted monthly report)
- Export to QuickBooks/Xero (small business integration)

### Platform
- iOS app (native)
- Widget: quick expense entry

---

## Round 5: Social, AI, Scale

### Multi-user / Shared
- Shared wallet for couples/households
- "We spent £X on groceries this month"
- Shared budget goals

### AI Insights
- "You spend 40% more on food when you eat out"
- "Your biggest spending month was December (£X)"
- Savings suggestions: "If you cut £100/month, you'd save £1,200/year"

### Freemium Polish
- Free: 20 expenses/month, no summaries, no export
- Track: unlimited expenses, weekly summaries, category insights
- Overview: monthly reports, budget goals, export, multiple currencies
