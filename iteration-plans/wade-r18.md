# wade — Round 18: Team, Company, Investment Readiness, Foundation

## Goal
Build the team and company infrastructure to support Wade as a fintech startup — preparing for potential investment, regulatory compliance, and sustainable long-term operation.

---

## Scope

### Team Building
- Hire: 1 senior iOS engineer, 1 Android engineer, 1 backend engineer
- Head of Business Development (B2B financial coach platform)
- ML engineer (for AI financial agent)
- Part-time designer
- Compliance consultant: financial data regulations (PCI DSS, GDPR)

### Regulatory & Compliance
- **PCI DSS compliance**: If storing payment card data — ensure PCI DSS compliance
- **Data privacy**: GDPR, CCPA compliance for financial data
- **Financial data security**: Third-party security audit of financial data handling
- **Insurance**: Product liability insurance for financial advice features

### Engineering Infrastructure
- CI/CD: GitHub Actions for iOS + Android builds
- Crash reporting: Crashlytics / Sentry
- Analytics: Mixpanel or PostHog (PHI-compliant)
- Error monitoring: track which features cause most issues
- Accessibility linter in CI

### Company Structure
- Legal: LLC or corporation for Wade
- Banking: separate business account
- Accounting: set up bookkeeping
- IP: trademark Wade brand, logo

### Investment Readiness
- **Metrics deck**: MAU, DAU, retention curves, conversion funnel, LTV, churn, MRR/ARR, B2B pipeline
- **Product vision document**: Wade 3.0 and beyond
- **Competitive landscape**: Wade vs. Mint, YNAB, Copilot, other fintech apps
- **Growth plan**: Path to $1M ARR
- **Regulatory status**: PCI DSS, GDPR compliance documentation
