# Muse — Rounds 3, 4, 5

## Round 3: Patterns, Watch, Polish

### Session History Expansion
- Weekly stats (total sessions, total minutes, average duration)
- Streak counter (consecutive days with at least one session)
- "Best streak" record
- "You've completed X sessions totalling Y hours"

### Apple Watch Companion
- Watch app: control session from watch
- Watch shows orb animation + haptic guidance on wrist
- Start/stop from watch, see duration on watch
- Independent session tracking (no iPhone needed after start)

### Guided Breath Guide
- Visual breath guide (not just orb — text: "Breathe in 3... 2... 1...")
- Session types: "Sleep", "Focus", "Relax", "Wake Up"
- Each type has different orb color palette and haptic pattern

## Round 4: Sounds, Social, Platform

### Soundscapes (Optional)
- Master tier: ambient audio (optional — not the main feature)
- Subtle ambient sounds that layer with breathing
- Not "rainforest" clichés — more abstract: white noise variants, low hums, nature close-ups
- Volume mixing with breathing

### Reminders / Nudges
- "Time to breathe" notification (configurable time)
- Daily reminder to do at least one session
- Streak preservation nudge

### Platform
- iOS app (native)
- watchOS standalone app
- macOS app

## Round 5: Community, AI, Scale

### Community
- Share breathing patterns publicly
- Browse and import others' patterns
- "Popular patterns this week"

### AI Pattern Analysis
- "Your best sessions are in the morning"
- "You breathe better when you haven't had caffeine"
- Adaptive: adjust breathing pattern based on time of day

### Freemium Polish
- Free: 5 sessions/day, 1-10min, no history
- Practice: unlimited sessions, 5-45 min, history
- Master: custom patterns, haptic patterns, Watch, soundscapes
