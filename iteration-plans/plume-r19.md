# plume — Round 19: Team, Company, Investment Readiness, Foundation

## Goal
Build the team and company infrastructure to support Plume as a standalone product — preparing for potential investment and sustainable long-term operation.

---

## Scope

### Team Building
- Hire: 1 senior iOS engineer, 1 Android engineer
- Part-time designer for ongoing UI/UX iteration
- Content/community manager (for author partnerships and community)
- Contractor: ML engineer for AI recommendations (part-time)

### Engineering Infrastructure
- CI/CD: GitHub Actions for iOS + Android builds
- Crash reporting: Crashlytics / Sentry
- Analytics: Mixpanel or PostHog
- Error monitoring: track which features cause most issues
- Accessibility linter in CI

### Company Structure
- Legal: LLC or corporation for Plume
- Banking: separate business account
- Accounting: set up bookkeeping
- IP: trademark Plume brand, logo

### Investment Readiness
- **Metrics deck**: MAU, DAU, retention curves, conversion funnel, LTV, churn, MRR/ARR
- **Product vision document**: Plume 3.0 and beyond
- **Competitive landscape**: Plume vs. Goodreads, StoryGraph, Bookly, other reading apps
- **Growth plan**: Path to $500K ARR
- **Community metrics**: reader engagement, reviews, list curation — soft assets that matter

- Plume 3.0 vision document (R20)
