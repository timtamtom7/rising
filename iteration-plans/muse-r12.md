# Muse — R12: Collaboration & Ensemble Features

## Goal
Enable Muse for collaborative music creation with shared projects, real-time collaboration, and ensemble features.

---

## Scope

### Collaborative Projects
- Share Muse projects via iCloud with collaborators
- Real-time collaborative editing (multiple musicians in same project)
- Presence indicators: see who's working on what track
- Role-based access: Producer, Musician, Listener

### Ensemble Sessions
- Virtual ensemble: record multiple musicians separately, sync in project
- "Take 1, Take 2, Take 3" for each musician
- Align takes automatically by tempo/time
- Comment on specific takes

### Sample Sharing
- Share custom samples/instruments with other Muse users
- Browse and download community samples
- Sample packs by genre
- Sample attribution and licensing

### Collaborative Loops
- Build loop libraries collaboratively
- Community loop packs
- Credit loop creators

### Remote Recording
- Record audio into Muse remotely
- Latency compensation
- Metronome sync across locations

### Music Sharing
- Share compositions to Muse community
- Listen and comment on others' music
- Collaboration requests: "Looking for a drummer for this track"

---

## Out of Scope
- Video chat integration
- Live streaming performances
- Distribution/streaming to Spotify/Apple Music
