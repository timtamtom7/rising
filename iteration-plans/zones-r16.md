# Zones — R16: Subscription Tiers & Monetization

## Goal
Introduce Zones Pro and Zones Home subscription tiers for power users and families.

---

## Scope

## Free Tier (Core)
- Up to 3 custom zones
- Manual zone switching
- Basic automations (3)
- 7-day history
- Local storage

## Zones Pro ($3.99/month or $29.99/year)
- Unlimited zones
- ML-powered zone prediction
- Activity detection
- Smart notification filtering
- Unlimited automations
- Location-based triggers
- Full history
- iCloud sync
- Shortcuts integration
- Priority email support

## Zones Home ($6.99/month or $49.99/year)
- Everything in Pro
- Up to 5 household members
- Shared zone configurations
- Family location awareness
- Shared automations
- Guest zones
- Family dashboard

## Zones Enterprise (custom pricing)
- Everything in Home
- Office zone management
- Team spaces
- Access control
- SSO / SAML
- Audit logs
- MDM support
- Volume licensing
- Dedicated account manager

## Subscription Infrastructure
- StoreKit 2 for in-app purchases
- RevenueCat for cross-platform subscription management
- 14-day free trial for Pro and Home
- Same iCloud account → unlock on all devices
- Family Sharing: Pro unlocked for all family members

---

## Out of Scope
- Lifetime purchase option
- Ad-supported tier
