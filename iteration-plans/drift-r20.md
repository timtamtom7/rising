# drift — Round 20: Platform Ecosystem, Awards, 5-Year Vision

## Goal
Cement Drift as the world's leading consumer sleep health platform — with full ecosystem integration, industry recognition, and a compelling 5-year vision.

---

## Scope

### Platform Ecosystem
- **Drift for Apple Vision Pro**: Immersive sleep health experience — see your sleep data in 3D spatial view
- **Drift Embedded SDK**: Allow other wellness apps to embed Drift's sleep tracking (wearable manufacturers)
- **Drift API**: Developers can access sleep data via REST API (with user consent)
- **Integration marketplace**: Certified integrations with Oura, Whoop, Eight Sleep, Tempur-Pedic smart beds

### Awards & Press
- **Apple Design Awards submission**: Full submission package
- **CES Innovation Award**: Submit for sleep tech category at CES
- **Forbes sleep tech roundup**: Pitch Drift for Forbes, Wired, NYT sleep tech articles
- **Sleep tech conference**: Present at SLEEP conference (annual sleep research meeting)

### Revenue Milestones
- Target: $500K ARR
- 5,000 paying subscribers
- 3 enterprise/corporate wellness accounts (team sleep challenges)
- Partnership revenue: first integration revenue shares

### Long-Term Vision (2027-2030)
- **Sleep as a vital sign**: Position sleep alongside heart rate, respiration as a core health metric tracked continuously
- **Preventive health alerts**: "Your sleep patterns changed 3 weeks before your doctor visit — is everything okay?"
- **Drift for kids**: A child sleep tracking product for parents of toddlers/teens
- **Drift for athletes**: Elite athlete sleep optimization — integration with team coaches
- **AI sleep agent**: An AI agent that attends to your sleep health — optimizes your environment, adjusts your schedule, and reports to you
- **Clinical FDA clearance**: Pursue FDA clearance as a digital therapeutic for insomnia (DT-1)
