# plume — Round 12: Social Features, Public Reviews, Shared Reading

## Goal
Build social reading features — public reviews, shared reading lists, and a community of passionate readers who share their reading lives.

---

## Scope

### Public Reviews & Profiles
- **Public reader profile**: Optional public profile — show your reading stats, top books, reviews
- **Book reviews**: Write and publish reviews — rated, with optional spoiler warnings
- **Review voting**: Community votes on most helpful reviews
- **Reading timeline**: See a reader's reading history — what they read when, how they rated it

### Shared Reading Lists
- **Curated lists**: Create and share reading lists — "Best Sci-Fi of 2025", "Books That Made Me Cry"
- **List collaboration**: Invite friends to co-curate a list
- **Featured lists**: Plume editorial team highlights best community lists
- **List rankings**: Community-voted rankings of lists by category

### Reading Challenges
- **Annual reading challenge**: Set a goal (e.g., 52 books in 2025) — track progress
- **Genre challenges**: "Read 10 memoirs this year" — sub-challenges within annual challenge
- **Social challenges**: Challenge friends — "Who can read 10 books first?"
- **Achievements**: Reading streaks, page milestones, genre completion badges

### Community Features
- **Anonymous social**: Share a quote anonymously to a public feed
- **Reading status updates**: "Just started X", "Finished X" — friend activity feed
- **Author fan pages**: Community pages for popular authors — share quotes, discuss themes
- **Book discussions**: Per-book discussion threads — spoiler-tagged sections

---

## Out of Scope
- Subscription optimization (R13)
- Platform expansion (Android, web) (R14)
