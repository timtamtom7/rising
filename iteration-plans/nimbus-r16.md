# Nimbus — R16: Subscription Tiers & Monetization

## Goal
Introduce Nimbus Pro and Nimbus Team subscription tiers for power users and organizations.

---

## Scope

## Free Tier (Core)
- Up to 100 notes
- Basic rich text formatting
- 3 notebooks
- Basic search
- 7-day version history
- Local storage

## Nimbus Pro ($4.99/month or $39.99/year)
- Unlimited notes
- All formatting options
- Unlimited notebooks
- Full-text search + semantic search
- Unlimited version history
- ML summarization and writing assistance
- Topic clustering
- Smart linking and backlinks
- iCloud sync
- Shortcuts integration
- Priority email support

## Nimbus Team ($9.99/month or $79.99/year)
- Everything in Pro
- Up to 10 team members
- Shared notebooks with real-time collaboration
- Comments and mentions
- Guest access (unlimited)
- Team activity feed
- Review workflows
- Shared templates
- Team admin controls

## Nimbus Enterprise (custom pricing)
- Everything in Team
- Unlimited team members
- Wiki / knowledge base features
- SSO / SAML
- Audit logs
- Permission hierarchies
- Compliance tools
- API access
- Dedicated account manager

## Subscription Infrastructure
- StoreKit 2 for in-app purchases
- RevenueCat for cross-platform subscription management
- 14-day free trial for Pro and Team
- Same iCloud account → unlock on all devices
- Family Sharing: Pro unlocked for all family members

---

## Out of Scope
- Lifetime purchase option
- Ad-supported free tier
- Per-note pricing
