# Pulse — R20: Platform Expansion & Long-Term Vision

## Goal
Expand Pulse to Android and establish it as the most comprehensive heart health companion across all platforms.

---

## Scope

### Android App
- Native Android app (Kotlin)
- Works with Android Wear OS / Samsung Health integration
- Heart rate from Android Wear device
- Full feature parity with iOS companion
- Google Play Store listing

### Wear OS Integration
- Full Pulse app for Wear OS
- Heart rate monitoring, trends, anomaly alerts
- Complication support for Wear OS watch faces
- Sync with Pulse iOS/macOS via cloud

### Apple Vision Pro
- visionOS app
- Spatial health dashboard
- Heart rate as ambient spatial element
- 3D health trend visualization

### Open Source Research Kit
- Open-source the anonymization and aggregation engine (pulse-research on GitHub)
- MIT license
- Researchers can build on Pulse's data pipeline
- Ethics board for research approvals

### Integration Ecosystem
- Integration registry at pulse.app/integrations
- Strava, TrainingPeaks, Runkeeper: sync workout HR data
- Calm, Headspace: correlate meditation with HRV
- Sleep tracking apps: correlate sleep quality with HRV
- Zapier/Make/IFTTT: full webhook support

### Long-Term Research
- Research partnerships with cardiovascular research institutions
- Anonymized dataset for academic research (with consent)
- Contributing to open health research standards

### Public Roadmap
- Public roadmap at pulse.app/roadmap
- User health goals voting
- Monthly health feature updates
- Community forum

---

## Out of Scope
- Medical device certification (FDA, CE)
- Prescription or treatment recommendations
- Insurance integration (future consideration)
- Non-consensual data sharing
