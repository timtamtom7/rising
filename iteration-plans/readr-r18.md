# readr — Round 18: Platform Ecosystem, Spatial Computing, Vision, Long-Term Vision

## Goal
Extend Readr into a full platform ecosystem — spatial reading experiences, a plugin ecosystem, and a compelling long-term vision for Readr as the world's definitive quote and idea capture platform.

---

## Scope

### Readr for Apple Vision Pro
- **Spatial quote gallery**: View your quote collections in 3D spatial space — walk through your library
- **Immersive quote reading**: Read quotes in a beautiful spatial environment
- **Quote art installation**: Turn favorite quotes into 3D art pieces for your space
- **Shared spatial sessions**: Share a quote collection with a friend in Vision Pro space

### Plugin Ecosystem
- **Plugin store**: Plugins for citation managers (Zotero, Mendeley), writing tools (iA Writer, Scrivener), PKM tools (Obsidian, Roam)
- **Plugin SDK**: Publish Readr's plugin API — allow developers to build plugins
- **Readwise plugin**: Bidirectional sync with Readwise — highlights flow both ways
- **Twitter/X plugin**: Save tweets as quotes — "quote" tweets from thinkers

### Quote API
- **Readr API**: REST API for third-party developers — access quotes (with user consent)
- **Quote of the day API**: Developers can embed Readr's quote of the day in their apps

### Long-Term Vision (2027-2030)
- **The world's quote database**: Readr as the definitive repository of human wisdom — quotes from every book, every language, every era
- **Idea memory**: AI remembers every idea you've ever captured — searchable, analyzable, connectable
- **Writing with quotes**: Readr becomes a writing tool — seamlessly insert quotes from your library into any document
- **Quote certification**: Authors can publish "certified quotes" directly to Readr — verified, official
- **PhD reading companion**: Academic reading tools — citation management, literature review, thesis tracking
- **Quote NFTs**: For digital-age quotes — a tweet from someone famous, captured as a "quote" — provenance preserved
