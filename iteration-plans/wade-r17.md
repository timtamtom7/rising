# wade — Round 17: Platform Ecosystem, AI Agents, Spatial Computing, Vision

## Goal
Extend Wade into a full platform ecosystem — AI financial agents that proactively manage money, spatial computing experiences, and a compelling long-term vision.

---

## Scope

### AI Financial Agent
- **Wade Agent**: An AI agent that manages money proactively — "I noticed your subscription spending spiked. Should I cancel the duplicates?"
- **Automated savings**: AI moves small amounts to savings when it detects windfalls
- **Bill negotiation agent**: AI reviews bills and suggests negotiations ("Your internet bill is 20% above market — want me to find a competitor?")
- **Investment rebalancing agent**: AI monitors portfolio and rebalances when allocations drift

### Wade for Apple Vision Pro
- **Spatial financial dashboard**: View your complete financial picture in 3D spatial space
- **Net worth visualization**: See your net worth as a 3D object — watch it grow over time
- **Budget room**: Visualize your budget as a room — each category takes up space proportional to spending
- **Financial planning session**: Meet with a financial advisor in Vision Pro space

### Platform & API
- **Wade API**: REST API for third-party developers — access financial data (with user consent)
- **Accounting software integration**: QuickBooks, Xero — sync expenses for tax purposes
- **Payroll integration**: Import salary data automatically
- **Tax filing integration**: TurboTax, H&R Block — export financial data for tax filing

### Long-Term Vision (2027-2030)
- **The financial brain**: Wade becomes your AI financial brain — knows your income, expenses, goals, dreams
- **Proactive financial health**: Wade prevents financial mistakes before they happen — alerts when you're about to overspend
- **Financial planning AI**: Wade as a financial planner — accessible to everyone, not just the wealthy
- **Wealth building for all**: Wade democratizes financial advice — everyone gets a personal financial advisor
- **Net worth prediction**: AI predicts your net worth trajectory — "At this rate, you'll be a millionaire by 2045"
