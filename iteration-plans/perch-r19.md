# perch — Round 19: Team, Company, Investment Readiness, Foundation

## Goal
Build the team and company infrastructure to support Perch as a climate tech startup — preparing for potential investment, regulatory engagement, and sustainable long-term operation.

---

## Scope

### Team Building
- Hire: 1 senior iOS engineer, 1 Android engineer, 1 ML engineer
- Head of Business Development (B2B corporate sales)
- Head of Climate Partnerships (offset providers, NGOs)
- Part-time designer
- Regulatory consultant for EU CSRD reporting

### Engineering Infrastructure
- CI/CD: GitHub Actions for iOS + Android builds
- Crash reporting: Crashlytics / Sentry
- Analytics: Mixpanel or PostHog
- Error monitoring: track which features cause most issues
- Accessibility linter in CI

### Company Structure
- Legal: LLC or corporation for Perch
- Banking: separate business account
- Accounting: set up bookkeeping
- IP: trademark Perch brand, logo
- Climate accounting: track Perch's own carbon footprint (offsets for company operations)

### Investment Readiness
- **Metrics deck**: MAU, DAU, retention curves, conversion funnel, LTV, churn, MRR/ARR, B2B pipeline
- **Impact metrics**: Total CO₂ offset by Perch users (impressive marketing number)
- **Product vision document**: Perch 3.0 and beyond
- **Competitive landscape**: Perch vs. Google Travel Carbon, South Pole, and other carbon tracking apps
- **Growth plan**: Path to $1M ARR
- **Impact thesis**: Climate tech is growing — position Perch as the consumer leader
