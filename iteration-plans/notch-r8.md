# Notch — R8: App Store Submission, Launch

## Goal
Submit Notch to the Mac App Store and execute a successful launch.

---

## Scope

### App Store Connect
- App entry: name "Notch", subtitle: "Make your MacBook notch useful"
- Description: highlights — battery, weather, timer, calendar, CPU monitor all in your notch, customizable, auto-hide
- Keywords: notch, macbook, menubar, widgets, battery, weather, timer, mac, toolbar, status
- Category: Utilities / Personalization
- Pricing: Free with Pro IAP ($2.99 one-time), or $2.99 paid
- Screenshots: 6-8 showing notch bar with different widgets, preferences, mode switching
- App preview video: 30-second walkthrough
- Age rating
- App Privacy: location (weather), calendar (if used), no tracking
- Contact info

### IAP Setup
- Pro: $2.99 one-time unlock
- Pro features: all notch apps, custom wallpaper, iCloud sync, Shortcuts
- Free: date, battery, weather + 2 notch apps

### Build, Code Sign & Notarize
- Universal binary
- Hardened runtime
- Notarize
- Entitlements: network, location, iCloud, App Groups

### Submission
- Upload via Xcode Organizer
- Submit for review
- Expedited review if needed

### Launch Day
- Social posts: X, Reddit r/macapps, Product Hunt
- Update beta testers
- Monitor crash rate
- Respond to reviews
