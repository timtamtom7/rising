# Volt — R16: Subscription Tiers & Monetization

## Goal
Introduce Volt Pro and Volt Enterprise subscription tiers with differentiated features and meaningful value.

---

## Scope

## Free Tier (Core)
- Real-time battery monitoring in menu bar
- Basic power modes (3 presets)
- Battery health display
- Basic charge history (7 days)
- iOS notifications (low battery, fully charged)

## Volt Pro ($1.99/month or $12.99/year)
- Unlimited power modes (create custom modes)
- Advanced analytics: ML predictions, anomaly detection, usage patterns
- Energy cost estimation
- Full history (unlimited)
- Shortcuts integration (all Volt actions in Shortcuts app)
- Advanced widgets (desktop widgets, interactive)
- Priority email support
- Early access to beta features

## Volt Household ($3.99/month or $29.99/year)
- Up to 5 Macs monitored in one household
- Family shared power modes
- Shared battery health dashboard
- iOS/Watch apps for all family members
- Smart alerts when any family Mac needs attention

## Volt Enterprise (team pricing)
- Everything in Household
- Fleet dashboard for IT admins
- MDM configuration support
- SSO / SAML
- Audit logs and compliance reporting
- Volume license pricing
- Dedicated account manager

## Subscription Infrastructure
- StoreKit 2 for in-app purchases
- RevenueCat or equivalent for cross-platform subscription management
- 14-day free trial for Pro and Household
- Cross-device unlock: same iCloud account unlocks on all devices
- Subscription management portal at account.volt.app

---

## Out of Scope
- One-time lifetime purchase option
- Hardware bundle (smart plug, etc.)
