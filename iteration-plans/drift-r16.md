# drift — Round 16: International Expansion, Localization, Regional Adaptation

## Goal
Take Drift global — full localization, regional pricing, international partnerships, and cultural adaptation for sleep patterns that vary by region.

---

## Scope

### Full Localization (i18n)
- All user-facing strings in `Localizable.strings` / `.stringsdict`
- Languages: English (base), German, French, Spanish, Italian, Portuguese (Brazilian), Japanese, Korean, Simplified Chinese, Arabic (RTL)
- Localized screenshots for App Store in top 5 languages
- Pluralization, date/time formatting, number formatting — all locale-aware

### Regional Sleep Norms
- **Sleep norm database**: Different cultures have different sleep patterns — adapt insights accordingly
- **Time zone intelligence**: When traveling, Drift tracks jet lag and suggests recovery schedule
- **Seasonal adaptation**: In extreme-latitude regions (Nordic countries), address seasonal sleep disruption
- **Regional health guidelines**: Customize recommendations based on regional health authority guidelines (NHS vs. CDC sleep recommendations)

### Regional Pricing & Payments
- PPP-adjusted pricing for developing markets
- Local payment methods: Alipay, UPI, Boleto, Paytm
- Regional App Store page optimization (keywords, screenshots per locale)

### International Marketing
- Partner with international wellness influencers
- Localized press outreach in Germany, France, Japan
- "Sleep Around the World" content series: how do people in different countries sleep?
- WWOOF / travel integration: when traveling, Drift adapts recommendations

- Android app (R17)
- Platform expansion beyond Apple (R17)
