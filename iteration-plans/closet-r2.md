# Closet — Round 2: Outfit Evolution, Weather Plus, Sharing

## Context
R1 built: wardrobe capture, outfit generator, style profile, editorial ivory design. R2: deeper style AI, weather integration, social sharing.

## Features

### Outfit Evolution
- Track what you actually wore vs. what was suggested — AI learns your veto patterns
- "Why not?" button — skip an outfit suggestion and tell AI why (too formal, too casual, weather wrong)
- Outfit score — rate each outfit 1-5, AI builds preference model
- Trend detection: "You've been wearing more neutral tones this month"

### Weather Plus
- Pull weather via wttr.in — if rain, suggest rain-appropriate outfits
- Temperature layering suggestions — "You have 3 cold-weather tops, consider adding a sweater"
- Season transition advisor — "It's going to be cold next week. Here's what to pull forward."

### Calendar Integration
- Event context: if you have a meeting, suggest "smart casual"
- Travel detection: if traveling, suggest packing list from wardrobe
- Date night mode — suggest something nicer than usual

### Sharing
- Share outfit as card — photo + AI caption ("Effortlessly elegant in neutrals")
- Weekly lookbook — generate a 7-day outfit grid
- "Dress for how you want to feel" — AI suggests mood-based outfits

### Design Polish
- Outfit cards — swipe left/right through suggestions
- Wardrobe grid — masonry layout, varied card sizes
- Camera capture — overlay guides for best clothing photo angle
