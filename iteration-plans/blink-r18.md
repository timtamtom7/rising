# blink — Round 18: International Expansion, Localization, Awards

## Goal
Take Blink global — both in terms of international user acquisition and industry recognition. Build a sustainable international growth engine.

---

## Scope

### International Markets
- Full localization: 10 languages (English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Japanese, Korean, Simplified Chinese, Arabic)
- RTL layout support for Arabic and Hebrew
- Regional pricing: PPP-adjusted pricing for developing markets (India, Brazil, Southeast Asia)
- Local App Store optimization: keywords, screenshots, descriptions per locale
- Regional App Store page customization (UK, Australia, Canada, India, etc.)

### International Marketing
- Partner with local micro-influencers in key markets (Spain, Brazil, Japan)
- Local press outreach in major markets
- "Year in Blink" personalized videos — auto-generated in user's language
- Cultural customization: holidays (Diwali, Lunar New Year, Ramadan) get special moment highlights

### Awards & Press Push
- Apple Design Awards submission (prepare full submission package)
- TechCrunch, The Verge, Wired product reviews
- "Best of 2025" App Store feature nominations (work with Apple's PR team)
- Film festival partnership: allow Blink users to submit 30-second memory films

### Brand Development
- Refresh brand assets: new app icon, updated color palette, refined typography
- Design system audit and refresh (SwiftUI 5 adoption)
- App preview video for App Store: 30-second cinematic highlight reel

---

## Out of Scope
- Office setup / team expansion (R19)
- Blink 2.0 major redesign (R20)
