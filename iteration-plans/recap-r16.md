# Recap — R16: Subscription Tiers & Monetization

## Goal
Introduce Recap Pro and Recap Team subscription tiers for power readers and organizations.

---

## Scope

## Free Tier (Core)
- 3 content sources
- Daily recap generation
- 7-day recap history
- Basic summarization
- Local storage

## Recap Pro ($4.99/month or $39.99/year)
- Unlimited content sources
- ML-powered intelligent summarization
- Topic clustering
- Personalized digest
- Full history
- Smart recap scheduling
- iCloud sync
- Shortcuts integration
- All export formats
- Priority email support

## Recap Team ($8.99/month or $69.99/year)
- Everything in Pro
- Up to 10 team members
- Shared recap feeds
- Collaborative curation
- Team digest
- Commenting and mentions
- Shared sources
- Team analytics
- Team admin controls

## Recap Enterprise (custom pricing)
- Everything in Team
- Unlimited team members
- SSO / SAML
- Editorial workflow
- Brand voice training
- Publishing integration
- Audit logs
- Volume licensing
- Dedicated account manager

## Subscription Infrastructure
- StoreKit 2 for in-app purchases
- RevenueCat for cross-platform subscription management
- 14-day free trial for Pro and Team
- Same iCloud account → unlock on all devices
- Family Sharing: Pro unlocked for all family members

---

## Out of Scope
- Lifetime purchase option
- Ad-supported tier
