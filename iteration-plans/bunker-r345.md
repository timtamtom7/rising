# Bunker — Rounds 3, 4, 5

## Round 3: Collaboration, Visualization, Polish

### Collaboration (Teams Tier)
- Invite collaborator to a decision
- Shared decision view (both see same page)
- Comments on specific options
- Real-time presence indicator (who's viewing)

### Visualization
- Decision timeline visualization
- Pros/cons visual weight chart
- "Regret probability" meter
- Confidence score gauge

### Templates
- Decision templates: "Job offer comparison", "Moving decision", "Major purchase"
- Pre-fill template with example options
- User can save their own templates

### Notification System
- Push notifications for deadline reminders
- "Decision deadline tomorrow" — 2 days before
- "Have you decided yet?" — on deadline day
- "How did it go?" — 1 week after deadline

---

## Round 4: AI, History, Insights

### AI Advice (Real)
- Connect to OpenAI API or local model
- Analyze options and suggest missing considerations
- Risk assessment for each option
- "Worst case / best case" modeling with AI

### History & Patterns
- Decision history with outcomes
- Outcome tracking: "Did it work out?" — user marks good/bad/neutral
- Pattern detection: "You make decisions faster when you've slept on it"
- "Average decision time" stat

### Export & Share
- Export decision as formatted PDF
- Share decision framework as a read-only link
- Print-friendly version
- Generate shareable image (decision summary card)

### Mobile App
- iOS app for decision management on the go
- Push notifications for deadlines
- Mobile-optimized decision entry

---

## Round 5: Platform, Community, Scale

### Community
- Public decisions (anonymous): "See how others made similar decisions"
- Decision patterns: "Most people choosing Option A cite X as reason"
- Anonymous sharing for inspiration

### integrations
- Calendar sync (add deadline to Google Calendar/Apple Calendar)
- Notion export
- Slack integration (post decision summary to Slack channel)

### Freemium Polish
- Free: 5 decisions, 1 collaborator
- Pro: unlimited decisions, unlimited collaborators, AI advice
- Teams: admin controls, billing, priority support

### Onboarding Expansion
- Guided first decision ("Let's make your first decision together")
- Example decision pre-loaded
- Decision coach: "Answer these 5 questions to structure your thinking"
