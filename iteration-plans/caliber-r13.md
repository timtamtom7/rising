# Caliber — R13: Enterprise & Academic Features

## Goal
Position Caliber for academic institutions and enterprise training programs with class management, progress tracking, and compliance tools.

---

## Scope

### Class / Course Management
- Instructors create classes and assign decks
- Add students via email or institutional SSO
- Class deck sets: curated decks for specific courses
- Course calendar: assignment due dates

### Student Progress Dashboard
- Instructor view: student progress across all decks
- Identify struggling students (low retention rate)
- Time spent studying per student
- Class-wide analytics: average retention, most missed cards
- Individual student reports for advising

### Assignments & Due Dates
- Assign specific decks with due dates
- Due card count per student
- Lock cards until course reaches certain point
- Proctored review mode (can't skip cards)

### Academic Integrity
- Card editing is logged (who changed what)
- Audit trail for instructors
- Student can't delete reviewed cards (instructor-configurable)

### Institutional Features
- Bulk student import (CSV, LMS integration)
- LMS integration (Canvas, Blackboard, Moodle) via LTI
- SCORM package export for LMS
- Grade passback (submit Caliber scores to LMS)

### Enterprise Training
- Corporate training deck libraries
- Compliance training with completion tracking
- Training expiration alerts (renew training every year)
- Department-level progress reporting

### SSO / SAML
- SAML 2.0 for academic and enterprise institutions
- Support for Okta, Azure AD, Google Workspace, Canvas

---

## Out of Scope
- Grading or academic credit
- Live instruction integration
- Real-time classroom polling
