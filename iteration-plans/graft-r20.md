# graft — Round 20: Graft 3.0 Vision, Long-Term Legacy, 10-Year Mission

## Goal
Define Graft's 10-year vision and write the Graft 3.0 product spec — capturing the audacious long-term mission of making deliberate practice accessible, measurable, and enjoyable for everyone.

---

## Scope

### Graft 3.0 — Vision Document
- **Write the Graft 3.0 product spec**: Comprehensive vision for Graft 3.0 — what it does, why it matters, how it works
- **Core thesis**: Mastery is a skill. Like any skill, it can be learned, practiced, and improved. Graft 3.0 is the definitive tool for mastering the skill of mastery.
- **Key innovations in 3.0**:
  - **Universal skill graph**: Your personal constellation of skills linked to a global knowledge graph — see how your skills connect to every other skill
  - **Transfer learning engine**: Graft identifies which skills transfer to others — "Learning piano will improve your math skills by 23%"
  - **Adaptive curriculum**: AI generates a curriculum for any skill — based on your goals, time available, current level
  - **Practice marketplace**: Expert practitioners sell structured programs — verified, rated, iterated
  - **Embodied practice**: Graft for AR glasses — AI demonstrates a physical technique overlaid on your real-world view

### Long-Term Vision (2027-2035)
- **Skill as currency**: A world where skills are tracked, valued, and shared — like a fitness profile for your brain
- **Education reform**: Schools use Graft as the framework for skill-based education (vs. grade-based)
- **Career navigation**: Graft as career development tool — what skills do you need for what job? How close are you?
- **Graft for athletes**: Elite sports training — professional sports teams use Graft for player development
- **Graft for rehabilitation**: Physical therapy, stroke recovery — measurable skill rebuilding
- **Mastery as a human right**: In an AI world, uniquely human skills become more valuable — Graft helps people develop them

### Revenue Vision

- B2B: Graft for Teams (corporate learning & development)
