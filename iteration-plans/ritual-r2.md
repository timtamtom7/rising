# Ritual — Round 2: Deeper Insights, Community, Streaks

## Context
R1 built: morning intention, breathing session, evening check-in, pattern insights. R2: deeper AI correlation, community rituals, streak refinements.

## Features

### Advanced AI Pattern Insights
- Intention → outcome correlation: "Intentions with a deadline succeed 2x more"
- Context layering: how does sleep quality, exercise, social time affect intention success?
- Weekly ritual report — AI-written narrative of your intention week
- Monthly theme detection: "This month you set a lot of work intentions. Relationship intentions dropped."

### Community Rituals
- Share an intention template (not your personal intention — a category/template)
- Browse popular templates: "Morning focus ritual", "Evening wind-down", "Creative project kickoff"
- Adopt a community template to your daily practice
- Community intention streak — see how others are doing

### Streak Refinements
- Streak freeze — use 1 to protect a day when you miss
- Longest streak record with celebration animation
- Miss tracking — visualize where streaks broke
- Streak anniversary — "You hit 30 days. Intention-setting is now a habit."

### Breathing Expansion
- More breathing patterns (4-7-8, coherent, energize, sleep, custom)
- Breathing history — see what patterns you've used
- Adaptive breathing — AI suggests best pattern for time of day / goal

### Design Polish
- Breathing orb — more fluid spring animations
- Intention cards — handwritten-feeling typography
- Streak flame — animated fire effect when active
