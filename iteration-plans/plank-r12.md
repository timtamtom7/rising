# Plank — R12: Community & Team Themes

## Goal
Enable Plank with community theme sharing, team presets, and collaborative theme management.

---

## Scope

### Theme Marketplace
- Browse and download themes from community marketplace
- Theme categories: dark, light, minimal, colorful, professional, etc.
- Ratings and reviews
- Download counts and trending themes
- Featured themes curated by team

### Theme Upload
- Publish your themes to the marketplace
- Set price (free or paid — Plank takes 30% of paid themes)
- Theme listing: preview, screenshots, description
- Update theme anytime — subscribers get updates

### Team Shared Presets
- Share presets with team via iCloud
- Team members can use shared presets
- Admin can push preset to team devices
- Version control for team presets

### Theme Collections
- Curated collections by Plank team: "Best Dark Themes", "Focus-Friendly", etc.
- User-created collections: "My Work Setup", "Personal Favs"
- Follow collections to get notified of new additions

### Collaboration
- Invite collaborator to work on a theme
- Commenting on themes
- Fork a theme: copy someone else's theme as a starting point

---

## Out of Scope
- Real-time collaborative theme editing
- Plugin system for themes
- Video/animated themes
