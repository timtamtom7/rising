# Sash — R16: Subscription Tiers & Monetization

## Goal
Introduce Sash Pro and Sash Team subscription tiers for individuals and teams needing advanced sync features.

---

## Scope

## Free Tier (Core)
- 1 synced folder
- Basic sync between 2 devices
- Manual sync (no automatic)
- 7-day activity history
- Basic conflict resolution UI

## Sash Pro ($3.99/month or $29.99/year)
- Unlimited synced folders
- Automatic background sync
- ML-powered conflict resolution
- Unlimited device connections
- Unlimited activity history
- Priority sync queue
- Shortcuts integration
- Third-party cloud provider support (Dropbox, Google Drive, OneDrive)
- Priority email support

## Sash Team ($7.99/month or $59.99/year)
- Everything in Pro
- Up to 10 team members per shared folder
- Real-time collaboration (presence, locking)
- Team activity feed
- Guest access
- File locking
- Change notifications (email + in-app)
- Team admin controls

## Sash Enterprise (custom pricing)
- Everything in Team
- Unlimited team members
- SSO / SAML
- Audit logs
- DLP controls
- MDM support
- Custom data retention
- Dedicated account manager
- SLA guarantees

## Subscription Infrastructure
- StoreKit 2 for in-app purchases
- RevenueCat for cross-platform subscription management
- 14-day free trial for Pro and Team
- Same iCloud account → unlock on all devices
- Family Sharing: Pro unlocked for all family members

---

## Out of Scope
- Lifetime purchase option
- Ad-supported tier
- Unlimited lifetime storage
