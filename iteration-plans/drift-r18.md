# drift — Round 18: Subscription Business Optimization, Retention, Revenue

## Goal
Build a sustainable subscription business for Drift — optimizing pricing, reducing churn, maximizing LTV, and building a customer success operation that drives retention.

---

## Scope

### Pricing Tier Refinement
- Re-evaluate current tiers based on conversion data
- **Drift Free**: Basic sleep tracking, 7-day history, basic insights
- **Drift Pro**: Unlimited history, all AI insights, smart alarm, programs, ($7.99/month or $69.99/year)
- **Drift Complete**: Pro + health reports, therapist integration, CPAP integration, priority features ($14.99/month or $129.99/year)
- **Family plan**: $19.99/month for up to 5 family members
- **Lifetime**: one-time $149 (vs. $7.99/month ongoing)
- Test anchor pricing via A/B testing (remote config)

### A/B Testing & Analytics
- Remote config for: pricing, feature gates, paywall copy, trial length
- Trial-to-paid funnel: track at each step, optimize drop-off
- Churn analysis: why do people cancel? Survey on cancellation
- LTV by cohort and acquisition channel

### Retention Mechanics
- **Onboarding sequence**: Day 1: first sleep session. Day 3: first insight. Day 7: first program.
- **Sleep streaks**: Earn badges for consecutive days of sleep tracking
- **Re-engagement**: If no tracking in 3 days, gentle nudge; if no tracking in 7 days, special offer
- **Annual upgrade prompt**: "Switch to annual and save 2 months" — highest-margin conversion

### Customer Success
- In-app messaging for support (Intercom or equivalent)
- FAQ / Help Center with sleep education content
- Email sequence: weekly sleep tips, monthly sleep reports
- NPS survey: quarterly, track NPS trend
