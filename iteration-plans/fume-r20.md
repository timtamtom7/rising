# fume — Round 20: Fume 3.0 Vision, Long-Term Roadmap, Legacy

## Goal
Define Fume's 10-year vision and ship a concrete milestone — Fume 3.0 is a vision document, not implementation, capturing the audacious long-term mission of building the world's most powerful personal knowledge system.

---

## Scope

### Fume 3.0 — Vision Document
- **Write the Fume 3.0 product spec**: A comprehensive vision document for Fume 3.0 — what it does, why it matters, how it works
- **Core thesis**: The world has more information than ever but wisdom is scarce. Fume 3.0 is the system that converts information into wisdom.
- **Key innovations in 3.0**:
  - **Memory**: Fume remembers everything you've ever read — you can query "what did I read about X in 2024?"
  - **Synthesis**: Fume can synthesize insights across your entire library — "given everything you've read, here's what you believe about X"
  - **Epistemics**: Fume helps you identify blind spots — "you've read 20 sources about X, all from the same perspective. Here are opposing views."
  - **Embodied knowledge**: Fume for Apple glasses (when they exist) — surface relevant knowledge when you're physically in a context where it applies

### Long-Term Vision (2027-2035)
- **The personal knowledge OS**: Fume as the operating system for human knowledge — integrated into every app you use
- **Knowledge as a public good**: Federated knowledge graphs — your knowledge graph linked to others, with privacy controls
- **Wisdom markets**: A marketplace where experts offer synthesized knowledge — "Pay $5 to get an expert's synthesis of X"
- **Fume for education**: K-12 and university curriculum — teach kids how to build a knowledge system
- **AI + human symbiosis**: Fume's AI agent researches alongside you — a true thinking partner, not just a search engine

### Revenue Vision
- Target: $5M ARR by 2028
- B2B revenue: Fume for Teams / Enterprise
- API revenue: Developers pay to build on Fume's knowledge graph
- Education licensing: Universities and schools
- Partnership revenue: Knowledge marketplace royalties
