# Plank — R16: Subscription Tiers & Monetization

## Goal
Introduce Plank Pro and Plank Studio subscription tiers with marketplace monetization.

---

## Scope

## Free Tier (Core)
- 3 custom themes
- Basic preset creation
- 5 built-in presets
- Local theme storage
- Basic export (CSS)

## Plank Pro ($2.99/month or $19.99/year)
- Unlimited themes
- AI theme generation
- All export formats (Swift, Android, Figma Tokens, etc.)
- Accessibility analysis
- Smart preset suggestions
- iCloud sync
- Shortcuts integration
- Priority email support

## Plank Studio ($5.99/month or $39.99/year)
- Everything in Pro
- Theme marketplace: publish paid themes
- Revenue share: 70% to creator, 30% to Plank
- Marketplace analytics
- Team presets (up to 5 members)
- Collaborative theme editing
- Theme collections

## Plank Enterprise (custom pricing)
- Everything in Studio
- Unlimited team members
- SSO / SAML
- Audit logs
- MDM policy enforcement
- Brand theme enforcement
- Volume licensing
- Dedicated account manager

## Subscription Infrastructure
- StoreKit 2 for in-app purchases
- RevenueCat for cross-platform subscription management
- 14-day free trial for Pro and Studio
- Same iCloud account → unlock on all devices
- Family Sharing: Pro unlocked for all family members

---

## Out of Scope
- Lifetime purchase option
- Ad-supported free tier
