# graft — Round 19: Team, Company, Investment Readiness

## Goal
Build the team and company infrastructure to support Graft as a standalone, growing product — preparing for potential investment and sustainable long-term operation.

---

## Scope

### Team Building
- Hire: 1 senior iOS engineer, 1 Android engineer, 1 ML engineer
- Part-time or contractor: designer for ongoing UI/UX iteration
- Customer Success lead (part-time initially)
- Contractor relationships for localization (9 languages)

### Engineering Infrastructure
- CI/CD: GitHub Actions for automated builds
- Crash reporting: Crashlytics / Sentry
- Analytics: Mixpanel or PostHog
- Accessibility linter in CI: catch accessibility regressions
- Security audit: third-party review of AI coaching features

### Company Structure
- Legal: establish LLC or corporation for Graft
- Banking: separate business account
- Accounting: set up bookkeeping
- IP: trademark Graft brand

### Investment Readiness
- **Metrics deck**: MAU, DAU, retention curves, conversion funnel, LTV, churn, MRR/ARR
- **Product vision document**: Graft 3.0 and beyond
- **Competitive landscape**: Graft vs. Habitica, Todoist, Simply Piano, other skill apps
- **Growth plan**: Path to $1M ARR
- **Team bios**: Founding team story

---

## Out of Scope
- Graft 3.0 vision document (R20)
