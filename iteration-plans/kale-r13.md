# kale — Round 13: Family Sharing, Subscription Business, Pricing Optimization

## Goal
Build subscription business infrastructure for Kale — family sharing, pricing optimization, retention mechanics, and the launch of a B2B product for health coaches and physicians.

---

## Scope

### Subscription Tier Refinement
- **Kale Free**: Track up to 5 supplements, basic reminders, 30-day history
- **Kale Pro**: Unlimited supplements, AI insights, symptom tracking, health correlations ($7.99/month or $69.99/year)
- **Kale Complete**: Pro + pharmacy integration, auto-refill, priority features ($14.99/month or $129.99/year)
- **Kale Family**: Up to 6 members, family health dashboard, pediatric supplements ($19.99/month)
- **Lifetime**: one-time $149
- A/B test pricing via remote config

### Family Features (Kale Family)
- **Family health dashboard**: See all family members' supplement compliance in one view
- **Child profiles**: Track pediatric supplements — dosage adjusts for age/weight
- **Elderly care**: Monitor parent's supplement compliance — alert if they're missing doses
- **Caregiver access**: Allow a caregiver (non-family) to monitor compliance for elderly relatives

### Retention Mechanics
- **Onboarding**: Day 1: add first supplement. Day 3: log first dose. Day 7: view first insight.
- **Consistency streaks**: Earn badges for 7-day, 30-day, 90-day consistency streaks
- **Weekly health recap**: "You took 92% of your supplements this week"
- **Churn prevention**: No logging in 3 days → gentle nudge; no logging in 7 days → special offer
- **Annual upgrade**: "Switch to annual and save 2 months"

### Analytics & Customer Success
- MRR, ARR, churn rate, LTV by cohort
- Trial-to-paid funnel optimization
- NPS survey quarterly
- In-app support (Intercom or equivalent)
