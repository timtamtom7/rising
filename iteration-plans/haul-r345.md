# Haul — Rounds 3, 4, 5

## Round 3: Templates, Weather, Multi-Bag

### Packing Templates
- Save packing list as template ("Beach Trip", "Business Trip", "Camping")
- Pre-built templates with common items
- One-tap apply template to new trip
- Edit and save custom templates

### Weather Integration
- Weather at destination → suggest items ("Rainy in Tokyo — add umbrella")
- Weather forecast for trip dates
- "It's going to be hot — don't forget sunscreen"

### Multiple Suitcases
- Track more than one bag per trip
- Name each bag (checked, carry-on, personal item)
- Per-bag packing lists
- Transfer items between bags

---

## Round 4: Post-Trip, Wardrobe, Platform

### Post-Trip Feedback
- "How was the trip?" after trip ends
- What did you forget? What didn't you wear?
- Learn from past trips: "You never used X — skip it next time"
- Trip rating (1-5 stars)

### Wardrobe Linkage
- Photo of your wardrobe
- Suggest items from wardrobe (don't double-pack)
- Track what you wore vs didn't wear

### Platform
- iOS app (native)
- Widget: quick access to active trip

## Round 5: Community, Print, Scale

### Community
- Share packing list templates publicly
- Browse and import others' templates
- "Popular templates this week"

### Print / Share
- Print-friendly packing checklist
- Share list with travel companion
- Export to PDF

### Freemium Polish
- Free: 2 trips, 20 items per trip
- Pack: unlimited trips, unlimited items, photos
- Travel: multiple bags, templates, weather, wardrobe
