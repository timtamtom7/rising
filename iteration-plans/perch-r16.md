# perch — Round 16: Perch 2.0, B2B Corporate Travel, Carbon Reporting

## Goal
Ship Perch 2.0 — a comprehensive redesign — and launch a B2B product for corporate travel carbon tracking and reporting, opening a new enterprise revenue stream.

---

## Scope

### Perch 2.0 — Major Redesign
- **New UI**: Immersive, nature-inspired design — deep greens, earth tones, topographic map motifs
- **Carbon home screen**: Your carbon dashboard is front and center — today's footprint, weekly trend, budget status
- **Trip story mode**: Each trip becomes a visual story — map, photos, CO₂, offset status
- **SwiftUI 5 + iOS 18+**: Full redesign with latest SwiftUI capabilities, interactive widgets, App Intents for Siri

### Apple Intelligence Integration
- **Siri + Perch**: "Hey Siri, log my flight to London" — one voice command to add trip
- **Predictive travel**: Apple Intelligence predicts when you'll travel and suggests lower-carbon options
- **Live Activities**: Active trip as Live Activity on Lock Screen — current location, transport mode, CO₂ accumulated

### B2B Corporate Travel Product
- **Perch for Business**: Corporate dashboard for travel carbon tracking
- **Employee travel tracking**: Employees log business trips — HR sees aggregated corporate travel carbon
- **Carbon reporting**: Generate CSRD-compliant carbon reports for corporate sustainability reporting
- **Carbon offset procurement**: Company-wide carbon offset purchasing — bulk pricing
- **Benchmarking**: Compare corporate travel carbon vs. industry peers (anonymized)

### B2B Pricing
- **Perch Business**: $5/user/month, minimum 10 seats
- **Perch Enterprise**: Custom pricing — full sustainability reporting suite, API access, dedicated support
- Target: B2B revenue = 40% of total by end of year
