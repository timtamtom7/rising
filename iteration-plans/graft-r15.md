# graft — Round 15: Subscription Business Optimization, Retention, Revenue

## Goal
Build a sustainable subscription business for Graft — optimize pricing tiers, reduce churn, maximize LTV, and create a customer success operation that drives long-term retention.

---

## Scope

### Pricing Tier Refinement
- **Graft Free**: 1 skill, 3 sessions/week, basic tracking
- **Graft Pro**: Unlimited skills, unlimited sessions, AI coach, adaptive plans ($7.99/month or $69.99/year)
- **Graft Coach**: Pro + mentorship platform access, curriculum enrollment ($14.99/month)
- **Graft Teams**: Pro features + team challenges, team stats ($9.99/user/month, min 5 users)
- **Lifetime option**: one-time $149
- A/B test anchor pricing via remote config

### Retention Mechanics
- **Onboarding sequence**: Day 1: log first session. Day 3: first skill level change. Day 7: first challenge.
- **Practice streak preservation**: If streak is at risk, send push notification + "use your streak freeze"
- **Milestone celebrations**: Level up, 100-hour milestone, longest session — animated celebration
- **Weekly recap**: "You practiced 4h this week — up from 3h last week!"
- **Churn prevention**: No session in 7 days → "You haven't practiced in a week. Here's a 5-minute session to get back on track."

### Analytics & Insights
- Track: MRR, ARR, churn rate, LTV by cohort and acquisition channel
- Trial-to-paid funnel: optimize 7-day vs. 14-day vs. 30-day trial
- Churn analysis: survey on cancellation
- Feature adoption: which features predict retention?

### Customer Success
- In-app messaging for support
- Help Center: video tutorials for each skill category
- Email sequence: weekly tips, monthly progress reports
- NPS survey: quarterly
