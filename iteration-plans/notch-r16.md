# Notch — R16: Subscription Tiers & Monetization

## Goal
Introduce Notch Pro and Notch Team subscription tiers for power users and organizations.

---

## Scope

## Free Tier (Core)
- Basic show/hide menu bar items
- Manual configuration
- 3 presets
- Basic usage stats
- Local storage

## Notch Pro ($2.99/month or $19.99/year)
- Unlimited presets
- ML-powered suggestions
- Menu bar heatmap and analytics
- Smart layout suggestions
- iCloud sync
- Shortcuts integration
- Priority email support

## Notch Team ($5.99/month or $39.99/year)
- Everything in Pro
- Up to 10 team members
- Shared presets
- Team presets library
- IT-managed configurations
- Role-based access
- Team analytics

## Notch Enterprise (custom pricing)
- Everything in Team
- Unlimited team members
- SSO / SAML
- Audit logs
- MDM support
- Fleet management dashboard
- Compliance controls
- Volume licensing
- Dedicated account manager

## Subscription Infrastructure
- StoreKit 2 for in-app purchases
- RevenueCat for cross-platform subscription management
- 14-day free trial for Pro and Team
- Same iCloud account → unlock on all devices
- Family Sharing: Pro unlocked for all family members

---

## Out of Scope
- Lifetime purchase option
- Ad-supported tier
