# plume — Round 13: Subscription Business, Pricing, Retention

## Goal
Build a sustainable subscription business for Plume — pricing optimization, retention mechanics, and customer success infrastructure.

---

## Scope

### Subscription Tier Refinement
- **Plume Free**: Track up to 10 books, basic stats, 1 reading list
- **Plume Reader**: Unlimited books, AI recommendations, reading intelligence ($7.99/month or $69.99/year)
- **Plume Scholar**: Reader + unlimited lists, public reviews, book clubs, all social features ($14.99/month or $129.99/year)
- **Lifetime option**: one-time $149
- A/B test pricing via remote config

### Retention Mechanics
- **Onboarding sequence**: Day 1: add first book. Day 3: log first reading session. Day 7: get first recommendation.
- **Reading streak**: Log reading every day — build a streak
- **Monthly reading recap**: "You read 4 books this month — up from 3 last month"
- **Book completion celebration**: Animated badge when finishing a book
- **Churn prevention**: No reading session in 30 days → "We noticed you haven't read in a while — here's what's new"
- **Annual upgrade**: "Switch to annual and save 2 months"

### Analytics & Metrics
- Track: MAU, DAU, books logged per month, session length, feature adoption
- MRR, ARR, churn rate, LTV by cohort and acquisition channel
- Which features drive upgrades?
- Book completion rate as a key health metric

### Customer Success
- In-app support
- Help Center: how to log reading, use AI recommendations, write reviews
- Newsletter: monthly "Reading Preview" — top new releases personalized to taste
- NPS survey quarterly
