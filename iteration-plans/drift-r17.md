# drift — Round 17: Android, Web, Cross-Platform, Apple Watch Ultra

## Goal
Expand Drift to Android and the web, ensuring that sleep tracking is available on any device — while also deepening Apple Watch Ultra integration for the most dedicated sleep trackers.

---

## Scope

### Android App
- Full Drift app on Android (Google Play Store)
- Feature parity with iOS: sleep tracking, smart alarm, health integrations
- Fitbit integration: import sleep data from Fitbit devices
- Google Fit: bidirectional sync with Google Fit
- Samsung Health: integrate with Samsung Galaxy Watch
- Material Design 3 UI — fully follows Android design language
- Android widgets: sleep score widget, smart alarm widget

### Web Platform
- Responsive web app at drift.sleep
- View sleep history, trends, health correlations
- Weekly and monthly sleep reports (downloadable PDF)
- Browser notifications for bedtime reminders
- Web-based onboarding and settings management

### Apple Watch Ultra Features
- **Watch-only mode**: No iPhone needed — full sleep tracking on Watch Ultra
- **Dive mode compatibility**: Track sleep for multi-day outdoor adventures (dive, expedition)
- **Ultra-specific insights**: For Watch Ultra users — altitude sleep adaptation, extreme temperature adaptation
- **Extended battery tracking**: Watch Ultra's multi-day battery enables uninterrupted sleep tracking

### Cross-Platform Sync
- Unified backend: Firebase or custom
- Unified timeline: sleep sessions from iPhone + Android + web all in one view
- Export: full data export as JSON or CSV at any time

## Out of Scope
- Subscription business optimization (R18)
- Drift 2.0 major redesign (R19)
