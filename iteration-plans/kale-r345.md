# Kale — Rounds 3, 4, 5

## Round 3: History, Calendar, Polish

### Per-Vitamin History
- Tap any vitamin → history view
- Last taken date, consistency %, total days taken
- Per-vitamin streak tracking
- "Vitamin D: 23/30 days this month"

### Full Calendar View
- Monthly calendar (not just heatmap)
- Each day shows which vitamins were taken
- Tap day → detail: what taken, what missed
- Color coding: green (all taken), yellow (some), gray (none)

### Low Stock Alerts
- Track vitamin usage based on daily intake
- When running low → alert
- "Vitamin D is running low (5 capsules left)"
- Manual stock count entry

---

## Round 4: Family, Integration, Platform

### Multi-user / Family (Complete Tier)
- Family sharing (up to 6 members)
- Each member has own vitamins and tracking
- Family consistency competitions
- "Who in the family is most consistent?"

### Calendar Integration
- Sync with Apple Calendar
- Show vitamin reminders as calendar events
- Import calendar events to predict days traveling (skip reminders)

### Platform
- iOS app (native)
- Widget: today's vitamins + one-tap check

## Round 5: Health, Community, Scale

### Health Insights
- Monthly consistency report: "You were 90% consistent in March. February was 75%."
- Correlation: "You forget Vitamin D more on weekends"
- Supplement interaction detection (basic): calcium + iron compete

### Community
- Share vitamin routines publicly
- "Popular routines this month"
- Import others' routines

### Freemium Polish
- Free: 3 vitamins, no barcode, basic reminders
- Daily: unlimited vitamins, barcode scanning, reminders
- Complete: monthly reports, family, health insights
