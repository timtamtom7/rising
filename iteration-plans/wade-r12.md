# wade — Round 12: Social Features, Shared Expenses, Household Finance

## Goal
Add social and collaborative features — shared expense tracking for households and trips, financial goal sharing, and light social accountability features.

---

## Scope

### Shared Expenses
- **Household accounts**: Share a household with partner/family — track shared expenses
- **Split logic**: Auto-calculate who owes whom — for shared rent, utilities, groceries
- **Expense splitting**: Easy split options — equal, percentage, exact amounts
- **Settlement tracking**: Track when debts are settled — "You owe me $45" → mark as paid
- **Joint account management**: Track both personal and joint account spending

### Trip Expenses
- **Trip expense tracker**: For group trips — all expenses logged, split calculated automatically
- **Trip wallet**: Pool money for a trip — track contributions and expenses
- **Currency conversion**: Multi-currency trip expenses — auto-convert and split
- **Post-trip summary**: At trip end, generate a complete expense report with who paid what and who owes whom

### Financial Goals
- **Savings goals**: Set savings goals with target amount and date — track progress
- **Goal sharing**: Share a savings goal with a friend — see each other's progress (opt-in)
- **Accountability partner**: Link with a friend — see each other's progress on shared goals
- **Competition**: Light challenge — "Who can save more in March?"

### Privacy Controls
- **Granular sharing**: Control exactly what financial data is shared with partner (transactions vs. just summary)
- **Hide sensitive categories**: Option to hide specific transactions from shared view
- **Read-only access**: Give a parent or financial advisor read-only access to your finances (without login)
