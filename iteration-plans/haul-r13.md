# haul — Round 13: Subscription Business, Pricing, Retention

## Goal
Build a sustainable subscription business for Haul — pricing optimization, retention mechanics, and customer success infrastructure.

---

## Scope

### Subscription Tier Refinement
- **Haul Free**: 3 trips/year, basic packing lists, manual item adding
- **Haul Pack**: Unlimited trips, AI packing suggestions, weather integration, multiple suitcases ($7.99/month or $69.99/year)
- **Haul Travel**: Pack + shared packing, travel companion app, household inventory ($14.99/month or $129.99/year)
- **Lifetime option**: one-time $149
- A/B test pricing via remote config

### Retention Mechanics
- **Onboarding sequence**: Day 1: create first trip. Day 3: pack first bag. Day 7: take first trip with Haul.
- **Trip streak**: Log packing for consecutive trips — build a packing habit
- **"Pack for your next trip"**: Proactive push notification when a trip is approaching
- **Post-trip recap**: After each trip, "How did packing go? Did you forget anything?"
- **Churn prevention**: No trip in 90 days → "Where are you going next? Haul can help you pack"
- **Annual upgrade**: "Switch to annual and save 2 months"

### Analytics & Metrics
- Track: MAU, DAU, trips created per month, item completion rate, feature adoption
- MRR, ARR, churn rate, LTV by cohort and acquisition channel
- Which features drive upgrades?
- Forgotten items rate as a key health metric

### Customer Success
- In-app support
- Help Center: how to pack efficiently, use AI suggestions, share lists
- Email sequence: seasonal packing tips ("Spring break is coming — start packing!")
- NPS survey quarterly
