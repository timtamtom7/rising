# Dreamscape — Round 3: Dream Art, Recurring Analysis, Polish

## Context
R1: dream entry, voice, map. R2: cloud sync, symbol clusters. R3: dream art generation, recurring dream deep dive, design polish.

## Features

### AI Dream Art
- Generate abstract art from dream symbols — AI creates a unique image from your dream's emotional palette
- Dream illustration on save — optional AI-generated visual representation
- Dream gallery — beautiful grid of dream art
- Share dream as art card — abstract, anonymous, poetic

### Recurring Dream Analysis
- Track variations of the same dream — "This is the 4th version of the 'school dream'"
- Dream evolution — "In January you dreamed about water 3 times. In February, 8 times."
- Lucid dreaming coach — tips and tricks when you realize you're dreaming
- WBTB (Wake Back To Bed) reminders — scientifically optimized lucid dream triggers

### Sleep Quality Correlation
- Link dream to how you slept — did a scary dream correlate to bad sleep?
- Best sleep nights — what did you dream about on your best sleep nights?
- AI summary: "Your worst dreams happen after late screen time"

### Design Polish
- Dream entry — star field parallax as you type
- Dream map — 3D-ish feel, symbols float in space
- Dream cards — aurora-like gradient backgrounds
- Smooth page transitions throughout
