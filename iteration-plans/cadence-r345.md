# Cadence Station — Rounds 3, 4, 5

## Round 3: Matching, Community, Polish

### Partner Matching System
- Real matching algorithm (pair two users looking for focus)
- Partner status: searching, matched, in session
- If no partner available: solo session with ambient sounds
- "Partner disconnected" handling — offer to re-match or continue solo

### Leaderboards / Stats
- Weekly focus leaderboard (teams or global)
- "Top focusers this week" — not competitive, just interesting
- Personal stats: total hours focused, sessions completed, streak

### Sound Polish
- Sound mixing (layer rain + coffee shop + lo-fi)
- Volume per sound
- Timer sounds (gentle bell when session ends)
- "No sound" option (silence, just timer)

---

## Round 4: Teams, Streaks, Gamification

### Team Features
- Create/join a team (Premium tier)
- Team sessions: multiple people in same virtual room
- Team ambient sound (shared atmosphere)
- Team stats: collective focus time

### Streaks & Motivation
- Daily streak (consecutive days with at least one session)
- Weekly challenge ("Complete 10 sessions this week")
- Achievement badges (first session, 7-day streak, 100 hours)

### Focus Modes
- "Deep Work" (50 min)
- "Creative" (25 min)
- "Study" (90 min)
- Custom duration
- Each with different ambient sound default

### iOS App
- Native iOS app for starting/joining sessions
- Apple Watch companion app

---

## Round 5: Platform, API, Scale

### Web App Polish
- PWA: install to home screen
- Offline mode (ambient sounds cached, session tracking continues)
- Push notifications for session reminders

### Integrations
- Google Calendar sync (block focus time on calendar)
- Notion integration (track focus sessions in Notion)
- Slack integration (broadcast "focus mode" status)

### Platform
- macOS menu bar app (quick start focus session from menu bar)
- Windows app
- Browser extension (start focus session from browser)

### Freemium Polish
- Free: 3 sessions/day, no stats
- Pro: unlimited, stats, sound mixing
- Premium: team sessions, challenges, priority support
