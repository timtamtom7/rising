# graft — Round 18: Platform Ecosystem, AI Agents, Spatial Computing

## Goal
Build Graft's platform ecosystem — enabling AI agents that practice alongside users, spatial computing experiences, and third-party integrations that make Graft the operating system for skill development.

---

## Scope

### AI Practice Agent
- **Graft Agent**: An AI agent that practices alongside you — demonstrates technique, pauses for you to imitate
- **Music practice partner**: AI plays a piano accompaniment while you practice melody
- **Language conversation partner**: AI has a conversation with you in the target language — tracks what you said, provides feedback
- **Chess opponent**: AI plays chess against you — logs the game, reviews mistakes afterward

### Graft for Apple Vision Pro
- **Spatial practice room**: Practice music in a spatial environment — virtual instrument in your space
- **Overlaid guidance**: Watch AI demonstrate a technique as an overlay in your physical space
- **Immersive language learning**: Practice a language in a virtual café, market, or street
- **Remote lessons**: Teacher and student meet in Vision Pro space for immersive lessons

### Integrations
- **Spotify/Apple Music**: Recommend practice music, log listening as "passive learning"
- **YouTube tutorial sync**: Watch a tutorial on YouTube, Graft tracks it as a learning session
- **Coursera/edX**: Import course progress — "I completed Week 3 of the guitar course"
- **Nintendo Switch/Sony**: Log gaming as skill practice (gaming is deliberate practice!)
- **Fitness apps**: Log physical conditioning for sports skills

### Long-Term Architecture
- **Federated learning**: Improve AI coaching model without sharing user data — aggregate learnings from thousands of practice sessions
- **Skill graph**: A global graph of skills and how they connect — what skills transfer to other skills?
- **Open skill tracking standard**: Publish Graft's data format — other apps can log to Graft
