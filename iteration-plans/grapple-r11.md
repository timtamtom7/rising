# Grapple — Round 11: Topics, Community, Publication

## Goal
Build Grapple's topic organization and public community for idea sharing.

---

## Scope

### Topic Organization
- Topic tags for syntheses
- Topic clusters — group related syntheses
- "Topics I grapple with" personal taxonomy
- Auto-suggest topics based on source content
- Topic-based search and filtering
- "Deep dive" mode: all syntheses on one topic

### Community Features
- Public synthesis sharing (opt-in)
- Community feed: "Popular ideas this week"
- Upvote/save others' syntheses
- "Thinkers to follow" based on your topics
- Community topic leaderboard
- Anonymous mode: share without profile

### Synthesis Editor
- Rich text editing for synthesis writing
- Inline citation linking
- Markdown support
- AI writing suggestions: "You haven't addressed [counterargument]"
- "Synthesis completeness" score
- Version history for edits

### Publication
- Custom publication pages (e.g., mythinkings.com)
- Custom domain support
- RSS feed for your publications
- "Featured synthesis" on your page
- Newsletter signup on publication page
- Export as PDF ebook

---

## Out of Scope
- Paid subscriptions for publications (R12)
- Collaboration on syntheses (R13)
