# Axiom — Round 11: Community Debates, Evidence Library

## Goal
Build Axiom's belief community with structured debates and an evidence library for belief-backed discussions.

---

## Scope

### Community Debates
- "Defend your belief" debate threads
- Structured debate format: claim → evidence → counter → response
- Upvote/downvote arguments (anonymous)
- "Most convincing argument" highlight
- Debate outcome: belief strength adjusts based on argument quality
- Report/block problematic content

### Evidence Library
- User-submitted evidence links with source credibility scoring
- "Peer-reviewed", "News article", "Personal anecdote" tags
- Evidence rating: 1-5 stars from community
- "Evidence for/against" per belief
- Citation format support (APA, MLA, Chicago)
- "What evidence is most cited" leaderboard

### Belief Evolution Tracking
- "Your belief changed" timeline — when you updated a belief
- Update reasons: "New evidence", "Debate changed my mind", "Personal growth"
- "Belief consistency score" — how often you practice what you preach
- Monthly belief review reminders

### Anonymous Sharing
- Share belief publicly without profile (anonymous)
- Follow beliefs anonymously
- "Anonymous belief of the day" feature
- Community belief heatmap (what matters most to people)

---

## Out of Scope
- Real-time debate events (R12)
- Expert verification (R13)
