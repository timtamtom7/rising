# crisp — Round 19: Crisp 2.0 — The Meeting Intelligence Platform

## Goal
Ship Crisp 2.0 — a comprehensive redesign and feature release that positions Crisp as the definitive meeting intelligence platform for individuals, teams, and enterprises.

---

## Scope

### Crisp 2.0 — Major Redesign
- **New UI**: Modern, minimal, focused — meeting library takes center stage, recording UI is distraction-free
- **SwiftUI 5 + iOS 18+**: Full redesign with latest SwiftUI capabilities, interactive widgets, App Intents for Siri
- **Dark mode 2.0**: Complete dark mode redesign with OLED true blacks
- **New branding**: Refreshed visual identity, updated app icon, refined typography

### AI Meeting Intelligence 2.0
- **Real-time AI coaching**: During a meeting, AI suggests: "You haven't spoken in 10 minutes" or "Good point — consider writing that down"
- **Sentiment analysis**: Is the meeting productive, tense, collaborative? Track tone over time
- **Conflict detection**: AI flags when conversation becomes heated or unresolved
- **Predictive agenda**: AI suggests agenda items for recurring meetings based on past topics
- **Meeting ROI score**: "This meeting generated 3 action items and resolved 2 open questions"

### Collaborative Meeting Workspace
- **Shared meeting workspace**: After a meeting, all participants can view, comment, and edit the transcript
- **Action item tracker**: Shared board of all action items from all meetings — assigned, tracked, completed
- **Meeting calendar**: See all meetings in one calendar view with Crisp notes inline
- **Recurring meeting threads**: All sessions of a recurring 1:1 linked together

### Apple Intelligence Deep Integration
- **Siri + Crisp**: "Hey Siri, summarize my last meeting" — Apple Intelligence reads the transcript and generates summary
- **Live Captions**: Real-time transcription during calls with Live Captions
- **Focus mode integration**: When Focus is on, Crisp auto-declines meeting invites that conflict
- **Calendar + Crisp**: Unified view of meetings and Crisp notes in Calendar app

### Enterprise Features
- **SSO**: Google Workspace, Microsoft Entra ID (Azure AD) single sign-on
- **SCIM provisioning**: Automated user provisioning/deprovisioning for enterprise IT
- **Audit logs**: Full activity logs for compliance
- **Data residency**: EU data residency option for GDPR compliance

---

## Out of Scope
- Nothing — Crisp 2.0 is the culmination of R1–R19.
