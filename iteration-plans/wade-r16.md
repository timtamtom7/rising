# wade — Round 16: Awards, Brand, Press, B2B Product

## Goal
Build Wade's brand, earn industry recognition, and launch a B2B product for financial coaches and therapists who help clients with money management.

---

## Scope

### Awards & Press
- **Apple Design Awards submission**: Showcase Wade's conversational AI and beautiful financial dashboard
- **Product Hunt**: Launch Wade 2.0 on Product Hunt
- **Fintech press**: Finextra, Bank Nerd, The Financial Brand — Wade as a fintech innovator
- **Consumer tech press**: TechCrunch, The Verge — Wade as a personal finance game changer
- **"Best of" nominations**: App Store Best of — work with PR team

### Brand Development
- Refresh brand assets: new app icon, friendly financial palette
- Design system: full component library
- Wade's visual language: "Money, understood" — approachable, not intimidating
- Promotional video: 30-second cinematic — talking to Wade about money feels natural

### Wade for Financial Coaches
- **Coach dashboard**: Web dashboard for financial coaches — see clients' financial data (with consent)
- **Client management**: Manage multiple clients — financial overview, budget progress, net worth
- **Secure messaging**: In-app messaging between coach and client
- **Session prep**: Before a coaching session, generate a summary of client's financial situation
- **Progress reports**: Generate PDF reports for clients — income, spending, savings rate, goals

### B2B Pricing
- **Wade Pro for Coaches**: $29/month per coach — unlimited client management
- **Wade for Clinics**: $9/client/month — for therapists and financial counselors
- Target: B2B revenue = 30% of total by end of year
