# perch — Round 15: International Expansion, Localization, Climate Policy

## Goal
Take Perch global — full localization, regional carbon databases, PPP-adjusted pricing, and partnerships with international climate organizations.

---

## Scope

### Full Localization (i18n)
- All user-facing strings in `Localizable.strings` / `.stringsdict`
- Languages: English (base), German, French, Spanish, Italian, Portuguese, Japanese, Korean, Simplified Chinese, Arabic (RTL)
- Localized screenshots for App Store in top 5 languages
- Local carbon emission factors: EU rail vs. US rail have different emission factors — use regional data

### Regional Carbon Databases
- **Transport emission factors per country**: Planes, trains, cars — different emission factors by region
- **Accommodation carbon data per country**: Different energy grids = different hotel carbon footprints
- **Regional offset providers**: Gold Standard, Verra are global; add regional providers (South Pole, Climate Vault)
- **Regional regulations**: EU CSRD (Corporate Sustainability Reporting Directive), UK carbon reporting — support for business users

### International Pricing & Payments
- PPP-adjusted pricing for developing markets
- Local payment methods: Alipay, UPI, Boleto, Paytm
- Regional App Store page optimization

### Climate Policy Integration
- **Country-level carbon budgets**: Align individual budget with national climate targets
- **Climate action suggestions**: "Your country aims to reduce aviation emissions by 20% by 2030 — here's how you can help"
- **UN SDG alignment**: Map Perch features to UN Sustainable Development Goals
- **Partnership with climate NGOs**: 1% of Perch Pro revenue goes to verified climate projects

---

## Out of Scope
- Perch 2.0 redesign (R16)
- B2B / corporate travel (R16)
