# Folio — R16: Subscription Tiers & Monetization

## Goal
Introduce Folio Pro and Folio Team subscription tiers for power users and organizations.

---

## Scope

## Free Tier (Core)
- Up to 50 documents
- Basic capture
- Manual tagging
- Local storage
- Basic search

## Folio Pro ($4.99/month or $39.99/year)
- Unlimited documents
- ML classification and tagging
- Smart capture with auto-detection
- Semantic search
- Full-text OCR search
- iCloud sync
- All export formats (PDF, PNG, text)
- Shortcuts integration
- Priority email support

## Folio Team ($8.99/month or $69.99/year)
- Everything in Pro
- Up to 10 team members
- Shared libraries
- Collaborative annotation
- Document approval workflow
- Version history
- Client sharing links
- Team templates
- Team admin controls

## Folio Enterprise (custom pricing)
- Everything in Team
- Unlimited team members
- SSO / SAML
- Audit logs
- Retention policies
- Legal hold
- Compliance reporting
- Volume licensing
- Dedicated account manager

## Subscription Infrastructure
- StoreKit 2 for in-app purchases
- RevenueCat for cross-platform subscription management
- 14-day free trial for Pro and Team
- Same iCloud account → unlock on all devices
- Family Sharing: Pro unlocked for all family members

---

## Out of Scope
- Lifetime purchase option
- Ad-supported tier
