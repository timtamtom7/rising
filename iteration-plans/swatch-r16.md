# Swatch — R16: Subscription Tiers & Monetization

## Goal
Introduce Swatch Pro and Swatch Studio subscription tiers with meaningful feature differentiation for individuals and teams.

---

## Scope

## Free Tier (Core)
- Up to 5 palettes
- Basic color picker (image, screen)
- 10 colors per palette
- HEX, RGB, HSL export
- Basic color harmony suggestions

## Swatch Pro ($2.99/month or $19.99/year)
- Unlimited palettes
- Unlimited colors per palette
- All export formats (CSS, Swift, Android, React, Figma, Sketch, etc.)
- ML-powered color extraction and suggestions
- WCAG accessibility checking
- Palette history (undo/redo)
- Colorblind simulation
- Priority email support

## Swatch Studio ($6.99/month or $49.99/year)
- Everything in Pro
- 1 team workspace (up to 5 members)
- Shared palettes
- Style guide generation
- Design token export
- Approval workflows
- Brand portal (shareable read-only link)
- Guest access (unlimited)
- API access

## Swatch Agency (custom pricing)
- Everything in Studio
- Unlimited brand workspaces
- Unlimited team members
- White-label option
- SSO / SAML
- Audit logs
- Dedicated account manager
- Volume licensing

## Subscription Infrastructure
- StoreKit 2 for in-app purchases
- RevenueCat for cross-platform subscription management
- 14-day free trial for Pro and Studio
- Same iCloud account → unlock on all devices
- Family Sharing: Pro unlocked for all family members

---

## Out of Scope
- Lifetime license
- Ad-supported free tier
- Hardware products
