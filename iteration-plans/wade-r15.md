# wade — Round 15: Wade 2.0, International Expansion, Localization

## Goal
Ship Wade 2.0 — a comprehensive redesign — combined with international expansion to take Wade global.

---

## Scope

### Wade 2.0 — Major Redesign
- **New UI**: Financial dashboard reimagined — charts that tell stories, not just data
- **Conversation redesign**: Wade's chat interface is redesigned — more personality, more helpful
- **Dark mode 2.0**: Full dark mode with OLED true blacks
- **SwiftUI 5 + iOS 18+**: Full redesign with latest SwiftUI capabilities, interactive widgets, App Intents for Siri

### Apple Intelligence Integration
- **Siri + Wade**: "Hey Siri, log $25 to Wade for coffee" — one voice command
- **Predictive notifications**: Apple Intelligence predicts when you'll overspend and warns in advance
- **Live Activities**: Budget status as Live Activity on Lock Screen — spend vs. budget

### International Expansion
- Full i18n: English (base), German, French, Spanish, Italian, Portuguese, Japanese, Korean, Simplified Chinese, Arabic (RTL)
- Localized screenshots for App Store in top 5 languages
- **Regional bank integration**: Plaid for US/Canada, Open Banking APIs for EU (PSD2), Plaid UK
- **Regional tax categories**: Different expense categories for different tax systems
- PPP-adjusted pricing for developing markets
- Local payment methods: Alipay, UPI, Boleto, Paytm

### Multi-Language Expense Logging
- Log expenses in any language — Wade understands
- Currency conversion for 50+ currencies
- Regional number/currency formatting
