# Dust — R16: Subscription Tiers & Monetization

## Goal
Introduce Dust Pro and Dust Team subscription tiers for power users and organizations.

---

## Scope

## Free Tier (Core)
- Basic focus timer
- Block up to 5 apps / websites
- Simple schedule (fixed times)
- 7-day history
- Basic statistics

## Dust Pro ($4.99/month or $39.99/year)
- Unlimited app/website blocking
- ML-powered intelligent blocking
- Focus effectiveness analysis
- Workload-aware blocking
- Advanced schedules (multiple per day)
- Unlimited history
- Shortcuts integration
- Break timer (Pomodoro)
- Priority email support

## Dust Team ($8.99/month or $69.99/year)
- Everything in Pro
- Up to 10 team members
- Team focus sessions
- Accountability partners
- Team challenges
- Manager dashboard (aggregate stats)
- Shared allowlists
- Team goal tracking

## Dust Enterprise (custom pricing)
- Everything in Team
- Unlimited team members
- SSO / SAML
- Audit logs
- MDM policy enforcement
- Compliance reporting
- Volume licensing
- Dedicated account manager

## Subscription Infrastructure
- StoreKit 2 for in-app purchases
- RevenueCat for cross-platform subscription management
- 14-day free trial for Pro and Team
- Same iCloud account → unlock on all devices
- Family Sharing: Pro unlocked for all family members

---

## Out of Scope
- Lifetime purchase option
- Ad-supported free tier
- Per-minute focus time billing
