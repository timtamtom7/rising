# Blink — Rounds 3, 4, 5

## Round 3: Year-End, Privacy, Polish

### Year-End / Highlights
- Year in review: auto-generate a highlight reel (best moments as 30-sec compilation)
- Monthly highlight reel: every month, a short compilation of top clips
- "On this day": show clips from same date in previous years
- "This day 1 year ago": show same date from last year

### Trim / Edit
- Trim start/end of clip (30 seconds is short — trim dead air)
- Show waveform or timeline scrubber
- Save as new clip or overwrite

### Privacy
- Passcode/TouchID lock for app
- Lock individual clips (hide from year view)
- Auto-delete clips older than X days (optional)

---

## Round 4: Search, Export, Platform

### Search & Navigation
- Add a title/note to a clip
- Search clips by date or keyword
- Jump to specific month/year
- Filter: clips with audio only, clips longer than X seconds

### Export & Backup
- Export individual clips to camera roll
- Export a month's clips as a video file
- Export year in review as a single video
- iCloud backup (opt-in)

### Platform
- iOS app (native)
- macOS app (browse and export clips)
- Apple Watch app (quick record)

---

## Round 5: Social, AI, Scale

### Social Sharing
- Share a clip as a private link (expires after viewing)
- "Blink to friends" — share to contacts
- Public feed: "today's most meaningful clips" (anonymized)

### AI Highlights
- AI selects "most meaningful" moments from the year
- "This clip captures your smile" — AI analysis
- Automatic highlight reel generation

### Freemium Polish
- Free: 1 clip/day, 30-sec max, 30-day storage
- Memories: unlimited clips, 60-sec videos, cloud backup, 1-year storage
- Archive: unlimited duration, export all, lifetime storage, monthly highlights
