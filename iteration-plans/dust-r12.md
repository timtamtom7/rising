# Dust — R12: Collaboration & Team Focus

## Goal
Enable Dust to support team-based focus with shared focus schedules, team challenges, and accountability features.

---

## Scope

### Team Focus Sessions
- Start a team focus session: invite colleagues to a synchronized focus block
- All participants blocked during team focus
- "Tommaso and 3 others are in a focus session" status
- See team members' focus streaks

### Accountability Partners
- Pair with an accountability partner (1:1)
- Each sees the other's focus stats (with permission)
- "You were focused for 2 hours today, your partner was focused for 1.5 hours"
- Gentle nudge feature: "Your partner is struggling — send encouragement"

### Team Challenges
- Weekly team challenge: "Let's collectively get 100 focus hours this week"
- Leaderboard (opt-in)
- Team streak: days where everyone hit their focus goal
- Trophy case: achievements for team milestones

### Manager Dashboard
- Managers can see team focus stats (opt-in from team members)
- Aggregate focus time per team
- "Team is averaging 4.2 hours of focus per day this week"
- No individual surveillance — only aggregate data

### Shared Focus Goals
- Set team focus goals: "Let's all do a 2-hour deep work block Thursday morning"
- Calendar integration for shared focus blocks
- Reminders for upcoming team focus sessions

### Shared Allowlists
- Team admin can set shared allowlists (approved sites for all team members)
- "Engineering team allows GitHub, blocks social media"
- Personal allowlists can still override team settings

---

## Out of Scope
- Surveillance or keystroke logging
- Performance management based on focus data
- Automatic reward/punishment systems
