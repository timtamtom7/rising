# graft — Round 17: Awards, Brand, Community, Press

## Goal
Build Graft's brand, earn industry recognition, and cultivate a passionate community of deliberate practitioners — positioning Graft as the definitive tool for anyone serious about skill development.

---

## Scope

### Awards & Press
- **Apple Design Awards submission**: Showcase Graft's AI coach and practice mode
- **Product Hunt**: Launch Graft 2.0 on Product Hunt
- **Tech press**: TechCrunch (productivity category), The Verge, Fast Company
- **Education press**: EdSurge — Graft as a tool for lifelong learners
- **"Best of" nominations**: App Store Best of — work with PR team

### Brand Development
- Refresh brand assets: new app icon, color palette, tagline
- Design system: full component library, design tokens
- Graft's visual language: "Deliberate practice made visible"
- Promotional video: 60-second video showing Graft in action

### Community Building
- **Graft community forum**: Discourse-based community — share progress, tips, curriculum recommendations
- **"Practitioner Stories"**: User spotlights — how did you master X? What was your journey?
- **Graft on YouTube**: Tutorial series, "A week in my practice life" vlogs
- **Twitter presence**: #GraftDaily — share your daily practice with the community
- **Reddit**: Active presence in r/deliberatepractice, r/SkillBuilding

### Competitions & Events
- **Annual Graft Challenge**: Month-long skill competition — who can make the most progress?
- **Skill-specific tournaments**: Chess ratings, language challenges, music recitals
- **Scholarships**: Grant free Graft Pro to talented practitioners who can't afford it
