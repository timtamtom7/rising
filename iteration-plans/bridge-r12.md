# Bridge — R12: Collaboration & Shared Connections

## Goal
Enable Bridge for teams and households with shared device configurations and collaborative workflows.

---

## Scope

### Household Device Sharing
- Share device configurations across household members
- "My partner can now control our shared smart home devices"
- Unified view of household devices (each person's devices listed separately)
- Privacy controls: which devices are shared, which are private

### Team Shared Workflows
- Share automations with team members
- Team workflow library: common device workflows anyone can use
- Role-based access: Admin, Member, Viewer
- Workflow version control

### Guest Device Access
- Invite guests to control specific devices temporarily
- Time-limited access (1 hour, 1 day, 7 days)
- "Guest Wi-Fi" style device access
- Audit log of guest access

### Device Management for Families
- Parent controls: manage what children can control
- Screen time for device usage
- "Child's iPad is connected — enable content restrictions"

### Cross-Household Support
- Link multiple households (extended family)
- Share specific devices across households
- "My parent's Bridge can access our shared HomePod"

### Collaborative Scene Control
- Create group scenes: "Movie Night" involves TV + receiver + lights
- Invite household members to co-author scenes
- Vote on scene settings

---

## Out of Scope
- Multi-user real-time device control (simultaneous)
- Video/voice integration
- Financial splitting for shared device costs
