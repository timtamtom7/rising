# haul — Round 12: Social Features, Shared Packing, Travel Companion

## Goal
Add social and collaborative features — share packing lists with travel companions, collaborative packing, and a travel companion app for the journey itself.

---

## Scope

### Shared Packing Lists
- **Travel companion sync**: When traveling with family/friends, share the packing list — everyone sees the same list
- **"I'm bringing X"**: Each person marks what they're bringing — no duplicates, no gaps
- **Category ownership**: Assign packing categories to people — "You handle clothes, I'll handle toiletries"
- **Household inventory**: Build a shared household inventory — what do we have at home that we can bring?

### Collaborative Packing
- **"We'll share a suitcase"**: Mark shared vs. personal items — shared items tracked together
- **Weight splitting**: If sharing a suitcase, track each person's contribution to weight
- **Discussion threads**: Comment on specific items — "Should we bring the good camera?"
- **Packing approval**: Parent can approve child's packing list before a trip

### Travel Companion App
- **Trip timeline**: During the trip, see the packing list + itinerary in one view
- **"Did you pack X?"**: During trip, quick check — "Where is the [item]?"
- **Post-trip unpacking**: After trip, mark items as unpacked — track what's dirty, what's clean
- **"What did we forget?"**: After trip, Haul analyzes — "You packed X but never used it"

### Packing Challenges
- **Minimalist challenge**: Pack for a week in a carry-on — can you do it? Track and share results
- **Friend challenges**: "Who can pack lighter for this trip?"
- **Travel hack sharing**: Share packing tips — "I roll my clothes and fit 40% more"

## Out of Scope
- Subscription optimization (R13)
- Platform expansion (Android, web) (R14)
