# kale — Round 19: Team, Company, Investment Readiness, Foundation

## Goal
Build the team and company infrastructure to support Kale as a standalone, growing health tech company — preparing for potential investment, regulatory clearances, and sustainable long-term operation.

---

## Scope

### Team Building
- Hire: 1 senior iOS engineer, 1 Android engineer, 1 ML engineer (health data)
- Head of Business Development (B2B health coach platform)
- Customer Success lead (for B2B + B2C)
- Contractor: designer for ongoing UI/UX iteration
- Regulatory consultant: help navigate FDA/health authority requirements

### Regulatory & Compliance
- **HIPAA compliance**: If handling medical data (blood tests, doctor prescriptions), ensure HIPAA compliance
- **FDA notification**: File FDA notification for AI recommendation engine (FDA- regulated software)
- **GDPR compliance**: EU data handling compliance
- **CCPA compliance**: California consumer privacy compliance

### Engineering Infrastructure
- CI/CD: GitHub Actions for automated builds
- Crash reporting: Crashlytics / Sentry
- Analytics: Mixpanel or PostHog (PHI-compliant)
- Security audit: third-party security review for health data handling
- Accessibility linter in CI

### Company Structure
- Legal: LLC or corporation
- Banking: separate business account
- Accounting: set up bookkeeping (QuickBooks or equivalent)
- IP: trademark Kale brand, logo
- Insurance: product liability insurance for health app

### Investment Readiness
- **Metrics deck**: MAU, DAU, retention curves, conversion funnel, LTV, churn, MRR/ARR, B2B revenue
- **Regulatory pathway document**: FDA notification status, HIPAA compliance, clinical study results
- **Competitive landscape**: Kale vs. Care/of, Rootine, Nutrisystem, health coach platforms
- **Growth plan**: Path to $1M ARR, B2B as primary growth vector
