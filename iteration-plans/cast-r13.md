# Cast — R13: Enterprise & Venue Features

## Goal
Position Cast for commercial venues with multi-zone casting, digital signage, and content management.

---

## Scope

### Multi-Zone Casting
- Cast different content to different zones in a venue
- Zone 1: sports, Zone 2: news, Zone 3: ambient
- Per-zone volume control
- Zone scheduling: change content by time of day

### Digital Signage
- Cast static content as digital signage
- Display images, videos, text overlays
- Scheduling: rotate content by time
- Template library for common signage layouts

### Content Management
- Centralized content library for venue
- Upload and organize content
- Content approval workflow
- Usage analytics per piece of content

### Venue Dashboard
- Web-based admin console for venue managers
- Zone management, content library, scheduling
- Real-time monitoring of all casting zones
- Alert when a zone goes offline

### Commercial Licensing
- Commercial use licensing
- Support for public performance rights
- Compliance documentation for licensing bodies

### SSO / SAML
- SAML 2.0 SSO for venue dashboard
- Support for Okta, Azure AD, Google Workspace

---

## Out of Scope
- Full video wall management
- Live TV channel management (beyond streams)
- POS / payment integration
