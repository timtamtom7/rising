# fume — Round 16: Fume 2.0, International Expansion, Localization

## Goal
Ship Fume 2.0 — a major redesign that makes knowledge management feel effortless — combined with full international expansion to take Fume to a global audience.

---

## Scope

### Fume 2.0 — Major Redesign
- **New UI paradigm**: "Knowledge Feed" replaces library list — a continuous, AI-curated stream of sources organized by relevance to your interests
- **Concept-first navigation**: Navigate by concept ("show me everything about creativity") rather than by source
- **Write mode**: Write essays, articles, or notes that cite directly from your library — Fume auto-cites sources
- **Citation graph**: When writing, Fume shows relevant sources from your library — one tap to cite
- **SwiftUI 5 + iOS 18+**: Full redesign with latest SwiftUI capabilities, interactive widgets, App Intents for Siri

### International Expansion
- Full i18n: English (base), German, French, Spanish, Italian, Portuguese, Japanese, Korean, Simplified Chinese, Arabic (RTL)
- Localized screenshots for App Store in top 5 languages
- PPP-adjusted pricing for developing markets
- Local payment methods: Alipay, UPI, Boleto

### AI Writing Assistant
- **Fume Writer**: AI writing assistant that reads your library and suggests relevant sources while you write
- Works in Fume app (built-in note editor) and via system-wide share sheet
- Citation formatting: APA, MLA, Chicago, BibTeX — auto-applied
- Research mode: Fume suggests "you haven't cited anything about X yet — here are relevant sources"

### Citation & Academic Tools
- **Plagiarism checker**: Compare your writing against your library — detect if you're accidentally repeating ideas
- **Literature review generator**: Given a topic, Fume generates a structured literature review from your library
- **Research dashboard**: For academic users — track all sources for a research project
