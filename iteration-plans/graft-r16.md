# graft — Round 16: Graft 2.0, International Expansion, Localization

## Goal
Ship Graft 2.0 — a major redesign combining the AI coach, social features, and teacher platform into a cohesive skill mastery platform — and expand internationally.

---

## Scope

### Graft 2.0 — Major Redesign
- **New UI**: Clean, focused design — practice session takes center stage, stats are secondary
- **Practice Mode**: Distraction-free practice mode — timer, current exercise, metronome — nothing else
- **Session journal**: After each session, AI asks 3 reflection questions — what went well, what was hard, what's next
- **Skill constellation**: A visual map of all your skills as a "constellation" — see how skills connect
- **SwiftUI 5 + iOS 18+**: Full redesign with latest SwiftUI capabilities, interactive widgets, App Intents for Siri

### Apple Intelligence Integration
- **Siri + Graft**: "Hey Siri, start my guitar practice" — launches Graft in practice mode
- **Live Activities**: Practice session as Live Activity on Lock Screen — timer, BPM, current exercise
- **Predictive scheduling**: AI suggests optimal practice times based on calendar and historical data

### International Expansion
- Full i18n: English (base), German, French, Spanish, Italian, Portuguese, Japanese, Korean, Simplified Chinese
- PPP-adjusted pricing for developing markets
- Local payment methods
- Localization: skill categories are culturally relevant (e.g., Chess is more popular in Russia/India; Cricket coaching in India)

### Skill Marketplace
- **Pre-built curricula**: Skill experts create and sell curricula — Graft hosts the marketplace
- **Graft Certified**: Official Graft-certified curricula with quality seal
- Revenue share: 85% to curriculum creator, 15% to Graft
