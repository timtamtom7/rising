# kale — Round 16: Kale 2.0, B2B Health Coach Platform, AI Doctor Assistant

## Goal
Ship Kale 2.0 — a comprehensive redesign — and launch the B2B health coach platform, enabling health coaches and physicians to prescribe and monitor supplement programs using Kale.

---

## Scope

### Kale 2.0 — Major Redesign
- **New UI**: Clean, health-focused design — daily supplement view is the home screen, health correlations are prominent
- **Health score 2.0**: Composite score: supplement consistency + symptom trends + biomarker progress
- **Visual supplement map**: See all supplements as an interconnected system — which ones interact, which ones complement
- **SwiftUI 5 + iOS 18+**: Full redesign with latest SwiftUI capabilities, interactive widgets, App Intents for Siri

### Apple Intelligence Integration
- **Siri + Kale**: "Hey Siri, log my Vitamin D" — one voice command to confirm dose
- **Live Activities**: Supplement reminder as Live Activity on Lock Screen — tap to log
- **Predictive reminders**: AI predicts when you'll forget a dose and sends a reminder in advance

### B2B Health Coach Platform
- **Health coach dashboard**: Web dashboard for health coaches to manage client supplement programs
- **Prescribe supplements**: Coach prescribes a supplement protocol — client receives it in Kale
- **Progress monitoring**: Coach sees client's supplement compliance, symptom trends, biomarker progress
- **Client messaging**: Send messages, adjust protocols from dashboard
- **Insurance documentation**: Generate documentation for insurance claims

### B2B Pricing
- **Kale Pro for Coaches**: $29/month per coach — unlimited client management
- **Kale for Clinics**: $9/patient/month — for physicians and clinics
- Revenue from B2B: target 30% of total revenue by end of year
