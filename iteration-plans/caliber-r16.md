# Caliber — R16: Subscription Tiers & Monetization

## Goal
Introduce Caliber Pro and Caliber Scholar subscription tiers for power learners and students.

---

## Scope

## Free Tier (Core)
- Up to 5 decks
- Basic card types (front/back, cloze)
- Basic spaced repetition (SM-2 algorithm)
- 20 new cards/day
- Basic statistics (streak, review count)
- Local storage

## Caliber Pro ($4.99/month or $39.99/year)
- Unlimited decks
- All card types (image occlusion, typing, drawing)
- Advanced spaced repetition (FSRS algorithm with ML optimization)
- Unlimited new cards/day
- ML insights: learning patterns, optimal review times
- AI card generation from text
- Reading level analysis
- Shortcuts integration
- Cloud sync (iCloud)
- Priority email support

## Caliber Scholar ($9.99/month or $79.99/year)
- Everything in Pro
- Study groups (up to 20 members)
- Public deck library (unlimited access)
- Peer review
- Class management (up to 100 students)
- LMS integration (LTI)
- Progress analytics dashboard
- Study streak groups
- Caliber Pro for all enrolled students

## Caliber Institution (custom pricing)
- Everything in Scholar
- Unlimited students
- SSO / SAML
- Audit logs
- Custom branding
- Dedicated account manager
- On-premises deployment option

## Subscription Infrastructure
- StoreKit 2 for in-app purchases
- RevenueCat for cross-platform subscription management
- 14-day free trial for Pro and Scholar
- Same iCloud account → unlock on all devices
- Family Sharing: Pro unlocked for all family members

---

## Out of Scope
- Lifetime license
- Ad-supported tier
- Hardware bundling
