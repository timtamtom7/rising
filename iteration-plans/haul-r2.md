# Haul — Iteration 2 Plan

## What was built (Iteration 1 deep pass)
- Onboarding (4 screens)
- Pricing tiers (Free/Pack/Travel)
- Suitcase photo capture
- Tap-to-check packing list
- Category sections with collapse
- Pre-departure passport reminder
- Error states

## What's still missing (Iteration 2 focus)

### 1. Packing Templates
- Save packing list as template ("Beach Trip", "Business Trip", "Camping")
- Pre-built templates with common items per category
- One-tap apply template to new trip

### 2. Smart Suggestions
- "Based on your Beach Trip template, you might want: Sunscreen"
- Weather at destination → suggest items ("It's rainy in Tokyo — add umbrella")
- Trip duration → auto-calculate quantities ("5 days = 5 t-shirts?")

### 3. Multiple Suitcases
- Track more than one bag per trip
- Name each bag (checked, carry-on, personal item)
- Per-bag packing lists

### 4. Share & Export
- Share packing list with travel companion
- Export as PDF checklist
- Print-friendly version

### 5. Post-Trip
- "How was the trip?" feedback after trip ends
- What did you forget? What did you not wear?
- Learn from past trips: "You never used X — skip it next time"

### 6. Wardrobe Integration
- Take a photo of your wardrobe
- App suggests items from your wardrobe (don't double-pack a black t-shirt)
- Track what you wore vs didn't wear
