# Caliber — R15: iOS & watchOS Companion Apps

## Goal
Ship Caliber as native iOS and iPadOS companion apps for study on the go, plus an Apple Watch app for quick review.

---

## Scope

### iOS App
- Full Caliber experience on iPhone and iPad
- Review sessions with spaced repetition
- Deck management and card creation
- Study groups and shared decks
- Progress analytics
- Background sync via iCloud

### iPad Optimization
- Split View: deck list + card review side by side
- Keyboard shortcuts for review (space = flip, 1-4 = rating)
- Apple Pencil: handwrite card answers
- External display for presentation mode

### watchOS App
- Review due cards on Apple Watch
- Glance: "X cards due today"
- Quick review interface on watch: see front → tap to see back → rate
- Streak tracking
- Haptic feedback for correct/incorrect answers

### iOS Widgets
- Small widget: today's due cards count
- Medium widget: due count + streak status + next review time
- Large widget: study progress chart (7 days) + quick start review
- Lock screen widget: streak count
- Interactive widget: "Start Review" launches app

### Notifications
- Daily study reminder (customizable time)
- Streak at risk: "Study today to maintain your 15-day streak"
- "New cards added to your deck" notification
- Assignment due reminder (for students)

### Siri & Shortcuts
- "Start my Caliber review" → opens app to due cards
- "How many cards due today?" → Siri reads count
- "Add a card to Caliber" → voice card creation

---

## Out of Scope
- Android companion (separate round)
- Full class management on watch
- Real-time study group video
