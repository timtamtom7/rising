# Still — Round 11: Legacy, Guided, Community

## Goal
Complete Still's meditation experience with legacy planning, guided sessions, and community features.

---

## Scope

### Legacy Planning
- "Letter to future self" — scheduled delivery
- Reflection archive: all past reflections searchable
- "If I died tomorrow" — life summary from reflections
- Legacy document: AI-generated from all reflections
- Designated legacy recipient (trusted person)
- Memorial mode: share reflections after passing

### Guided Sessions
- Audio-guided meditation sessions (bundled MP3s)
- Session themes: grief, gratitude, transition, renewal
- Session duration options: 5/10/20/30 min
- Background ambient sounds during guided sessions
- Session completion tracking
- "Recommended for you" based on history

### Community Features
- Anonymous reflection sharing
- Community "moment of stillness" board
- "Streak friends" — accountability buddies
- Share reflection as image card
- Weekly community meditation challenge
- "Most reflected-upon prompt" leaderboard

### Reflection Tools
- Daily prompt notifications
- Voice-to-text reflection recording
- Photo attachment to reflections
- Mood tag with reflection
- "Write in the margins" — notes on past reflections
- Reflection consistency score

---

## Out of Scope
- Live meditation sessions (R12)
- Therapist integration (R13)
