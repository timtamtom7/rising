# crisp — Round 20: Platform Ecosystem, Awards, Long-Term Vision

## Goal
Cement Crisp as the definitive meeting intelligence platform with a full ecosystem of integrations, industry recognition, and a clear 5-year vision.

---

## Scope

### Platform Ecosystem Expansion
- **Crisp for Zoom** (native app): Full Crisp integration within Zoom — Crisp appears as a side panel, records automatically
- **Crisp for Microsoft Teams**: Native Teams app — Crisp meeting notes appear in Teams meeting chat
- **Crisp for Google Meet**: Chrome extension becomes a native Google Cloud partner app
- **Crisp Embedded**: SDK for any SaaS to embed Crisp recording + transcription directly in their product

### Industry Recognition
- **Apple Design Awards submission**: Full submission package for Apple Design Awards
- **Forbes AI 50**: Nominate Crisp for Forbes AI 50 list (AI-powered productivity category)
- **Product Hunt of the Year**: Launch Crisp 2.0 on Product Hunt
- **TechCrunch, The Verge, Wired**: Press outreach for Crisp 2.0 launch
- **Speaking engagements**: Submit to speak at Apple WWDC, SaaStr, ProductLed

### Revenue Milestones
- Target: $1M ARR
- Customer count milestone: 10,000 paying customers
- Team plan expansion: 100+ enterprise teams on Crisp
- Partner revenue: first partner payouts via partner program

### Long-Term Vision (2027-2030)
- **Crisp 2030**: Document the 5-year vision — AI agents that attend meetings on your behalf, summarize, and report back
- **Spatial computing**: Crisp for Apple Vision Pro — meeting notes in spatial 3D space
- **Ambient intelligence**: Crisp as always-on ambient recording (with explicit opt-in) — captures serendipitous conversations
- **Crisp Voice**: A voice interface to query your meeting intelligence ("When was the last time we discussed X?")
- **Crisp for Enterprises 2.0**: Full HRIS integration, performance review prep, team health analytics
