# Cast — R11: Advanced ML & Smart Streaming

## Goal
Bring on-device ML to Cast for intelligent stream discovery, quality optimization, and predictive casting.

---

## Scope

### Content Discovery
- On-device ML learns your viewing preferences
- Recommend streams based on: time of day, mood, viewing history
- "You've been watching cooking shows — here's a popular cooking stream"
- Discover similar streams to ones you like

### Quality Optimization
- ML predicts optimal stream quality based on network conditions
- Pre-buffer content based on predicted watch time
- "Your network is stable — switch to higher quality"
- Adaptive bitrate optimization

### Predictive Casting
- ML predicts what you'll want to cast based on patterns
- "It's 8pm on Friday — you usually watch movies now"
- Quick-cast suggestion: "Cast the last episode you were watching?"
- Auto-resume: continue where you left off on any device

### Stream Analytics
- Analyze stream performance: buffer events, quality shifts
- "This stream had 3 quality drops — source issue, not your network"
- Per-stream performance history

### Smart Queue
- Queue management: ML suggests queue additions
- "People who watched X also watched Y"
- Auto-fill queue based on preferences

---

## Out of Scope
- Cloud-based transcoding
- Content rights management
- PIRATE streams
