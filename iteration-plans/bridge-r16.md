# Bridge — R16: Subscription Tiers & Monetization

## Goal
Introduce Bridge Pro and Bridge Home subscription tiers with meaningful feature differentiation.

---

## Scope

## Free Tier (Core)
- Up to 5 connected devices
- Basic device info display
- Manual connection/disconnection
- 3 workflows
- 1 scene
- 7-day usage history

## Bridge Pro ($3.99/month or $29.99/year)
- Unlimited connected devices
- ML-powered device detection and predictions
- Unlimited workflows and scenes
- Usage analytics and insights
- Smart workflow suggestions
- Shortcuts integration
- Full history
- Priority email support

## Bridge Home ($6.99/month or $49.99/year)
- Everything in Pro
- Up to 5 household members
- Shared device configurations
- Guest device access
- Collaborative scenes
- Family usage dashboard
- Cross-household device sharing

## Bridge Enterprise (custom pricing)
- Everything in Home
- Unlimited household members
- SSO / SAML
- Audit logs
- MDM support
- Fleet management dashboard
- Volume licensing
- Dedicated account manager

## Subscription Infrastructure
- StoreKit 2 for in-app purchases
- RevenueCat for cross-platform subscription management
- 14-day free trial for Pro and Home
- Same iCloud account → unlock on all devices
- Family Sharing: Pro unlocked for all family members

---

## Out of Scope
- Lifetime purchase option
- Ad-supported tier
