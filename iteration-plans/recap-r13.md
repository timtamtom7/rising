# Recap — R13: Enterprise & Editorial Features

## Goal
Position Recap for enterprise editorial teams with content approval, brand voice, and publishing tools.

---

## Scope

### Editorial Workflow
- Submit recap for editorial review
- Editor reviews, edits, approves or rejects
- Editorial guidelines enforcement (via ML)
- Version history for editorial changes

### Brand Voice
- Train ML on company's writing style
- Recap summaries match brand voice
- Custom vocabulary: company术语
- Tone adjustment: formal, casual, technical

### Content Approval
- Multi-stage approval: writer → editor → publisher
- Approval notifications
- Audit trail of approval decisions
- Bulk approval for recurring content

### Analytics Dashboard
- Most-read recaps (within team)
- Reading time and completion rates
- Topic trends
- Team contribution leaderboard

### Publishing Integration
- Publish recaps to company blog
- Export to CMS (WordPress, Contentful)
- Auto-format for different platforms (LinkedIn, internal wiki)
- Scheduled publishing

### SSO / SAML
- SAML 2.0 SSO for enterprise dashboard
- Support for Okta, Azure AD, Google Workspace

---

## Out of Scope
- Full CMS functionality
- Advertising revenue management
- Social media management
