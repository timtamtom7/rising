# perch — Round 17: Awards, Brand, Climate Movement, Press

## Goal
Position Perch as a leader in the consumer climate tech space — earn industry recognition, build the Perch brand, and connect users to the broader climate movement.

---

## Scope

### Awards & Press
- **Apple Design Awards submission**: Showcase Perch's beautiful carbon dashboard and AI trip intelligence
- **Product Hunt**: Launch Perch 2.0 on Product Hunt
- **Climate tech press**: GreenBiz, Canary Media, Mongabay — Perch as consumer climate tool
- **Consumer tech press**: TechCrunch, Wired — Perch as the "Duolingo for carbon"
- **"Best of" nominations**: App Store Best of — work with PR team
- **Climate awards**: nominations for Ashoka, Google.org, Climate Tech awards

### Brand Development
- Refresh brand assets: new app icon, nature-inspired color palette, topographic design elements
- Design system: full component library
- Perch's visual language: "Every journey has an impact. Make it positive."
- Promotional video: 60-second video — travel beautifully, travel consciously

### Climate Movement
- **Perch Climate Pledge**: Users can take a "lower-carbon lifestyle" pledge — shareable commitment
- **Partnership with 1% for the Planet**: 1% of Perch Pro revenue to verified climate nonprofits
- **Community climate projects**: Users can fundraise for local climate projects via Perch
- **Carbon literacy content**: In-app climate education — what does CO₂ mean? How do flights compare to trains?

### B2B Marketing
- **Case studies**: "How [Company] reduced business travel carbon by 30% using Perch"
- **Conference presence**: Present at sustainability conferences (GreenBiz, CSRD Summit)
- **Sales outreach**: Target sustainability officers at mid-to-large companies
