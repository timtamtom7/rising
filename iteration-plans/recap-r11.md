# Recap — R11: Advanced ML & Intelligent Recap Generation

## Goal
Bring on-device ML to Recap for intelligent content extraction, smart summarization, and predictive recap scheduling.

---

## Scope

### Smart Content Extraction
- On-device ML extracts key content from source material
- Identify: main topics, key figures, important dates, significant quotes
- Distinguish signal from noise: what matters vs. what can be skipped
- "This article is about Q3 earnings — key points extracted"

### Intelligent Summarization
- ML generates summaries of varying lengths (bullets, paragraph, TL;DR)
- Summarize by theme: extract different angles from same source
- Multi-source recap: summarize a week's worth of content as one recap
- Reading level adjustment: simplify or elaborate based on preference

### Predictive Recap Generation
- ML predicts when user wants a recap (end of week, before meeting)
- Schedule recap generation based on content volume: "You have 20 articles — time for a recap"
- Proactive recap: "Your weekly tech news recap is ready"

### Topic Clustering
- Cluster articles/documents by topic
- Recap per topic cluster: "Here's your politics recap, your tech recap"
- Cross-topic connections: articles that span multiple topics

### Personalized Digest
- ML learns what user considers important
- Weight content by user's interest areas
- "You spend most time on AI news — leading with that"

---

## Out of Scope
- Cloud-based summarization
- Real-time news monitoring
- Social content aggregation
