# Cadence — Round 11: Focus Modes, Sound Mixing, Apple Watch

## Goal
Polish Cadence's focus session experience with custom focus modes, sound mixing, and Apple Watch companion.

---

## Scope

### Custom Focus Modes
- Pre-built modes: Deep Work, Creative, Study, Meditation, Workout
- Custom focus mode builder: name, icon, color, allowed apps suggestion
- Focus mode presets sharing (export/import JSON)
- "Adaptive focus" — app suggests mode based on time of day/task
- Focus mode scheduling (e.g., "Study mode weekdays 9-11am")

### Sound Mixing
- Layer up to 5 ambient sounds simultaneously
- Per-sound volume control with visual mixer
- Save custom mixes as presets
- "Sound of the day" — suggested mix based on weather/mood
- Background audio continues when app is backgrounded
- Lock screen and Control Center controls

### Apple Watch Companion
- Watch app with current session timer
- Haptic cues at session milestones
- Quick start focus session from watch
- Session completion notifications on watch
- Heart rate monitoring during sessions (HealthKit)
- Stand ring integration — focus = stand credit

### Session Analytics
- Weekly focus report with charts
- "Your peak focus hours" identification
- Focus streaks by mode type
- Distraction log: what broke your focus (manual)
- "Focus efficiency" score (focus time / total time)

---

## Out of Scope
- Team focus sessions (R12)
- Spotify/Sleep integration (R13)
