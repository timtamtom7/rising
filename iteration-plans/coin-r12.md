# Coin — R12: Collaboration & Household Finance

## Goal
Enable Coin for households with shared budgets, joint expense tracking, and family financial visibility.

---

## Scope

### Household Profiles
- Create a household with shared financial view
- Each member has their own profile with transactions
- Shared budgets and goals
- Each person sees only their own individual transactions unless shared
- Shared bills tracked jointly

### Joint Expense Tracking
- Tag transactions as "shared" — split 50/50 or custom
- Track who paid, who owes
- Running balance: household member owes whom
- "Settle up" action to resolve balances

### Family Budget
- Shared budget visible to all household members
- Family spending dashboard: total + per-member breakdown
- Alert when household is approaching budget limit
- Goal tracking: "Saving for vacation — 60% there"

### Allowance Tracking
- Set up allowance budgets for kids (if applicable)
- Kids see their own allowance balance
- Chore/tracking integration: "Completed chore → allowance increased"

### Guest Access
- Invite accountant or financial advisor as read-only guest
- Time-limited access (7/30/90 days)
- External guest doesn't see all household data — only shared view

### iCloud Sync
- Real-time sync across all household Macs
- Changes sync immediately
- Offline-first: full functionality without internet

---

## Out of Scope
- Multi-person joint bank accounts (use bank for that)
- Credit/debt management across households
- Investment tracking for multiple people
