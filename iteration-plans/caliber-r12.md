# Caliber — R12: Collaboration & Study Groups

## Goal
Enable Caliber users to collaborate through shared decks, study groups, and peer review workflows.

---

## Scope

### Shared Decks
- Share decks via iCloud with specific users
- Real-time sync of shared deck changes
- Contributor vs. viewer roles for shared decks
- Commenting on shared cards

### Study Groups
- Create or join study groups (up to 20 members)
- Group decks: shared pool of cards the group studies together
- Group progress tracking: see how group members are performing
- Study streak leaderboard (opt-in)

### Peer Review
- Request peer review of a deck
- Review other users' decks and leave suggestions
- Peer review queue: cards pending review
- Approve/edit/reject suggested changes to shared cards

### Card Suggestion System
- Users can submit card suggestions to shared decks
- Deck owner reviews and approves/rejects suggestions
- Credit to contributor in card history

### Study Together (Async)
- "Study streak" — everyone studies the same deck this week
- See group completion rate
- Group challenge: "Let's master 100 cards this month together"

### Public Deck Library
- Publish decks to the public Caliber library
- Browse and import public decks
- Deck ratings and reviews
- Creator can update published decks (subscribers get updates)

---

## Out of Scope
- Live video study sessions
- Real-time co-card editing (simultaneous)
- Tutoring or paid instruction
