# Folio — R15: iOS Companion App

## Goal
Ship Folio as a native iOS and iPadOS app for document capture and access on the go.

---

## Scope

### iOS App
- Full document library on iPhone and iPad
- Document capture with camera
- Search and browse
- View documents with annotation
- Share and export

### iPad Optimization
- Full iPad layout with sidebar + viewer
- Split View / Stage Manager
- Keyboard shortcuts
- External display for document presentation

### Apple Watch App
- Glance: document count
- Quick capture: take photo → saved to Folio
- Haptic feedback for capture confirmation

### iOS Widgets
- Small widget: recent document
- Medium widget: 3 recent documents + search
- Large widget: full library browser
- Lock screen widget: recent document title
- Interactive widget: capture document

### Camera Capture
- Point camera at document → auto-detect boundaries
- Auto-capture when stable
- Multi-page capture session
- Real-time OCR preview

### Siri & Shortcuts
- "Scan a document with Folio" → camera opens
- "Find my recent documents"
- Shortcuts: upload document, search, get recent

---

## Out of Scope
- Android companion (separate round)
- Full annotation on watch
