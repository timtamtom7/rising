# perch — Round 18: Platform Ecosystem, Wearables, Spatial Computing, Long-Term Vision

## Goal
Extend Perch into a full platform ecosystem — wearable integration, spatial computing experiences, API for travel data, and a compelling long-term vision for Perch as the world's personal carbon guardian.

---

## Scope

### Wearable Integration
- **Apple Watch Ultra**: Perch as the travel companion for extreme travelers — track expeditions, multi-day trips
- **Oura Ring**: Pull sleep/HRV data while traveling — correlate with travel stress
- **Garmin**: Pull fitness data — correlate with travel activity levels
- **Fitbit**: Pull activity data — does travel disrupt fitness routines?

### Perch for Apple Vision Pro
- **Spatial carbon dashboard**: View your carbon footprint in 3D spatial space
- **Trip replay**: Relive past trips in immersive 3D — fly through the route you took
- **Sustainable destination explorer**: Browse low-carbon destinations in an immersive 3D globe view
- **Carbon visualization**: See your CO₂ as a visible "cloud" — makes abstract carbon tangible

### Platform & API
- **Perch API**: REST API for travel data — third-party apps can access Perch trip data (with consent)
- **OTA integration**: Connect to booking platforms (Expedia, Booking.com) — auto-log trips when booked
- **GDS integration**: For B2B — connect to corporate travel booking tools (SAP Concur, Egencia)
- **Carbon data marketplace**: Sell anonymized, aggregate travel carbon data to researchers, policymakers

### Long-Term Vision (2027-2030)
- **Carbon as a universal metric**: Like calories or miles, carbon becomes a unit everyone tracks
- **Perch as carbon coach**: AI that helps you reduce your carbon footprint across all areas — travel, food, home energy, consumption
- **Policy influence**: Aggregate Perch data used to inform climate policy — "Perch users have flown 20% less since 2025"
- **Carbon karma**: The more you offset, the more you unlock — rewards for sustainable choices
- **Climate prediction**: AI predicts climate events that will affect your travel — "There's a drought in Southern Europe this summer — consider Portugal instead"
