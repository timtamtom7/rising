# readr — Round 16: Readr 2.0, AI Writing, Citation Assistant, Redesign

## Goal
Ship Readr 2.0 — a comprehensive redesign that makes Readr the definitive tool for capturing, organizing, and using quotes — now with AI-powered writing assistance that uses your reading.

---

## Scope

### Readr 2.0 — Major Redesign
- **New UI**: Editorial, minimal design — quotes are the hero, clean white space
- **Quote-first navigation**: Quote view is the home screen — collections are secondary
- **Card maker redesign**: Beautiful, customizable quote cards with more templates
- **SwiftUI 5 + iOS 18+**: Full redesign with latest SwiftUI capabilities, interactive widgets, App Intents for Siri

### Apple Intelligence Integration
- **Siri + Readr**: "Hey Siri, capture a quote" — voice capture mode
- **Live Captions**: Capture quotes spoken aloud in real-time
- **Writing from quotes**: Apple Intelligence helps you write using quotes from your library

### AI Writing Assistant
- **Quote inserter**: As you write (in any app via share sheet), Readr suggests relevant quotes
- **Citation generator**: "I need a quote about X for what I'm writing" → AI finds best quote and formats citation
- **Quote Remix**: AI takes a quote and rephrases it in your own words while preserving meaning — learn from the masters
- **Summary mode**: AI summarizes a long quote into a short, punchy version

### Quote Card Studio
- **New card designs**: 20+ new templates — minimal, typographic, illustrated, photo-background
- **Custom colors**: Per-card color customization
- **Font library**: Access to 50+ curated Google Fonts for quote cards
- **Animated quotes**: Export quotes as animated GIFs or looping videos — shareable on social media
