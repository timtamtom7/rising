# Bunker — Round 11: Calendar Sync, Templates, Groups

## Goal
Complete Bunker's decision-making framework with calendar sync, decision templates, and team groups.

---

## Scope

### Calendar Integration
- Sync decisions to system calendar as events
- "Decision due" calendar events with reminder
- "Time's up" alerts for time-limited decisions
- Calendar view of all pending decisions
- Two-way sync: complete in calendar → reflects in Bunker
- iCloud calendar sync for cross-device

### Decision Templates
- "Buy vs Lease" template
- "Job offer comparison" template
- "Major purchase" template
- "Life change" template (relocation, relationship)
- "Investment decision" template
- Custom template builder

### Team Groups
- Create team with shared decision space
- Team members can propose decisions
- Voting on team decisions (anonymous option)
- Team decision history and outcomes
- "What the team decided" summary view
- Invite via link or code

### Framework Enhancements
- Reversibility scale (1-10) with explanation
- Stake analysis: who is affected and how
- Time horizon selection: hours/days/weeks/months
- AI confidence scoring: "This decision has X% certainty given..."
- Post-decision review prompts

---

## Out of Scope
- Slack/Notion integration (R12)
- Decision market / prediction markets (R13)
