# Cast — R15: iOS & watchOS Companion Apps

## Goal
Ship Cast as a native iOS app and add Apple Watch support for casting control on the go.

---

## Scope

### iOS App
- Full casting control from iPhone and iPad
- Browse streams, manage queue
- Start/stop casting
- Watch party management
- Push notifications for watch party invites

### iPad Optimization
- Full iPad layout with Now Playing + queue
- Split View / Stage Manager
- Keyboard shortcuts
- External display for Now Playing

### Apple Watch App
- Now Playing: current cast status
- Watch complication: quick play/pause
- Tap to open Cast on iPhone
- Haptic feedback for casting events

### iOS Widgets
- Small widget: now casting
- Medium widget: now playing + queue
- Large widget: full control + queue + devices
- Lock screen widget: now playing
- Interactive widget: play/pause/stop from widget

### Siri & Shortcuts
- "Cast to Living Room" → start casting
- "Stop casting" → stop current
- "Start a watch party" → create party
- Shortcuts: cast content, manage queue

### Remote Control
- Full remote control: play, pause, seek, volume
- Gesture controls: swipe to seek
- Now Playing on Lock Screen and Control Center

---

## Out of Scope
- Android companion (separate round)
- Casting from watch directly
