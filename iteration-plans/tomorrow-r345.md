# Tomorrow — Rounds 3, 4, 5

## Round 3: Recipients, Templates, Polish

### Recipient Management
- Write letters to specific people (not just "future self")
- Recipient profile: name, relationship, email
- "To my future child" vs "To my partner" vs "To myself in 2030"
- Recipient history (letters sent to this person)

### Letter Templates
- "Letter to my future self (1 year)"
- "Letter to my child (born 2026, read at 18)"
- "Letter to my partner (anniversary)"
- "Letter to myself during hard times"
- Pre-written prompts for people who don't know what to write

### Writing Polish
- Rich text editor (bold, italic, underline)
- Photo attachments (Keeper tier)
- Voice recording attachments (Keeper tier)
- Draft auto-save

## Round 4: AI, Family Tree, Physical Mail

### AI Insights (Legacy Tier)
- AI reads past letters and identifies themes
- "You mentioned 'fear' 12 times — here's a pattern"
- "The person you were 5 years ago was worried about X, and now..."
- Cross-letter analysis

### Family / Legacy Tree
- Legacy tier: family tree builder
- Link letters to family members
- "Letters from your grandmother"
- Inherited letters (when original recipient passes, family can access)
- Tree visualization

### Physical Mail (Legacy Tier)
- Print and mail service
- Write letter in app → they print and mail it on delivery date
- Physical letter + digital copy
- International shipping
