# fume — Round 15: Subscription Business Optimization, Revenue, Retention

## Goal
Build a sustainable subscription business for Fume — optimize pricing, reduce churn, maximize LTV, and create systems for long-term customer success.

---

## Scope

### Pricing Tier Refinement
- Re-evaluate current tiers based on real conversion data
- **Fume Free**: 50 sources, basic search, 1 library
- **Fume Pro**: Unlimited sources, all AI features, semantic search, unlimited libraries ($9.99/month or $79.99/year)
- **Fume Teams**: Pro + shared libraries, team features, admin controls ($14.99/user/month, min 3 users)
- **Lifetime option**: one-time $199 (vs. $9.99/month)
- **Family**: $14.99/month for up to 5 family members
- Test anchor pricing via A/B testing

### A/B Testing Infrastructure
- Remote config: pricing, feature gates, paywall copy, trial length
- Trial-to-paid funnel: measure at each step, optimize drop-off
- Churn analysis: survey on cancellation — why are users leaving?
- LTV by cohort and acquisition channel

### Retention Mechanics
- **Onboarding sequence**: Day 1: save first source. Day 3: first AI insight. Day 7: first search.
- **Knowledge streak**: "You've saved 7 days in a row — your knowledge library is growing"
- **Milestone celebrations**: 100 sources, 500 sources, 1000 sources — animated badges
- **Re-engagement**: No activity in 7 days → "Your library has X unread sources. Here's what's trending."
- **Annual upgrade**: "Switch to annual and save 2 months" — highest-margin conversion

### Customer Success
- In-app support (Intercom or equivalent)
- Help Center with usage guides, video tutorials
- Email drip: weekly "Fume tip" highlighting advanced features
- Quarterly NPS survey
