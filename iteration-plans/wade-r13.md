# wade — Round 13: Subscription Business, Pricing, Retention

## Goal
Build a sustainable subscription business for Wade — pricing optimization, retention mechanics, and customer success infrastructure.

---

## Scope

### Subscription Tier Refinement
- **Wade Free**: Log expenses via conversation, basic categories, 30-day history
- **Wade Track**: Unlimited history, budget goals, investment tracking, predictive insights ($7.99/month or $69.99/year)
- **Wade Overview**: Track + shared expenses, household accounts, trip expenses, accountability features ($14.99/month or $129.99/year)
- **Lifetime option**: one-time $149
- A/B test pricing via remote config

### Retention Mechanics
- **Onboarding sequence**: Day 1: log first expense via conversation. Day 3: set first budget. Day 7: view first financial briefing.
- **Weekly summary**: Every week, Wade sends a summary of spending — "You spent $340 this week"
- **Milestone celebrations**: Hit savings goal, reach net worth milestone — animated celebration
- **Churn prevention**: No expense logged in 7 days → "Have expenses to log? Just ask Wade"
- **Annual upgrade**: "Switch to annual and save 2 months"

### Analytics & Metrics
- Track: MAU, DAU, expenses logged per week, budget adherence rate, investment tracking adoption
- MRR, ARR, churn rate, LTV by cohort and acquisition channel
- Which features drive upgrades?
- Average conversation length as engagement metric

### Customer Success
- In-app support
- Help Center: how to log expenses, set budgets, connect investment accounts
- Email sequence: weekly spending summaries, monthly financial briefings
- NPS survey quarterly
