# readr — Round 14: Subscription Business, Pricing, Retention

## Goal
Build a sustainable subscription business for Readr — pricing optimization, retention mechanics, and customer success infrastructure.

---

## Scope

### Subscription Tier Refinement
- **Readr Free**: 50 quotes, 3 collections, basic card designs
- **Readr Pro**: Unlimited quotes, unlimited collections, AI insights, semantic search ($7.99/month or $69.99/year)
- **Readr Scholar**: Pro + citation export, bibliography generator, research projects, all academic tools ($14.99/month or $129.99/year)
- **Lifetime option**: one-time $149
- A/B test pricing via remote config

### Retention Mechanics
- **Onboarding sequence**: Day 1: capture first quote. Day 3: create first collection. Day 7: use semantic search.
- **Collection milestones**: Celebrate 10 quotes, 50 quotes, 100 quotes — animated badges
- **Weekly quote**: "This week in your reading — your most thought-provoking quote"
- **Churn prevention**: No new quote in 30 days → "What are you reading? Share your favorite quote"
- **Annual upgrade**: "Switch to annual and save 2 months"

### Analytics & Metrics
- Track: MAU, DAU, quotes captured per month, collection creation rate, search usage
- MRR, ARR, churn rate, LTV by cohort and acquisition channel
- Which features drive upgrades?
- Quotes per user as a key health metric

### Customer Success
- In-app support
- Help Center: how to capture quotes, use semantic search, export citations
- Newsletter: monthly "Quote Preview" — curated quotes from trending books
- NPS survey quarterly
