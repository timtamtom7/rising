# Pulse — R13: Enterprise & Healthcare Features

## Goal
Position Pulse for enterprise wellness programs and healthcare-adjacent use cases with HIPAA compliance, analytics, and program management.

---

## Scope

### Enterprise Wellness Dashboard
- Admin console for HR / wellness program managers
- Aggregate (anonymized) heart health metrics across participating employees
- Participation rates, engagement metrics
- Wellness score trends for the organization
- Export reports for benefits administration

### HIPAA Compliance
- HIPAA-compliant mode for US healthcare organizations
- BAA (Business Associate Agreement) available for enterprise customers
- Data encrypted at rest and in transit
- Audit log of all data access
- Data retention controls (configurable)

### Clinical Research Support
- De-identified data export for approved research studies
- IRB-approved study framework
- Participant consent management
- Integration with research platforms (optional)

### Provider Dashboard
- Doctors can receive Pulse data via share link (with patient consent)
- Read-only trend view with annotations
- Comparison to population benchmarks
- Export as PDF for medical records

### Volume Pricing
- Enterprise pricing for 50+ employees: 30% discount
- Academic / researcher pricing for approved studies
- Non-profit organization discounts

### Integration with Wellness Platforms
- Integration with employee wellness platforms (Virgin Pulse, Limeade, etc.)
- API webhook to push Pulse wellness score to HR systems
- SSO via SAML for enterprise login

---

## Out of Scope
- Direct medical advice from Pulse
- Prescription or treatment recommendations
- FDA-cleared medical device functionality
