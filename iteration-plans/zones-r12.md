# Zones — R12: Collaboration & Household Zones

## Goal
Enable Zones for households with shared zone configurations, family location awareness, and collaborative automation.

---

## Scope

### Household Zone Sharing
- Share zone configurations with household members
- "Everyone in the family uses the same Home/Work/Sleep zones"
- Unified household zone dashboard

### Family Location Awareness
- See household members' current zones (with permission)
- "Kids are in School Zone" — parents can see
- Privacy controls per family member: what to share, what to hide

### Shared Automations
- Household automations that trigger for everyone
- "When anyone arrives Home → turn on porch light"
- Per-person customization within shared automation

### Guest Mode
- Temporary zones for guests (housekeeper, dog walker)
- Time-limited access: guest can access specific zones for X hours
- Auto-expire guest zones

### Zone Co-Authoring
- Co-author zones with partner: "Our Weekday Routine"
- Version history for shared zones

### Cross-Household Support
- Link with extended family zones
- "My parents' zones are visible during holidays"

---

## Out of Scope
- Tracking without consent
- Surveillance features
- Child location monitoring without age-appropriate consent
