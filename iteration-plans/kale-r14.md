# kale — Round 14: Android App, Web Platform, Cross-Platform Sync

## Goal
Expand Kale to Android and web — making supplement tracking accessible on any device, with full cross-platform sync and a web dashboard for reviewing health data.

---

## Scope

### Android App
- Full Kale app on Android (Google Play Store)
- Feature parity: supplement logging, AI insights, reminders, pharmacy integration
- Material Design 3 UI
- Android widgets: daily supplement checklist widget, reminder widget
- Fitbit / Samsung Health integration: pull body composition data

### Web Platform
- Responsive web app at kale.health
- View supplement history, health correlations, inventory
- Health dashboard: charts of supplement intake vs. symptoms over time
- Browser notifications for reminders
- Supplement encyclopedia: browse supplements, read AI-generated descriptions

### Cross-Platform Sync
- Unified backend (Firebase or custom)
- All supplement logs, inventory, health correlations sync across iOS + Android + web
- Offline support: log doses offline, sync when online

### Apple Watch Companion
- Quick-log supplements from watch
- Watch face complication: today's supplement checklist
- hapticTap to confirm taking a supplement

---

## Out of Scope
- International expansion (R15)
- Kale 2.0 redesign (R16)
