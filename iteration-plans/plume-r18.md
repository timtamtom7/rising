# plume — Round 18: Platform Ecosystem, AI Writing, Spatial Computing, Vision

## Goal
Extend Plume into a full platform ecosystem — AI-powered writing assistant that uses your reading, spatial reading experiences, and a rich plugin ecosystem.

---

## Scope

### AI Writing Assistant
- **Plume Writer**: AI writing assistant that reads your library — cite books, reference ideas from what you've read
- **"You wrote about X"**: As you write (in any app), Plume surfaces relevant quotes and ideas from your reading
- **Citation assistant**: Generate citations in any format (APA, MLA, Chicago, BibTeX) from your library
- **Book-based essay outlines**: AI generates an essay outline based on relevant books in your library

### Plume for Apple Vision Pro
- **Spatial library**: Browse your library in 3D — walk through shelves of your books
- **Immersive reading**: Read in a beautiful spatial environment — a cabin, a library, a park
- **Book club in spatial**: Meet with your book club in a shared Vision Pro space
- **Quote art**: Turn favorite quotes into spatial art pieces for your space

### Plugin & Integration Ecosystem
- **Notion integration**: Pull Plume highlights into Notion notes
- **Obsidian plugin**: Link book notes to your PKM system
- **Readwise integration**: Send Plume highlights to Readwise for spaced repetition
- **Kindle integration**: Import highlights from Kindle directly to Plume
- **Pocket/Instapaper**: Import articles you've saved to read (and track reading time)

### Long-Term Vision (2027-2030)
- **Reading as memory**: Plume knows every book you've ever read — searchable, analyzable, rememberable
- **Book discovery engine**: Plume as the definitive book recommendation engine — better than Amazon, better than Goodreads
- **Writing from reading**: Plume becomes a writing tool — your reading fuels your writing
- **Literary legacy**: Your reading history preserved — share with family, leave as a record
- **PhD reading companion**: Academic reading tools — citation management, literature review generation, thesis tracking
