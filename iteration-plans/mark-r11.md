# Mark — R11: Advanced ML & Intelligent Bookmark Management

## Goal
Bring on-device ML to Mark for intelligent bookmark classification, predictive retrieval, and smart organization.

---

## Scope

### Intelligent Classification
- On-device ML auto-classifies bookmarks by topic, type, urgency
- Categories: Work, Personal, To Read, Reference, Archive
- Confidence score per classification
- "This looks like a work reference — tagged accordingly"
- Learns your custom categories over time

### Predictive Retrieval
- ML predicts which bookmarks you'll need based on time, location, calendar
- "You usually check this article on Monday mornings"
- Quick-access suggestions on new tab
- "You haven't accessed this in 60 days — consider archiving"

### Duplicate Detection
- Detect near-duplicate bookmarks
- "This URL is similar to one you already saved"
- Merge duplicate bookmarks

### Content Analysis
- Analyze bookmarked page content
- Extract: title, description, key topics, reading time
- "This article will take 15 minutes to read"
- Tag extraction: auto-suggest tags based on content

### Smart Archiving
- Auto-archive bookmarks older than threshold
- "This news article is 6 months old — move to archive"
- Archive vs. delete suggestions

---

## Out of Scope
- Cloud-based content extraction
- Automatic screenshot of pages
- Social bookmark sharing (yet)
