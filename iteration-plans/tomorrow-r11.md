# Tomorrow — Round 11: Physical Mail, Templates, Legacy

## Goal
Ship Tomorrow's physical mail feature and expand letter templates. Add legacy planning for long-term letter preservation.

---

## Scope

### Physical Mail
- Print-and-mail service integration (Stamps.com / Lob.com API)
- Paper quality selection: standard, premium cardstock, recycled
- Envelope options: standard, handwritten-style
- Delivery confirmation tracking
- International mail support (US + 20 countries)
- Bulk mail pricing tiers

### Letter Templates
- "Template Pack: Holidays" — 10 holiday templates
- "Template Pack: Anniversaries" — romantic templates
- "Template Pack: Milestones" — graduation, birthday, new baby
- Custom template editor with photo slots + text areas
- Template bundling and gifting
- "Make it a template" — save any sent letter as reusable template

### AI Enhancements
- AI writing assistant for letter drafts
- "Suggested openers" based on relationship type
- Sentiment analysis: "This letter sounds quite serious — lighter tone?"
- Multilingual translation suggestions
- "Letter readability" score

### Legacy Planning
- Letter archive with full search
- "If I pass away" instructions document
- Designated legacy contact who receives access
- "Memorial letters" pre-written for family
- Annual letter to future self (scheduled delivery)

---

## Out of Scope
- Video letter attachments (R12)
- Live letter writing sessions (R13)
