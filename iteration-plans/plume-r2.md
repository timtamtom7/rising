# Plume — Iteration 2 Plan

## What was built (Iteration 1 deep pass)
- Onboarding (4 screens)
- Pricing tiers (Free/Reader/Scholar)
- Book cover camera with frame guide
- Reading pace + finish date prediction
- Monthly reading chart (Swift Charts)
- Error states

## What's still missing (Iteration 2 focus)

### 1. Social / Sharing
- Share quote as beautiful image (multiple templates)
- Share to Instagram/Stories
- "Quote of the day" notification

### 2. Reading Lists
- "Want to read" list (before you start)
- "Currently reading" (active)
- "Finished" (completed)
- Mark books as abandoned (didn't finish)

### 3. Notes & Highlights
- Add personal notes to specific books
- Highlight specific passages within a quote
- Link notes to specific pages

### 4. Reading Challenges
- Set annual reading goal (e.g., 24 books this year)
- Monthly mini-challenge ("Read a biography this month")
- Progress toward goal

### 5. Book Recommendations
- Based on genres you read (detect from book titles)
- "Readers who liked X also read Y"
- Curated lists (via API or manual)

### 6. Export & Citations
- Export all quotes as PDF book
- Export to Readwise (real integration)
- Export quotes to Notion/Obsidian
- Citation formats: APA, MLA, Chicago

### 7. Widget
- Home screen widget: current book + progress
- Small widget: current page
- Medium widget: book cover + pace + finish date

## Custom Graphics for Iteration 2
- Shareable quote card templates (3-4 designs)
- Reading goal progress visual
- Book recommendation cards
- Widget mockups
