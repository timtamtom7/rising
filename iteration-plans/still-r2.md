# Still — Round 2: Ambient Sound, Memory Lane, AI Refinements

## Context
R1 built: evening ritual, breathing orb, reflection flow, week review, archive. R2: ambient soundscapes, memory lane, deeper AI questions.

## Features

### Ambient Soundscapes
- Background sounds while reflecting — rain, fireplace, brown noise, ocean, or silence
- Sound fades out slowly as you type
- Optional ambient track during breathing orb

### Memory Lane
- "One year ago tonight" — on the anniversary of a reflection, surface it back to you
- Monthly memory: "A month ago you wrote about X. Has anything changed?"
- "This day in history" — show reflections from this exact date across all years

### AI Question Refinement
- Questions become personalized over time — AI learns what kind of questions you respond to most deeply
- Question categories: What exhausted you? What surprised you? What did you almost say? What are you avoiding? What are you grateful for?
- Let user rate each question's depth — AI uses this to select better questions

### Deeper Week Review
- Week over week comparison: "You reflected 3 more times than last week"
- Week theme: "This week was about transitions — multiple reflections mentioned starting or ending things"
- Momentum score: "Your reflection practice is getting deeper"
- Sunday preview: AI prepares questions for the week ahead

### Design Polish
- Orb glow — more fluid, organic animation
- Question text — typewriter effect as it appears
- Archive — cards laid out like pages in a book
- Haptics — gentle pulse with each breath phase
