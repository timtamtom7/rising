# Recap — R12: Collaboration & Team Recaps

## Goal
Enable Recap for teams with shared recap feeds, collaborative curation, and team digest features.

---

## Scope

### Team Recap Feed
- Share recap feeds with team members
- Team members contribute to shared feed
- Real-time sync of shared recaps
- Role-based access: Admin, Editor, Viewer

### Collaborative Curation
- Team members vote on which articles deserve a full recap
- "Upvote this article for the team digest"
- Curated team digest: top-voted content summarized for the team

### Team Digest
- Weekly/monthly team digest combining all members' reads
- "The team read 50 articles this week — top 10 summarized"
- Anonymous or attributed (team chooses)
- Distribution: email, Slack, or in-app

### Shared Sources
- Team admins can add shared sources (feeds, newsletters)
- All team members receive content from shared sources
- Source contribution: any team member can add a source

### Commenting
- Comment on shared recaps
- @mention teammates
- Discussion threads per article

### Guest Access
- Invite external collaborators as guests
- Read-only or contributor access
- Time-limited links

---

## Out of Scope
- Real-time collaborative reading
- Video/audio content summarization (yet)
- Full social network
