# Mark — R16: Subscription Tiers & Monetization

## Goal
Introduce Mark Pro and Mark Team subscription tiers for power users and organizations.

---

## Scope

## Free Tier (Core)
- Up to 100 bookmarks
- Basic organization (folders, tags)
- Basic search
- 30-day history
- Safari extension
- Local storage

## Mark Pro ($3.99/month or $29.99/year)
- Unlimited bookmarks
- ML intelligent classification
- Predictive retrieval
- Duplicate detection
- Smart archiving
- Full-text search
- Reader mode
- iCloud sync
- Shortcuts integration
- Priority email support

## Mark Team ($7.99/month or $59.99/year)
- Everything in Pro
- Up to 10 team members
- Shared bookmark libraries
- Collaborative tagging
- Team knowledge base
- Collections
- Guest access
- Team analytics
- Team admin controls

## Mark Enterprise (custom pricing)
- Everything in Team
- Unlimited team members
- SSO / SAML
- Audit logs
- Retention policies
- Compliance documentation
- Volume licensing
- Dedicated account manager

## Subscription Infrastructure
- StoreKit 2 for in-app purchases
- RevenueCat for cross-platform subscription management
- 14-day free trial for Pro and Team
- Same iCloud account → unlock on all devices
- Family Sharing: Pro unlocked for all family members

---

## Out of Scope
- Lifetime purchase option
- Ad-supported free tier
