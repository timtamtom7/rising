# Drift — Iteration 2 Plan

## What was built (Iteration 1 deep pass)
- Onboarding (4 screens)
- Pricing tiers (Free/Insights/Complete)
- Sleep score ring, sleep stages bar
- AI insight card
- Weekly + History views
- Error states

## What's still missing (Iteration 2 focus)

### 1. Deeper Health Integration
- Heart rate variability (HRV) analysis
- Respiratory rate overnight
- Blood oxygen (if Apple Watch supports)
- Temperature wrist data (Apple Watch Series 8+)
- Correlate sleep quality with: exercise, caffeine, alcohol, stress

### 2. AI Insights (More Advanced)
- "This is your best sleep in X days" — contextual comparison
- "Your sleep debt is X hours" — cumulative fatigue calculation
- Correlation insights: "You slept 20% better on days you exercised"
- Weekly sleep report (text summary, every Sunday)
- Monthly sleep report with trends

### 3. Smart Alarms / Wake
- Smart alarm: wake up at optimal time (within window, during light sleep)
- Gentle wake vibration pattern (not jarring)
- Sunrise alarm simulation (watch screen brightens)

### 4. Trends & Visualization
- 30-day sleep quality trend line
- Compare this month vs last month
- Best/worst sleep nights
- Average bedtime and wake time
- Sleep efficiency (% time asleep while in bed)

### 5. Notifications
- Morning summary notification (when sleep data syncs)
- "Weekly report ready" — Sunday evening
- Low sleep warning ("You only slept 4h last night. Here's why that matters.")

### 6. Family / Multi-user
- Share sleep data with family member (Complete tier)
- Compare sleep patterns with partner

## Custom Graphics for Iteration 2
- HRV trend chart
- Sleep debt visualization
- Correlation scatter plot (exercise vs sleep quality)
- Smart alarm UI
- Weekly report template
