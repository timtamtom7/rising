# Plank — R13: Enterprise & Brand Management

## Goal
Position Plank for enterprises and agencies with brand-prescribed themes, MDM deployment, and team management.

---

## Scope

### Brand Theme Enforcement
- IT/brand team creates official themes
- Push themes to employee Macs via MDM
- Lock themes so employees can't override (configurable)
- "This Mac is set to Company Dark Theme — contact IT to change"

### Multi-Brand Support
- Agencies managing multiple clients: switch between brand themes
- Quick brand switcher
- Per-brand settings preserved

### Agency Dashboard
- Web-based dashboard at agency.plank.app
- Manage client themes
- View deployment status
- Client portal: clients can request theme changes

### Compliance & Audit
- Audit log: theme changes, who changed what and when
- Export audit log as CSV
- SOC 2 Type II documentation
- GDPR/CCPA compliance

### Volume Licensing
- Apple VPP enrollment for volume deployment
- Enterprise pricing tiers
- License management via MDM

### SSO / SAML
- SAML 2.0 SSO for dashboard
- Support for Okta, Azure AD, Google Workspace

---

## Out of Scope
- Full DLP (Data Loss Prevention)
- Custom OS theme injection
- Hardware configuration management
