# blink — Round 20: Blink 2.0 — The Memory OS

## Goal
Ship Blink 2.0 — a major redesign and feature release that positions Blink as "The Memory OS": the definitive platform for capturing, preserving, and reliving life's moments across the entire Apple ecosystem and beyond.

---

## Scope

### Blink 2.0 — Major Redesign
- **New UI paradigm**: The calendar is replaced with "Memory Streams" — a continuous, AI-curated flow of moments organized by time + theme + people
- **Unified capture UI**: One button to capture any moment (photo burst, video clip, audio note, text note) — all go into Blink
- **Memory People**: Blink identifies people in clips, creates "Memory People" albums — every moment with Mom, every moment with your best friend
- **Place Memory**: Clips auto-tagged with location, creating "Place Memories" — every moment at the cabin, at the beach
- **SwiftUI 5 + iOS 18+**: Full redesign using SwiftUI 5, new iOS 18 features (windowing, interactive widgets, App Intents)
- **Dark mode 2.0**: Fully revisited dark mode with OLED true blacks

### Apple Intelligence Integration
- **Memory Movie 2.0**: Apple Intelligence-powered memory movies with natural language prompts ("make me a highlight reel of this summer")
- **Live Captions**: Real-time transcription and captioning during recording
- **Siri integration**: "Hey Siri, capture a moment" — Live Captures a 10-second clip
- **Visual Look Up**: Identify places, objects in clips for auto-tagging

### New Subscription Tier: Blink Pro
- **Blink Pro**: $9.99/month or $79.99/year
- Everything in Archive: unlimited storage, all platforms, AI features, shared circles, cross-platform sync
- **+ Memory Cloud**: E2E encrypted cloud backup, 1TB storage
- **+ Priority AI**: faster AI processing, exclusive AI features (Memory Deep Dive, timeline reconstruction)
- **+ Print Shop**: Order prints of clip stills, physical photo books, custom calendars

### Community & Creator
- **Blink Creators**: A creator program for professional videographers to sell preset "Memory Styles" (color grade, music overlay, transitions)
- **Creator dashboard**: Analytics, earnings, reviews
- **Blink Premiere**: Share a clip as a "premiere" — scheduled public debut, like a film premiere for a moment

### Long-Term Vision Document
- Write Blink's 5-year vision: where is personal memory capture going?
- What does Blink look like in 2030? (AR glasses, AI agents, spatial computing)
- Document key bets: what technologies to watch, what to build

---

## Out of Scope
- Nothing. Blink 2.0 is the culmination of R1–R19.
