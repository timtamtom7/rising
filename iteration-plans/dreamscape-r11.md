# Dreamscape — Round 11: Dream Journal, Visualization, Community

## Goal
Complete Dreamscape's dream experience with a dream journal, lucid dreaming tips, and community dream sharing.

---

## Scope

### Dream Journal
- Morning prompt: "Record your dream before you forget"
- Audio dream recording (Speech-to-text auto-transcription)
- Dream quality rating (vivid/clear/fuzzy/forgotten)
- Lucid dreaming toggle and tip
- "Dreams I want to revisit" bookmark
- Full dream archive with search

### Dream Visualization
- AI-generated dream imagery (CoreML + Stable Diffusion on-device)
- Mood/emotion visualization as abstract art
- Dream symbol visual dictionary
- Before/after: dream description → visual
- "Dream gallery" of all your visualizations
- Export as wallpaper/lock screen image

### Community Features
- Anonymous dream sharing
- "Lucid dreamers" group
- Dream symbol dictionary (community-built)
- "Dreams about [symbol]" search across community
- Weekly "most relatable dream" feature

### Lucid Dreaming Tools
- Reality check reminders throughout day
- MILD (Mnemonic Induction of Lucid Dreams) technique guide
- WBTB (Wake Back To Bed) alarm settings
- Dream journal streak tracker
- "Signs you're about to lucid dream" checklist

---

## Out of Scope
- Sleep stage tracking with wearables (R12)
- Dream recording headband hardware (R13)
