# Pulse — R12: Family & Care Team Sharing

## Goal
Enable Pulse users to share health data with family members, caregivers, or healthcare providers for collective wellness awareness.

---

## Scope

### Family Circle
- Invite up to 5 family members to a private "Care Circle"
- Each member sees others' heart rate trends (not raw data — trends and highlights)
- "Dad's resting HR has been elevated for 3 days" alert
- Privacy controls: what each person shares and sees

### Care Team Access
- Generate a read-only share link for healthcare providers
- Time-bounded: link expires after 7/30/90 days (configurable)
- Exportable PDF summary for doctor visits
- What the doctor sees: trends, averages, anomaly history, not raw minute-by-minute data

### Guardian Monitoring (with consent)
- Parents can monitor children's heart rate (with child's Apple Watch, with consent)
- Alert when child's HR goes outside normal range
- School mode: monitor during school hours, pause outside school

### Shared Goals
- Family health challenges: "Let's all improve our resting HR by 5% this month"
- Group streaks: family check-in streak
- Leaderboard (opt-in): who has the best sleep HR this week

### Emergency Contact
- Set emergency contacts who get notified of cardiac events
- Configurable thresholds: "If HR exceeds 180 for more than 10 minutes → alert emergency contact"
- Clear disclaimer: this is not a medical device, not a substitute for 911

### HIPAA Consideration
- Document how health data is handled in care circle context
- BAA available for healthcare providers (enterprise tier)

---

## Out of Scope
- Real-time video/audio calling within Pulse
- Prescription or medication tracking
- Integration with electronic health records (EHR)
