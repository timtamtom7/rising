# Perch — Rounds 3, 4, 5

## Round 3: CO2, Multi-Trip, Polish

### CO₂ Expansion
- CO₂ total for the year
- CO₂ breakdown by transport type (flight, train, car)
- "Your travel carbon footprint this month"
- Compare to average traveler

### Multi-trip Management
- Run multiple trips simultaneously
- Trip comparison: "This trip was 3 days longer than your average"
- Trip diary: add notes to specific cities in a trip
- "Cities you've visited X times"

### Automatic Detection
- Auto-detect when user is traveling (significant location change + distance from home)
- Prompt: "Are you on a trip?"
- Auto-end when back home

---

## Round 4: Export, Social, Platform

### Itinerary Export
- Export trip as PDF itinerary
- Share trip summary with family/friends
- Export to Apple Maps / Google Maps
- Print-friendly version

### Privacy / Sharing
- "Private trip" — don't show in travel history
- Share trip summary (public link)
- Trip photo gallery

### Platform
- iOS app (native)
- Apple Watch app (start/end trip from watch)
- macOS app

---

## Round 5: Community, Integration, Scale

### World Map
- Interactive world map with visited countries highlighted
- Countries colored by visit count
- "Heat map" style

### Integrations
- Google Maps / Apple Maps (show trip route)
- Flight tracker (auto-import flights from email)
- Photos app (trip photos from camera roll)

### Freemium Polish
- Free: 3 trips, 30-day history, no CO₂
- Wander: unlimited trips, 90-day history, CO₂ tracking
- Explorer: lifetime history, insights, map export, multiple trips
