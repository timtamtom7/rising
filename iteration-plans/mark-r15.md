# Mark — R15: iOS Companion App

## Goal
Ship Mark as a native iOS and iPadOS app for bookmark management and reading on the go.

---

## Scope

### iOS App
- Full bookmark management on iPhone and iPad
- Browse, search, organize bookmarks
- Read saved articles with reader mode
- Collections and tags
- Push notifications for shared bookmarks

### iPad Optimization
- Full iPad layout with sidebar + content
- Split View / Stage Manager
- Keyboard shortcuts
- External display for reading

### Apple Watch App
- Watch glance: bookmark count
- Quick add bookmark
- Complication: "New Bookmark"
- Haptic confirmation

### iOS Widgets
- Small widget: recent bookmark
- Medium widget: recent + quick access
- Large widget: full library + collections + search
- Lock screen widget: recent bookmark
- Interactive widget: add bookmark

### Safari Extension
- "Save to Mark" from Safari share sheet
- Quick-add bookmark with tags
- "Read Later" from Safari

### Siri & Shortcuts
- "Bookmark this page" → from Safari
- "Add a bookmark to Mark"
- "Find my bookmarks about X"
- Shortcuts: add bookmark, search, list collections

---

## Out of Scope
- Android companion (separate round)
- Full reading on watch
