# Dreamscape — Round 9: Family Dreams, Legacy, Archive

## Features

### Family Dream Sharing
- Share dream symbol with partner/family (opt-in)
- "Our recurring dream" — track dreams you share with someone
- Compare dream maps — "We both dream about water a lot"

### Legacy / Archive
- Dream archive — all dreams, searchable, exportable
- Annual dream review — AI-written year in dreams
- "If you died tomorrow" — your dream life distilled
- Memorial mode — share with someone after you're gone

### Advanced Analytics
- Dream frequency by season, by year
- Most common emotions in dreams over time
- First-remembered-dream vs now — how has your dreaming changed?
- Dream prediction — "You tend to have anxious dreams before big decisions"

## Design Polish
- Legacy view — archival, book-like
- Analytics dashboard — data visualization
- Memorial mode — soft, dignified design
