# Wade — Iteration 2 Plan

## What was built (Iteration 1 deep pass)
- Onboarding (4 screens)
- Pricing tiers (Free/Track/Overview)
- Conversation UI (text messages → Wade responses)
- NLP expense parsing
- Weekly summary notifications
- Error states

## What's still missing (Iteration 2 focus)

### 1. Budget Goals
- Set a monthly spending limit
- Alert when approaching limit ("You've spent £1,400 of £2,000 this month")
- Daily spending cap
- Category-specific budgets

### 2. Better Parsing & Intelligence
- Multi-currency support (Wade already mentioned this)
- Auto-categorize more accurately (learns from your patterns)
- Detect subscriptions ("You spent £9.99 — is this a subscription?")
- Recurring expenses detection ("You spend ~£50 on groceries every week")

### 3. Reports & Trends
- Monthly spending breakdown (pie chart by category)
- Compare this month vs last month
- "Biggest expense category this month: Transport (£340)"
- Yearly spending summary

### 4. Bills / Recurring
- Mark expenses as "recurring"
- Track monthly bills separately
- Upcoming bills list ("Rent due in 3 days")

### 5. Data Export
- Export to CSV
- Export to PDF (formatted monthly report)
- Export to QuickBooks/Xero (small business integration)

### 6. Multi-user / Shared
- Shared wallet for couples/households
- "We spent £X on groceries this month"

- Monthly spending pie chart
