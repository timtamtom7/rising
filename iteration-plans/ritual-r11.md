# Ritual — Round 11: Group Sessions, Community, Video

## Goal
Build Ritual's community with group breathing sessions and video sharing capabilities.

---

## Scope

### Group Breathing Sessions
- Scheduled group sessions (e.g., "Morning calm — 7am daily")
- Up to 50 participants per session
- Synchronized breath pacer across all participants
- "Session host" role with controls
- Session replay — watch recorded session
- Public session calendar

### Video Attachments
- Record video response to ritual
- "Moment of gratitude" video capture
- Video diary per ritual
- Trim/edit video before saving
- Video reactions to rituals

### Community Features
- Public profiles with ritual history
- "Follow" other practitioners
- Community intention board
- Weekly themed rituals (community-voted)
- "Ritual streak" leaderboard
- Share ritual as image card

### Breath Analysis
- Track breath rate over time
- "Your calm score" — breath consistency metric
- Session completion streaks
- "Minutes of mindfulness" weekly total
- AI breath form feedback: "Your exhale is shorter than inhale — try equal duration"

---

## Out of Scope
- Live video sessions (R12)
- Certified instructor marketplace (R13)
