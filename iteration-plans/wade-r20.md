# wade — Round 20: Wade 3.0 Vision, Long-Term Legacy, 10-Year Mission

## Goal
Define Wade's 10-year vision and write the Wade 3.0 product spec — capturing the audacious long-term mission of building the world's most intelligent and empathetic personal financial advisor.

---

## Scope

### Wade 3.0 — Vision Document
- **Write the Wade 3.0 product spec**: Comprehensive vision for Wade 3.0 — what it does, why it matters, how it works
- **Core thesis**: Financial literacy is a skill that can be learned. Wade 3.0 is the AI financial advisor that meets you where you are, speaks to you like a friend, and helps you build wealth — no matter how much or how little you start with.
- **Key innovations in 3.0**:
  - **Proactive financial agent**: Wade doesn't wait to be asked — it notices patterns, predicts problems, and acts on your behalf
  - **Emotional financial coaching**: Wade understands that money is emotional — it helps you separate emotion from logic
  - **Wealth building for everyone**: Not just expense tracking — Wade helps you build actual wealth, step by step
  - **Financial memory**: Wade remembers every financial decision you've ever made — patterns across years, not just months
  - **Ambient money management**: Wade is always listening — in messages, in apps, in life — capturing financial moments passively

### Long-Term Vision (2027-2035)
- **The financial advisor for everyone**: Wealth management used to be for the rich. Wade makes it for everyone.
- **Financial health as a metric**: Like credit score, but better — Wade's "Financial Health Score" becomes a standard metric
- **Predictive financial health**: Wade predicts financial problems before they happen — debt spirals, missed payments, overspending
- **Wealth trajectory**: AI shows you exactly how to reach your financial goals — "Save $X more per month, and you'll retire at 60"
- **Banking without banks**: Wade becomes your primary financial interface — banks become back-end infrastructure
- **The Wade effect**: Measurable improvement in financial health for Wade users vs. non-users

### Revenue Vision
- Target: $5M ARR by 2029
- B2C subscriptions (Track, Overview, Lifetime)
- B2B: Financial coach platform, therapist partnerships
- Referral revenue: financial product referrals (with full disclosure)
- Data insights: aggregate, anonymized financial insights (sold to financial institutions)

### Personal Legacy
- Document what Wade means to Tommaso: the belief that money doesn't have to be stressful, that financial intelligence is learnable, that one person can build something that genuinely helps people
- Write the Wade origin story: why personal finance, why conversational AI, why empathy in fintech
- Build in public: share the Wade journey openly
- The ultimate goal: help 1 million people feel in control of their money — no more anxiety, no more guesswork, just clarity
