# haul — Round 16: Awards, Brand, Press, B2B Travel Product

## Goal
Build Haul's brand, earn industry recognition, and launch a B2B product for travel management companies and airlines that offer packing tools to their customers.

---

## Scope

### Awards & Press
- **Apple Design Awards submission**: Showcase Haul's beautiful packing flow and AI intelligence
- **Product Hunt**: Launch Haul 2.0 on Product Hunt
- **Travel tech press**: Skift, Travel Weekly, PhocusWire — Haul as travel tech innovator
- **Consumer tech press**: TechCrunch, The Verge — Haul as the intelligent packing assistant
- **"Best of" nominations**: App Store Best of — work with PR team

### Brand Development
- Refresh brand assets: new app icon, travel-inspired color palette
- Design system: full component library
- Haul's visual language: "Pack with confidence, travel with joy"
- Promotional video: 60-second cinematic — the joy of packing perfectly

### B2B Travel Product
- **Haul for Airlines**: Airlines can offer Haul as a customer benefit — "Pack with Haul" for loyalty members
- **Haul for Hotels**: Hotels offer Haul as a pre-arrival tool — help guests know what to bring
- **Haul for Travel Agents**: Travel agents use Haul to prepare client packing lists
- **Haul for DMC** (Destination Management Companies): Help DMC staff prepare for group travel

### B2B Pricing
- **Haul Pro for Partners**: Custom pricing per partner
- Revenue from: white-label licensing, affiliate revenue from travel bookings, data insights
