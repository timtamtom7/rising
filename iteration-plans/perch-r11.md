# perch — Round 11: AI Trip Intelligence, Predictive Planning, Carbon Offset

## Goal
Transform Perch from a passive trip tracker into an AI-powered trip intelligence platform — predicting travel impacts, recommending lower-carbon alternatives, and enabling automatic carbon offset purchasing.

---

## Scope

### AI Trip Intelligence
- **Trip impact prediction**: Before a trip, AI predicts CO₂ footprint based on destinations, transportation modes, accommodation type
- **"Your flight emits 2.4x more CO₂ than the train"**: Show comparison with alternatives before booking
- **Route optimization**: AI finds lower-carbon routes — "Taking the train adds 2 hours but saves 85% CO₂"
- **Accommodation carbon footprint**: Estimate hotel/Airbnb carbon based on size, amenities, location
- **Carbon budget**: Set a monthly/yearly carbon budget — get warned when approaching limit

### Carbon Offset Integration
- **Offset purchasing**: Integrate with offset providers (Gold Standard, Verra, Terrapass) — one-tap purchase to offset trip emissions
- **Auto-offset**: Set "auto-offset all trips" — automatically purchase offsets for every trip logged
- **Portfolio tracking**: Track offset certificates — see total offset purchased, retired certificates
- **Certificate sharing**: Share offset achievements on social media

### Predictive Travel Planning
- **"Should you travel?"** recommendation: Based on carbon budget + urgency + alternative availability, AI advises whether to travel
- **Workation planner**: Plan a workation that minimizes carbon — train destinations, sustainable hotels
- **Weekend getaway optimizer**: For short trips, find the closest, lowest-carbon destination
- **Seasonal planning**: "Winter beach trips are your highest-carbon travel — consider a ski trip closer to home"

### Trip Data Enrichment
- **Destination carbon database**: Pre-computed CO₂ data for 10,000+ destinations and transport routes
- **Mode-specific factors**: Flight class (business vs. economy), train type, car size — factor these into estimates
- **Hotel sustainability ratings**: Pull sustainability scores for hotels from external databases
