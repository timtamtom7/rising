# fume — Round 17: Awards, Press, Brand, Community Building

## Goal
Build Fume's brand, earn industry recognition, and cultivate a passionate community of knowledge collectors — positioning Fume as the definitive tool for building a personal knowledge base.

---

## Scope

### Awards & Press
- **Apple Design Awards submission**: Full submission package — showcase Fume's knowledge graph and semantic search
- **Product Hunt**: Launch Fume 2.0 on Product Hunt — aim for #1 of the day
- **Tech press**: Outreach to TechCrunch, The Verge, Wired, Fast Company for Fume 2.0 launch coverage
- **Academic press**: Target EdSurge (education), Chronicle of Higher Education (academia)
- **"Best of" lists**: Nominate for App Store "Best of" — work with Apple's PR team

### Brand Development
- Refresh brand assets: new app icon, color palette, refined typography
- Design system: formal design system with component library
- Fume's visual language: "knowledge as a living graph" — incorporate concept graph motif into all branding
- Tagline testing: A/B test taglines ("Your knowledge, organized", "Think with Fume", "Know more, remember everything")

### Community Building
- **Fume community forum**: Discourse-based community for Fume users — share tips, libraries, workflows
- **Knowledge sharing**: Publish a monthly "Fume Letter" — best libraries, best workflows, user spotlights
- **User spotlight program**: Highlight power users — how they use Fume for research, writing, learning
- **Fume on YouTube**: Quick tutorial series, "How I use Fume" user videos
- **Twitter/community**: Active presence in #PKM (personal knowledge management) community

### Academic & Research Partnerships
- Partner with universities: provide Fume Pro to research groups
- Fume for Students: discounted educational pricing + academic-specific features
- Publish a white paper: "The Future of Personal Knowledge Management" — position Fume as thought leader
