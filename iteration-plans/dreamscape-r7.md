# Dreamscape — Round 7: Advanced AI Interpretation, Dream Dictionary

## Features

### Advanced Dream AI
- Deep dream interpretation — AI analyzes narrative arc of dream, not just symbols
- Emotional journey map — what you felt at each stage of the dream
- Shadow work prompts — AI surfaces unconscious patterns
- Integration suggestions — "This dream might be asking you to..."

### Personal Dream Dictionary
- AI builds YOUR personal dream dictionary — what water means to YOU specifically
- Not generic dream symbol meanings — YOUR patterns
- Track how your symbol meanings evolve over time

### Dream Incubation
- Set an intention before sleep — "Tonight I want to dream about..."
- AI helps frame the question
- Track if incubation worked
- Incubation success rate
