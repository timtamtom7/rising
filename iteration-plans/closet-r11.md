# Closet — Round 11: Style AI, Outfit Generation, Social

## Goal
Elevate Closet with AI styling, outfit generation, and social sharing features.

---

## Scope

### AI Style Suggestions
- "What to wear today" based on weather + calendar + past choices
- Outfit rating: "You've worn this combination 3 times this month"
- "Missing items" suggestions: "You have tops and bottoms but no layering pieces"
- Color palette analysis of your wardrobe
- "Outfit of the week" generated from your items
- Seasonal wardrobe rotation reminders

### Outfit Generation
- AI-generated outfit combinations
- "Style remix" — take existing items and suggest new combinations
- Occasion-based outfit packs: "Job interview", "Date night", "Weekend casual"
- Save favorite outfits as "capsule wardrobe"
- Drag-and-drop outfit builder
- Weather-appropriate outfit filtering

### Social Sharing
- Share outfit of the day as image
- "What I'm wearing" public profile
- "Outfit feedback" — ask friends for opinions
- Style comparison with friends
- Community "Most saved outfit this week"

### Virtual Closet
- Export wardrobe as PDF lookbook
- "Closet value" — total cost of items owned
- Most/least worn items report
- "Archive" old season items
- Duplicate detection in wardrobe

---

## Out of Scope
- In-app clothing purchase (R12)
- Personal stylist chat (R13)
