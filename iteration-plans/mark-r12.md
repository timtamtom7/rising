# Mark — R12: Collaboration & Team Bookmarks

## Goal
Enable Mark for teams with shared bookmark libraries, collaborative tagging, and team knowledge bases.

---

## Scope

### Shared Bookmark Libraries
- Share bookmark libraries via iCloud with team members
- Real-time sync of shared bookmarks
- Role-based access: Admin, Editor, Viewer

### Collaborative Tagging
- Team members can add tags to shared bookmarks
- Tag voting: most-used tags rise to top
- @mention teammates to draw attention to bookmarks

### Team Knowledge Base
- Build a team wiki from bookmarks
- Organize bookmarks into knowledge categories
- "Engineering Resources", "Design References"
- Search across all team bookmarks

### Bookmark Collections
- Curated collections: "Q4 Research", "Product Launch Materials"
- Collections can be public (company-wide) or private to team
- Collection version history

### Guest Access
- Invite external collaborators (clients, contractors)
- Read-only or contributor access
- Time-limited links (7/30/90 days)

### Comments & Reviews
- Comment on shared bookmarks
- Rate bookmarks (1-5 stars)
- Bookmark reviews: "Is this still relevant?"

---

## Out of Scope
- Real-time collaborative bookmark editing
- Full wiki with rich text pages (use Nimbus)
