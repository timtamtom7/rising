# Blink — Iteration 2 Plan

## What was built (Iteration 1 deep pass)
- Onboarding (4 screens)
- Pricing tiers (Free/Memories/Archive)
- Camera with countdown, record button with progress ring
- Yearly calendar view with thumbnails
- Full-screen playback
- Daily reminder notifications

## What's still missing (Iteration 2 focus)

### 1. Year-End / Highlights
- **Year in review**: at end of year, auto-generate a highlight reel (best moments as 30-sec compilation)
- **Monthly highlight reel**: every month, a short compilation of top clips
- **"On this day"**: show clips from same date in previous years

### 2. Search & Navigation
- Search clips by date or keyword (if user adds a title/note)
- Jump to specific month/year
- Filter: clips with audio only, clips longer than X seconds

### 3. Clip Editing (Light)
- Trim: cut start/end of clip (30 seconds is short — trim dead air)
- Add a title/note to a clip
- Favorite/star clips

### 4. Export & Backup
- Export individual clips to camera roll
- Export a month's clips as a video file
- iCloud backup (opt-in)
- Delete old clips automatically (Archive tier: 1 year local, unlimited cloud)

### 5. Privacy
- Passcode/TouchID lock for app
- Lock individual clips (hide from year view)
- Auto-delete clips older than X days (optional, not forced)

### 6. Widget
- Home screen widget: record button (opens app directly to camera)
- Small widget: "Record today" with date
- Medium widget: today's clip (if recorded)

## Custom Graphics for Iteration 2
- Year-in-review compilation visual
- "On this day" card
- Trim UI design
- Privacy lock icon
