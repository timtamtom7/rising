# kale — Round 15: International Expansion, Localization, Regional Adaptation

## Goal
Take Kale global — full localization, regional supplement databases, PPP-adjusted pricing, and cultural adaptation for health practices that vary by region.

---

## Scope

### Full Localization (i18n)
- All user-facing strings in `Localizable.strings` / `.stringsdict`
- Languages: English (base), German, French, Spanish, Italian, Portuguese, Japanese, Korean, Simplified Chinese, Arabic (RTL)
- Localized screenshots for App Store in top 5 languages
- Pluralization, date/time formatting — all locale-aware

### Regional Supplement Database
- **Supplement registry per region**: Supplements available in US, EU, UK, Australia, Japan, India
- **Regional regulations**: EU food supplement regulations, FDA vs. TGA vs. Health Canada labeling
- **Regional dosage norms**: Adjust recommended dosages based on regional health authority guidelines
- **Local pharmacy integration**: CVS (US), Boots (UK), dm-drogerie markt (Germany), Welcia (Japan)

### Regional Pricing & Payments
- PPP-adjusted pricing for India, Brazil, Southeast Asia, Eastern Europe
- Local payment methods: Alipay, UPI, Boleto, Paytm
- Regional App Store page optimization

### Cultural Health Practices
- **Ayurveda module**: Track Ayurvedic supplements and practices (India)
- **Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM)**: Track herbal medicine alongside Western supplements
- **European phytotherapy**: Track European herbal supplements with appropriate descriptions
- **Regional health campaigns**: Connect to regional public health initiatives (flu shots, Vitamin D campaigns in Nordic countries)

---

## Out of Scope
- Kale 2.0 redesign (R16)
- B2B / Health Coach platform (R16)
