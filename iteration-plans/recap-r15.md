# Recap — R15: iOS Companion App

## Goal
Ship Recap as a native iOS and iPadOS app for reading recaps and managing content on the go.

---

## Scope

### iOS App
- Read recaps on iPhone and iPad
- Browse content sources
- Generate and read personal recaps
- Team digest reading
- Push notifications for new recaps

### iPad Optimization
- Full iPad layout with reading pane
- Split View / Stage Manager
- Keyboard shortcuts
- External display for reading

### Apple Watch App
- Glance: "New recap ready"
- Complication: quick access to latest recap
- Haptic for new recap notification

### iOS Widgets
- Small widget: latest recap title
- Medium widget: recap + source + reading time
- Large widget: recap list + generate button
- Lock screen widget: recap title
- Interactive widget: generate recap

### Share Extension
- Share articles from Safari → add to Recap queue
- Save for later from any app

### Siri & Shortcuts
- "Read my daily recap" → opens latest recap
- "Add this article to Recap"
- Shortcuts: generate recap, get latest recap

---

## Out of Scope
- Android companion (separate round)
- Full editing on watch
