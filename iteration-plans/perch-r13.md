# perch — Round 13: Subscription Business, Pricing, Retention

## Goal
Build a sustainable subscription business for Perch — pricing optimization, retention mechanics, and customer success infrastructure.

---

## Scope

### Subscription Tier Refinement
- **Perch Free**: Track up to 5 trips/year, basic CO₂ estimates, carbon offset purchasing
- **Perch Wander**: Unlimited trips, AI trip intelligence, predictive planning, all transport modes ($7.99/month or $69.99/year)
- **Perch Explorer**: Wander + shared trips, group planning, challenges, priority features ($14.99/month or $129.99/year)
- **Lifetime option**: one-time $149
- A/B test pricing via remote config

### Retention Mechanics
- **Onboarding sequence**: Day 1: log first trip. Day 3: view first CO₂ insight. Day 7: set carbon budget.
- **Travel streak**: Earn badges for consecutive months with no high-carbon travel
- **"You saved X kg CO₂ this year"**: Celebratory metrics — make environmental impact tangible
- **Churn prevention**: No trip logged in 60 days → re-engagement email with "plan a trip" CTA
- **Annual upgrade**: "Switch to annual and save 2 months"

### Analytics & Metrics
- Track: MAU, DAU, trips logged per month, carbon offset purchases
- MRR, ARR, churn rate, LTV by cohort and acquisition channel
- Which features drive upgrades?
- Environmental impact metrics: total CO₂ offset by Perch users (for marketing)

### Customer Success
- In-app support
- Help Center: how to log trips, understand CO₂ estimates, purchase offsets
- Email sequence: monthly carbon insights, seasonal travel tips
- NPS survey quarterly
