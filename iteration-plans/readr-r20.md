# readr — Round 20: Readr 3.0 Vision, Long-Term Legacy, 10-Year Mission

## Goal
Define Readr's 10-year vision and write the Readr 3.0 product spec — capturing the audacious long-term mission of building the world's most comprehensive idea and quote intelligence platform.

---

## Scope

### Readr 3.0 — Vision Document
- **Write the Readr 3.0 product spec**: Comprehensive vision for Readr 3.0 — what it does, why it matters, how it works
- **Core thesis**: The best thinking is borrowed, stolen, and synthesized from the ideas of others. Readr 3.0 is the platform that captures, organizes, and activates the ideas that shape you — making you a better reader, writer, and thinker.
- **Key innovations in 3.0**:
  - **Idea memory**: AI remembers every idea you've ever captured — searchable, connectable, analyzable — you can never lose an insight again
  - **The quote OS**: Readr as the operating system for ideas — quotes flow into Readr from every source, are organized by meaning, and emerge when relevant
  - **Writing with reading**: Seamless integration with any writing app — cite, reference, and synthesize from your quote library without leaving your writing flow
  - **The idea graph**: A global graph of human ideas — how quotes, concepts, authors, and books connect — enabling discovery that feels like thinking
  - **Ambient idea capture**: Readr is always ready — voice, camera, clipboard — capture any idea the moment it strikes

### Long-Term Vision (2027-2035)
- **The world's intellectual memory**: Readr becomes a global repository of human wisdom — every important idea, captured, organized, accessible
- **Author partnerships**: Authors publish directly to Readr — exclusive quotes, early passages, behind-the-scenes thinking
- **Academic standard**: Universities and researchers use Readr as the citation and research tool — proper attribution, bibliography, literature review
- **Writing AI**: Readr's AI doesn't just find quotes — it synthesizes ideas, writes drafts, and helps you think through problems using everything you've ever read
- **Quote of the century**: Readr's annual "most saved quote" becomes a cultural moment — the quote that defined the year
- **Philanthropic extension**: Free Readr for students and educators — the reading underclass deserves access to the same tools

### Revenue Vision
- Target: $2M ARR by 2029
- B2C subscriptions (Pro, Scholar, Lifetime)
- Publisher partnerships: featured quote placement revenue
- B2B: Academic licensing, corporate knowledge management
- Affiliate revenue: book purchase referrals
