# drift — Round 19: Drift 2.0 — The Sleep Health Platform

## Goal
Ship Drift 2.0 — a comprehensive redesign that positions Drift as the definitive sleep health platform, blending beautiful design with clinical-grade insights.

---

## Scope

### Drift 2.0 — Major Redesign
- **New visual identity**: Dark, calming design language — deep navy/charcoal backgrounds, soft purple/blue accents
- **SwiftUI 5 + iOS 18+**: Full redesign with latest SwiftUI capabilities, interactive widgets, App Intents for Siri
- **New home screen**: Today's sleep score prominently, with quick actions (start sleep, set alarm, view trends)
- **Storytelling UI**: Sleep data presented as a narrative — "Last night was your best sleep in 12 days. Here's why."

### Apple Intelligence Integration
- **Siri + Drift**: "Hey Siri, how did I sleep?" — reads last night's sleep report
- **Live Activities**: Sleep tracking as a Live Activity on Lock Screen — current sleep phase, time asleep, time until wake
- **Live Captions**: Sleep insights narrated aloud for hands-free morning briefing
- **Predictive sleep**: AI predicts tonight's optimal bedtime based on calendar, activity, and historical data

### Predictive Sleep Modeling
- **30-day sleep forecast**: Based on trends, predict next month's sleep quality
- **Life event correlation**: "You're sleeping 18% worse since starting a new project" — proactive insight
- **Optimal schedule optimization**: AI finds your personal chronotype and optimal sleep/wake times
- **Sleep debt alerts**: "You have 4 hours of sleep debt — here's a plan to recover over the weekend"

### Sleep Bank & Longevity
- **Sleep banking**: Track "sleep debt" and "sleep surplus" — view sleep as a financial metaphor
- **Longevity correlation**: Research-based insights connecting sleep patterns to long-term health outcomes
- **Sleep score 2.0**: New composite score incorporating duration, efficiency, timing, and long-term trends
- **Annual sleep report**: Comprehensive yearly document — trends, correlations, recommendations, printable

---

## Out of Scope
- Nothing — Drift 2.0 is the culmination of R1–R19.
