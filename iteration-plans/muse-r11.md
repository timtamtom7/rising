# Muse — R11: Advanced ML & Intelligent Composition

## Goal
Bring on-device ML to Muse for intelligent music generation, style transfer, and predictive composition assistance.

---

## Scope

### AI Composition Assistance
- On-device ML generates musical suggestions based on your style
- Chord progression suggestions: "Based on your song so far, try these progressions"
- Melody completion: Muse suggests how a melody might continue
- Bass line and harmonic accompaniment generation

### Style Transfer
- Apply musical style transfer to your compositions
- "Make this sound like jazz" or "Make this sound like lo-fi"
- On-device ML models for different genres
- Real-time style preview

### Music Analysis
- ML analyzes your MIDI/audio for musical features
- Key detection, tempo analysis, complexity metrics
- "This section is in 7/8 — consider switching to 4/4 for contrast"
- Emotional tone detection: "This sounds melancholic, not dramatic as intended"

### Smart Loops
- ML suggests loops from your library that match current project
- "This guitar loop matches the key and tempo of your project"
- Auto-extend loops: ML continues loop pattern naturally

### Genre Detection & Tagging
- Auto-detect genre and sub-genre of compositions
- Tag compositions with musical features
- Search by feel: "Find songs that feel like this"

---

## Out of Scope
- Cloud-based synthesis or rendering
- Royalty-free music generation for commercial use
- Professional mastering (use dedicated mastering tools)
