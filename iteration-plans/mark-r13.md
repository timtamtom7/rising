# Mark — R13: Enterprise & Knowledge Management

## Goal
Position Mark for enterprise knowledge management with compliance, retention policies, and organizational tools.

---

## Scope

### Knowledge Management
- Hierarchical knowledge base from bookmarks
- Space → Section → Bookmark structure
- Table of contents auto-generated
- Internal cross-linking between bookmarks

### Retention Policies
- Configure bookmark retention rules
- "Marketing materials: retain 2 years"
- Auto-archive or review-required at expiration
- "This bookmark hasn't been accessed in 1 year — review?"

### Compliance & Audit
- Full audit log: all bookmark access, changes, shares
- Export audit log as CSV/JSON
- SOC 2 Type II documentation available
- GDPR/CCPA compliance documentation

### Access Control
- Organization-level roles: Admin, Member, Guest
- Per-space permissions
- Confidential bookmarks: visible only to specific users

### Enterprise Search
- Global search across all org bookmarks
- Search filters: author, date, space, tag
- Search analytics for admins

### SSO / SAML
- SAML 2.0 SSO for enterprise dashboard
- Support for Okta, Azure AD, Google Workspace
- JIT provisioning from SSO groups

### Volume Licensing
- Apple VPP enrollment
- Enterprise pricing tiers

---

## Out of Scope
- Full ECM (Enterprise Content Management)
- Document management (use Folio)
- Workflow automation beyond bookmarks
