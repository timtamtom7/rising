# plume — Round 20: Plume 3.0 Vision, Long-Term Legacy, 10-Year Mission

## Goal
Define Plume's 10-year vision and write the Plume 3.0 product spec — capturing the audacious long-term mission of building the world's most comprehensive reading intelligence platform.

---

## Scope

### Plume 3.0 — Vision Document
- **Write the Plume 3.0 product spec**: Comprehensive vision for Plume 3.0 — what it does, why it matters, how it works
- **Core thesis**: Reading is how we think, learn, and grow. Plume 3.0 is the platform that understands your entire reading life — what you've read, what it meant to you, and what you should read next.
- **Key innovations in 3.0**:
  - **Complete reading OS**: Plume as the operating system for reading — print, e-book, audiobook, article, research paper — all unified
  - **Reading memory**: AI remembers everything you've ever read — searchable insights, quotes, ideas — never lose an idea from a book again
  - **Writing from reading**: Plume's AI helps you write using everything you've read — cite, reference, synthesize
  - **Reading genome**: Every book mapped to a reading genome — discover books by their conceptual DNA
  - **Ambient reading intelligence**: Plume passively suggests relevant reading — as you think about a topic, relevant books surface

### Long-Term Vision (2027-2035)
- **The reading standard**: Plume becomes the de facto way to track and discover reading — like a library card for the digital age
- **Publisher partnerships**: Plume as a discovery channel for publishers — authors publish reading lists, early chapters, behind-the-scenes
- **Academic adoption**: Universities use Plume for reading curricula — track reading, manage syllabi, generate reading reports
- **Reading therapy**: Plume as a therapeutic tool — reading prescriptions for mental health ( bibliotherapy)
- **Literary legacy**: Your reading history preserved as a legacy — family inherits your reading life
- **The Plume effect**: Measurable improvement in reading comprehension and retention for Plume users vs. non-users

### Revenue Vision
- Target: $3M ARR by 2029
- B2C subscriptions (Reader, Scholar, Lifetime)
- Publisher partnerships: featured placement revenue
- B2B: Academic licensing, corporate reading programs
- Affiliate revenue: book purchase referrals
