# Shelf — R16: Subscription Tiers & Monetization

## Goal
Introduce Shelf Pro and Shelf Team subscription tiers with meaningful feature differentiation.

---

## Scope

## Free Tier (Core)
- Up to 3 shelves
- Manual item organization
- Basic search
- Recent items (last 7 days)
- 1 shared shelf (with up to 2 people)

## Shelf Pro ($3.99/month or $29.99/year)
- Unlimited shelves
- ML smart suggestions and predictive launching
- Smart stacks (auto-grouping)
- Full history (unlimited)
- Advanced search (full-text, filter by type, date, app)
- Shortcuts & Siri integration
- Priority email support
- Early access to beta features

## Shelf Team ($7.99/month or $59.99/year)
- Everything in Pro
- Up to 10 team members
- Shared shelves with real-time sync
- Team activity feed
- Collaborative stacks
- Guest access (unlimited external guests)
- Team admin controls
- Team usage analytics

## Shelf Enterprise (custom pricing)
- Everything in Team
- Unlimited team members
- SSO / SAML
- Audit logs
- MDM support
- Custom data retention
- Dedicated account manager
- SLA guarantees

## Subscription Infrastructure
- StoreKit 2 for in-app purchases
- RevenueCat for cross-platform subscription management
- 14-day free trial for Pro and Team
- Same iCloud account → unlock on all devices
- Family sharing: Pro can be shared with Family group

---

## Out of Scope
- One-time purchase option
- Hardware bundling
- Ad-supported free tier
