# plume — Round 15: International Expansion, Localization, Global Reading Culture

## Goal
Take Plume global — full localization, international book databases, cultural adaptation for reading traditions that vary by region.

---

## Scope

### Full Localization (i18n)
- All user-facing strings in `Localizable.strings` / `.stringsdict`
- Languages: English (base), German, French, Spanish, Italian, Portuguese, Japanese, Korean, Simplified Chinese, Arabic (RTL)
- Localized screenshots for App Store in top 5 languages
- Pluralization, date/time formatting — all locale-aware

### International Book Databases
- **Local book APIs**: Open Library (global), Goodreads (English bias), plus regional:
  - **Germany**: Vorbestellungen / Deutsche Nationalbibliothek
  - **France**: Electre / BnF (Bibliothèque nationale de France)
  - **Japan**: Calil /国立国会図書館
  - **China**: Douban / Dangdang
  - **Korea**: Naver Books / Ksic
- **Regional best-seller lists**: "Top 10 in France this week" — localized discovery

### Regional Reading Culture
- **Reading tradition tracking**: Track reading of local classics alongside international books
- **Local book awards**: Integrate with local literary awards (Prix Goncourt, Booker Prize, Akutagawa Prize)
- **Regional reading challenges**: "Read 5 French authors this year" — culture-specific challenges
- **Language preferences**: Prioritize books in user's preferred language for recommendations

### International Pricing & Payments
- PPP-adjusted pricing for developing markets
- Local payment methods: Alipay, UPI, Boleto, Paytm
- Regional App Store page optimization

---

## Out of Scope
- Plume 2.0 redesign (R16)
- Awards and press (R17)
