# Volt — R20: Platform Expansion & Long-Term Vision

## Goal
Extend Volt beyond macOS to become the definitive cross-platform power management suite for Apple devices and beyond.

---

## Scope

### Android Companion App
- Native Android app (Kotlin)
- Monitor Mac battery from Android phone
- Push notifications for low/fully charged
- Power mode switching from Android
- Google Play Store listing

### Windows Integration
- Volt for Windows (Electron or Tauri)
- Monitor Windows laptop battery alongside Mac
- Unified battery dashboard across platforms
- Share power mode profiles across Volt on all platforms

### iOS Open Source Core
- Open-source the battery monitoring core engine (volt-core on GitHub)
- MIT license for the engine, proprietary license for UI/features
- Community can build alternative UIs or integrations

### Integration Ecosystem
- Public integration registry: volt.app/integrations
- HomeKit as first-class citizen: Mac status visible in Home app
- IFTTT, Zapier, Make: full trigger/action support
- Smart home integrations: notify when Mac is asleep (trigger lights off)

### Apple Vision Pro Support
- visionOS app
- Spatial battery dashboard
- 3D battery health visualization
- Ambient battery status as spatial element

### Public Roadmap & Community
- Public roadmap at volt.app/roadmap
- User voting on features
- Monthly development updates
- Community Discord / forum

### Long-Term Research
- Battery health prediction model open-sourced for research
- Research partnerships with battery technology organizations
- Publication of anonymized battery usage pattern dataset (with permission)

---

## Out of Scope
- Hardware products (smart batteries, smart plugs)
- Cryptocurrency or carbon offset tracking
- Direct integration with energy retailers
