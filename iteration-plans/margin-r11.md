# Margin — Round 11: Groups, Threads, Insights

## Goal
Expand Margin with small group support, threaded discussions, and aggregate insights.

---

## Scope

### Small Groups
- Create private groups (up to 20 members)
- Group moments feed (anonymous within group)
- Group moments of the week digest
- "Group pulse" — aggregate mood of group
- Group sharing settings (anyone can share / approval required)
- Invite via link or code

### Threaded Discussions
- Reply to any moment in a thread
- "This resonated" reactions
- Thread highlighting: most resonant moments
- Nested threads (reply to reply)
- Thread muting
- Thread bookmarks

### Aggregate Insights
- "Community insights" — patterns across all moments
- "Most reflected-upon topics this week"
- Mood trends over time (anonymized aggregate)
- "Words that appear together often" co-occurrence
- Weekly insights email digest

### Anonymous Identity
- Random avatar + pseudonym per group
- Consistent identity within group
- "Anonymous to friends" — visible to friends, anonymous to others
- Identity reveal option (opt-in per moment)

---

## Out of Scope
- Public community (R12)
- Moderation tools (R13)
