# Pulse — R16: Subscription Tiers & Monetization

## Goal
Introduce Pulse Pro and Pulse Family subscription tiers with differentiated features and meaningful value.

---

## Scope

## Free Tier (Core)
- Real-time heart rate display
- 7-day history
- Basic trends (daily/weekly)
- 1 watch face complication
- Care Circle (up to 2 family members)
- Emergency contact (1 contact)

## Pulse Pro ($4.99/month or $39.99/year)
- Unlimited history
- ML anomaly detection and alerts
- Advanced health trends (30/60/90 day analysis)
- Sleep HR analysis
- Activity correlation insights
- AI health digest (weekly)
- Export to FHIR / PDF for doctors
- All watch complications
- Shortcuts & Siri integration
- Priority email support

## Pulse Family ($8.99/month or $69.99/year)
- Everything in Pro
- Care Circle for up to 5 family members
- Shared family goals and challenges
- Family health digest
- Care team access (unlimited read-only providers)
- Guardian monitoring for children (with consent)
- HIPAA mode (enterprise compliance)

## Pulse Enterprise (custom pricing)
- Everything in Family
- Enterprise wellness dashboard
- HIPAA BAA
- SSO / SAML
- Audit logs
- Volume pricing
- Dedicated account manager

## Subscription Infrastructure
- StoreKit 2 for in-app purchases
- RevenueCat for cross-platform subscription management
- 14-day free trial for Pro and Family
- Same iCloud account → unlock on all devices
- Family Sharing: Pro unlocked for all family members

---

## Out of Scope
- Lifetime purchase option
- One-time fee
- Ad-supported free tier
