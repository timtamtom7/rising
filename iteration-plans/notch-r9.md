# Notch — R9: Post-Launch, Monetization, Roadmap

## Goal
Analyze launch metrics, refine business model, and plan next features.

---

## Scope

### Launch Metrics
- Downloads, revenue
- Retention: day 1/7/30
- Crash rate vs beta
- IAP conversion rate
- Top notch apps used

### Business Model Refinement
- Review pricing
- Consider subscription vs one-time
- Family sharing
- Student discount

### Feature Roadmap (Next Cycle)
From reviews and analytics:
1. **Spotify notch app**: show current track in notch, tap to pause/play
2. **Voice memos notch app**: quick record button in notch
3. **Focus mode integration**: show current Focus mode in notch
4. **Custom notch app SDK**: let users create their own notch apps
5. **Animated notch wallpapers**: GIF/live photo as wallpaper

### Maintenance
- macOS update compatibility within 1 week of beta
- Xcode update within 1 week
- Security patches within 48 hours
- Dependency updates

- End-of-life planning
