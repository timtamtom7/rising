# haul — Round 17: Platform Ecosystem, Spatial Computing, Vision, Long-Term Vision

## Goal
Extend Haul into a full platform ecosystem — spatial packing experiences, travel partner integrations, and a compelling long-term vision for Haul as the complete travel companion.

---

## Scope

### Haul for Apple Vision Pro
- **Spatial packing room**: See your entire packing list as objects in your space — tap to confirm
- **Suitcase visualization**: See your suitcase in 3D — items arranged optimally, weight distributed
- **Trip planning session**: Plan the trip with family in a shared Vision Pro space
- **Packing tutorial**: AI demonstrates how to pack efficiently in 3D

### Travel Partner Integrations
- **Airbnb integration**: Pull accommodation details from Airbnb — "You're staying at X for 5 nights — here's what to pack"
- **Booking.com integration**: Same for Booking.com
- **Delta / United / American**: Pull flight details — seat, class, baggage allowance — into Haul
- **TSA PreCheck / Global Entry**: Help users prepare for security — "Do you have your liquids organized?"

### Gear & Product Integration
- **REI / Amazon**: When you add outdoor gear, suggest buying from partner retailers
- **Travel insurance**: Integrate travel insurance providers — "You haven't added travel insurance — want a quote?"
- **Luggage scale**: Bluetooth integration with travel luggage scales — weigh your bag before the airport

### Long-Term Vision (2027-2030)
- **The complete travel companion**: Haul starts with packing, but becomes the app for the entire trip — before, during, and after
- **RFID packing**: Scan tagged items — know exactly what's in your bag without opening it
- **Luggage tracking integration**: Integrate with Apple AirTag, Tile — know where your bag is
- **Predictive travel**: AI predicts what you'll need based on where you're going and who you're seeing
- **Packing for the planet**: Haul as a sustainability tool — pack lighter = lower carbon footprint
