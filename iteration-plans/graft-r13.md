# graft — Round 13: Teacher Platform, Mentorship, Structured Curriculum

## Goal
Build a Graft teacher/mentor platform — enabling expert practitioners to offer structured lessons, track student progress, and monetize their expertise through Graft.

---

## Scope

### Teacher Profiles
- **Teacher marketplace**: Browse verified teachers by skill category
- **Teacher profiles**: Bio, specialties, teaching style, student reviews, pricing
- **Verification**: Lightweight verification process (submit a video of skill in action)
- **Featured teachers**: Curated top teachers per skill category

### Mentorship Programs
- **Structured curriculum**: Teachers create a curriculum — 8-week program, 12 lessons, milestones
- **Enrollment**: Students enroll in a curriculum — their progress visible to teacher
- **Live lessons**: Teachers can offer live (video) lessons integrated via Graft — record for future reference
- **Assignment tracking**: Teacher assigns specific practice tasks — student logs completion in Graft

### Teacher Dashboard
- **Student roster**: See all enrolled students, their current progress, practice streaks
- **Progress reports**: Per-student progress reports (generate PDF to send to parent/student)
- **Session review**: Review recorded session data — identify where student needs help
- **Messaging**: Send voice/text messages to students directly in Graft

### Monetization
- **Teacher pricing**: Teachers set their own rates (per lesson, per month, per curriculum)
- **Graft takes 15%**: Marketplace fee — Graft handles payment processing
- **Teacher earnings dashboard**: Track earnings, payout history
- **Free tier for teachers**: Teachers can use Graft for free to track their own practice, paid when monetizing
