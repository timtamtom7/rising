# kale — Round 17: Awards, Brand, Community, Press

## Goal
Build Kale's brand, earn industry recognition, and cultivate a community of health-conscious users — positioning Kale as the definitive supplement intelligence platform.

---

## Scope

### Awards & Press
- **Apple Design Awards submission**: Showcase Kale's AI health insights and beautiful health dashboard
- **Product Hunt**: Launch Kale 2.0 on Product Hunt
- **Health tech press**: MobiHealthNews, Rock Health, FierceHealthcare — Kale as digital therapeutic
- **Consumer press**: TechCrunch, Wired, NYT (Well section) — Kale as consumer health tool
- **"Best of" nominations**: App Store Best of — work with PR team

### Brand Development
- Refresh brand assets: new app icon, color palette (health green + warm orange), refined typography
- Design system: full component library
- Kale's visual language: "Your health, in balance" — circular/cycle motifs
- Promotional video: 60-second video showing Kale in daily life

### Community Building
- **Kale community forum**: Discourse-based community — share supplement tips, health journeys
- **User stories**: "How Kale helped me fix my Vitamin D deficiency" — user testimonials
- **Health coach marketplace**: Kale-verified health coaches offer advice through Kale
- **Reddit**: Active presence in r/supplements, r/nutrition, r/health
- **Newsletter**: Monthly "Kale Health Digest" — best tips, new features, community highlights

### Clinical Validation
- **Partner with research institution**: Run a small clinical study on Kale's effectiveness (supplement compliance rates vs. traditional tracking)
- **Publish results**: If positive, publish in peer-reviewed journal
- **FDA notification**: If data supports, file as FDA notification (not full approval) for the AI recommendation engine
