# perch — Round 20: Perch 3.0 Vision, Long-Term Legacy, 10-Year Mission

## Goal
Define Perch's 10-year vision and write the Perch 3.0 product spec — capturing the audacious long-term mission of building the world's most comprehensive personal carbon intelligence platform.

---

## Scope

### Perch 3.0 — Vision Document
- **Write the Perch 3.0 product spec**: Comprehensive vision for Perch 3.0 — what it does, why it matters, how it works
- **Core thesis**: Climate change is the defining challenge of our generation. Perch 3.0 is the platform that empowers every individual to understand, reduce, and offset their carbon footprint — making climate action personal, measurable, and achievable.
- **Key innovations in 3.0**:
  - **Full carbon life cycle**: Not just travel — food carbon, home energy carbon, consumption carbon — the complete picture
  - **AI carbon coach**: An AI agent that proactively suggests carbon reductions — "You flew to NYC last month. Train is available for $50 more and saves 90% CO₂."
  - **Carbon social graph**: See how your carbon footprint compares to friends, neighbors, city averages (anonymized)
  - **Regenerative travel**: Not just low-carbon — travel that actively benefits the places you visit (plant trees, support local conservation)
  - **Policy voice**: Aggregate Perch data anonymized and used to advocate for stronger climate policy

### Long-Term Vision (2027-2035)
- **Carbon as second nature**: People check their carbon the way they check their calories — daily habit
- **Perch as climate currency**: A world where carbon budgets are as understood as financial budgets
- **Climate prediction market**: Perch data predicts climate trends — and informs policy before crises hit
- **Personal carbon credit**: Earn carbon credits for sustainable choices — trade them, use them, retire them
- **Climate digital twin**: A real-time digital twin of your carbon footprint — updated every moment
- **The Perch effect**: Measurable, documented reduction in Perch user carbon vs. non-users — published in peer-reviewed research

### Revenue Vision
- Target: $5M ARR by 2029
- B2C subscriptions (Pro, Explorer, Lifetime)
- B2B: Corporate travel carbon reporting
- Carbon offset marketplace (commission on offset sales)
- Data licensing: aggregate, anonymized travel carbon data
- Partnership revenue: booking platform integrations
