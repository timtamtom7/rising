# Dreamscape — Round 5: Apple Watch, Complications, watchOS App

## Features

### watchOS App
- Log dream from Watch — voice dictation, quick entry
- Dream symbol lookup — quick reference on wrist
- Morning reminder to log dreams — tap to open iPhone app
- Dream notification — see last night's dream on watch face

### watchOS Complications
- Symbol of the day complication
- Dream streak complication
- Moon phase display — what phase were you dreaming in?

### iOS 26 Polish
- Live Activities — show dream streak on lock screen
- Interactive widgets — tap to log dream
- Dynamic Island — dream reminder when you wake up
