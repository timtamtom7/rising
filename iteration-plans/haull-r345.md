# Haul — Rounds 3, 4, 5

## Round 3: Templates, Weather, Polish

### Packing Templates
- Save packing list as template ("Beach Trip", "Business Trip", "Camping")
- Pre-built templates with common items per category
- One-tap apply template to new trip
- Edit and save custom templates

### Smart Suggestions
- "Based on your Beach Trip template, you might want: Sunscreen"
- Weather at destination → suggest items ("It's rainy in Tokyo — add umbrella")
- Trip duration → auto-calculate quantities ("5 days = 5 t-shirts?")

### Multiple Suitcases
- Track more than one bag per trip
- Name each bag (checked, carry-on, personal item)
- Per-bag packing lists
- Transfer items between bags

---

## Round 4: Post-Trip, Sharing, Export

### Post-Trip
- "How was the trip?" feedback after trip ends
- What did you forget? What did you not wear?
- Learn from past trips: "You never used X — skip it next time"
- Trip rating (1-5 stars)

### Share & Export
- Share packing list with travel companion
- Export as PDF checklist
- Print-friendly version
- Share trip summary after

### Clothing Linkage
- Take a photo of your wardrobe
- App suggests items from your wardrobe (don't double-pack a black t-shirt)
- Track what you wore vs didn't wear

### iOS App
- Native iOS app
- Widget: quick access to active trip checklist

---

## Round 5: Platform, Community, Scale

### Platform
- macOS app
- Apple Watch app (check items from watch)

### Community
- Share packing list templates publicly
- Browse and import others' templates
- "Popular templates this week"

### Inventory Management
- Track clothing items you own
- "Do I own a rain jacket?" — search your wardrobe
- Link wardrobe to packing (auto-suggest from what you own)

### Freemium Polish
- Free: 2 trips, 20 items per trip
- Pack: unlimited trips, unlimited items
- Travel: multiple bags, templates, weather, wardrobe
