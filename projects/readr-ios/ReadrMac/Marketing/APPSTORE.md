## Description

ReadrMac transforms how you manage your reading life. Track every book you want to read, are reading, and have finished — with a warm, bookish aesthetic that makes your library a joy to browse.

### Features

- **Track Your Reading** — Add books, log your progress, and watch your reading journey unfold
- **Smart Insights** — Discover your reading patterns by genre, pace, and season
- **Reading Lists** — Organize books into custom reading lists with ease
- **Book Details** — Cover color, genre, page count, dates, and personal notes
- **Menu Bar Companion** — Stay on top of your current read without opening the app
- **Dark Mode Ready** — A warm, paper-like reading environment in any light

### Who It's For

Readers who want a beautiful, distraction-free way to manage their library — no social feeds, no gamification, just you and your books.
